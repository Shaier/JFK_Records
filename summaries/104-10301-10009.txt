This document is a draft list of Directorate of Science and Technology documents released (or considered for release) as sanitized under the President John F. Kennedy Assassination Records Act of 1992. It lists mission statements and organizational charts from various offices within the Directorate, including the Photographic Intelligence Center, the National Photographic Interpretation Center, the Office of Special Activities, and the Office of Technical Service, spanning from 1959 to 1975. Some documents are pending Directorate re-review.
