This document is a CIA message from December 12, 1963, reporting on arrests and militia vigilance along the northern coastal area of Cuba. The information comes from various sources, including fishermen and relatives of individuals who received related communications. The report states that the northern coastal area of Matanzas Province was under militia surveillance as of December 5, 1963. Additionally, on December 9, many people had been arrested in certain areas of the same province, and militia were present along the coastline. The message is classified as secret and specifies distribution to various recipients. It requests protection for sub-sources, and classifies the information as unevaluated, with the source gradings definitive but the content tentative.
