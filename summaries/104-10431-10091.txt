This document is a letter from a U.S. government agency to Anthony regarding Valeriy Vladimirovich Kostikov. The letter details Kostikov's travels and assignments, particularly his time in Mexico City, including his interactions with Lee Harvey Oswald, his alleged activities with Central American communist parties, and an unexpected departure from Mexico. The letter also mentions Kostikov's later assignment in Beirut and an unconfirmed report that he may have been a member of Department 13 (Executive Action).
