This is a letter from G. Robert Blakey, Chief Counsel and Director, to Mr. Scott Breckinridge, Principal Coordinator/HSCA, at the Central Intelligence Agency. The letter follows up on a conversation between Chairman Stokes and Admiral Turner. It requests the Agency's assistance in arranging interviews with individuals in Mexico, and provides a list of names. It also mentions that some of the information may be sensitive or classified, and that additional identifying data can be provided orally if needed.
