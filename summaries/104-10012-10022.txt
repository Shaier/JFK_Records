This document is a letter from the CIA responding to a request for information about Valeriy Vladimirovich Kostikov, a Soviet consular officer who served in Mexico City. It details his assignments, activities, and travels, including his involvement with Lee Harvey Oswald's visa request and his later work with Central American communist parties. The letter also mentions speculation surrounding Kostikov's sudden departure from Mexico and the CIA's knowledge of his later assignment in Beirut.
