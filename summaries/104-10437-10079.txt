This document is a memorandum from M.D. Stevens regarding the Lee Harvey Oswald case. It discusses Abram Chayes, Legal Officer of the State Department, and his role in Oswald's return to the United States from the Soviet Union in June 1962. The memo also refers to Samuel Wise, Counsel of the American Embassy in Moscow, who may be Samuel Griffin Wise Jr., a possible contract employee on AEACTIVE. The document details Wise's background, including his security approval for CIA employment, his association with William Orville Munsell, and his attendance at Scarsdale High School during a period of alleged Communist influence. Additionally, it mentions a case similar to Oswald's involving someone named Davis.
