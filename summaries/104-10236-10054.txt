This document is a classified message discussing the possibility of extending Cuban problem coverage from Jamaica. It raises concerns about the counter-productive nature of overt delegations, given the Jamaican government's position against using Jamaica for counter-revolutionary activities against Cuba, and their concern for the safety of Jamaican residents in Cuba. The message suggests maintaining covert activities and discusses the potential harm of certain delegations to KUBARK and ODYOKE interests. It also explores the possibility of assigning staff or a contract agent to Jamaica under non-official cover to handle Cuban and related Haitian and Dominican problems. Additionally, it mentions the potential for increased reporting with the addition of a secretary to handle administrative chores and requests consideration of proposed assignments.
