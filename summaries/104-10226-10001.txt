This document is a memorandum for the record, dated January 21, 1965, concerning the Yuriy Ivanovich Nosenko case. It details a meeting between the DDP, Chief of SR, and Ambassador Thompson to discuss plans for hostile interrogation of Nosenko. The document covers various aspects of the case, including the FBI's involvement, potential contingencies such as escape or suicide, communication from the Soviet side, the possibility of turning Nosenko back, and concerns about Soviet disinformation through FBI sources.
