This document is an annex to a chapter of a handbook, concerning a "201 system" used to identify and track persons of interest. It details the system's purpose, features, procedures for opening, maintaining, purging, and restricting 201 dossiers, as well as requesting information and machine listings. The annex includes information on assigning OI codes, handling sensitive information, and automatic dossier openings for specific categories of individuals. It also briefly describes the predecessor "IDN" system.
