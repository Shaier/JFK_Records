This document is a memorandum regarding an honor recommendation for James W. McCord, Jr., concerning security measures related to an award. It includes a recommendation form detailing McCord's work in the Technical Division of the Office of Security. It describes his role in developing a new technique for detecting clandestine microphones and listening devices using fluoroscopic scanning, which is considered a major breakthrough. The documentation also mentions the involvement of William M. Marcy and Alan F. Petit in this project.
