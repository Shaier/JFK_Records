This document is an FBI memorandum, dated November 28, 1961, from the FBI office in Salt Lake City to FBI Headquarters. The subject is a Cuban government attempt to acquire jute bags and hessian cloth through purchase or barter involving International Ore and Fertilizer Company of New York City. The memo also mentions CIA involvement, including the New York office, and unidentified individuals/entities. The memorandum includes photostats and information provided by Herbert Levy, Director of Central Operations at St. Regis Paper Company, regarding contacts with a CIA representative during Levy's time in Houston, Texas, focusing on gathering economic information related to India. The document classification is Secret, and it is paper based.
