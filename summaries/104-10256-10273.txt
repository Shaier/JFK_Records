This document is a memorandum from the Acting Director of the CIA to the Acting Director of the FBI, summarizing information provided by the CIA to the FBI regarding the Watergate incident. It details the CIA's relationships with individuals involved in the incident, including Frank Anthony Sturgis, Eugenio Rolando Martinez Careaga, Bernard L. Barker, Virgilio Gonzales, Everette Howard Hunt, James McCord, Juan Rigoberto Ruiz Villegas, Ross Ward Lambert, Manuel Ogarrio Daguerre, and Kenneth Harry Dahlberg. It also mentions the Robert R. Mullen Company and the General Foods Corporation, as well as the Association of National Advertisers. The memo emphasizes that the information is for the FBI's use only.
