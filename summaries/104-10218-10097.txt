This document is a classified message from the CIA station in Guatemala City to the Director, dated November 8, 1961. It provides details about an individual, referred to as A-1, who was denied entry into Guatemala on November 3rd due to lack of a tourist card. After causing a scene, A-1 returned to Guatemala on November 7th and was arrested on November 8th at the home of Rodrigo Asturias Amado, carrying two U.S. passports. A-1 was expelled from Guatemala on November 8th on charges of transporting communist propaganda and taken to the Mexican border near Tapachula. The arrest and expulsion were given prominent press coverage.
