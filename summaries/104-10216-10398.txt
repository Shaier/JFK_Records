This memorandum summarizes a meeting with AMLASH-1 in Paris between January 31st and February 3rd, 1965.  The discussion topics include a security briefing on the infiltration of Modesto Orlando Orozco into Cuba, plans for communication with operatives in Cuba, a resupply of S/W materials, a debriefing on a cache in Matanzas, potential recruitment targets within the Cuban Intelligence Service, upcoming FEU elections, sabotage precautions for locomotives being shipped to Cuba, contacts made by AMLASH-1, Cuban Intelligence Service personnel, information on various Cuban officials, conditions in Cuba, political developments, and Soviet influence. The document also mentions a few possible double agents.
