This memo discusses the Agency's involvement in the development of the Identi-kit, specifically the investment of funds and the role of Hugh C. McDonald. It also highlights the need to interview McDonald to understand the mention of U.S. Intelligence in an article and to ensure no classified information has been disclosed. The memo also mentions a school for Identi-kit users and the expectation of increased publicity. Finally, it indicates the memo is being forwarded to PSD to determine if other Agency components have used McDonald.
