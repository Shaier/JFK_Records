This document is a JFK Assassination System Identification Form and an associated Agent Report. It details agency information, record numbers, and document details, including subjects like Henry Beer and Joachim F. Pehl. The Agent Report reveals that on 4 March 1971 a check of the Provost Marshal's Office (PMO), US Army Berlin Brigade, West Berlin, APO New York 09742, were checked regarding SUBJECT'S step-son, Joachim S. Beer aka Joachim F. Pehl, DPOB: 4 December 1942, Berlin-Schoeneberg, and revealed Joachim Beer had a report of a fire on 19 December 1970 and was a complainant, disturbing the peace on 22 January 1971. This document was declassified on 18 October 1994.
