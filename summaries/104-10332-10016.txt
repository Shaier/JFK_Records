This document is a memorandum from the CIA to the Assassination Records Review Board regarding the release of the identities of human sources of intelligence in the JFK Collection. It expresses the CIA's concerns about the potential risks to these individuals and their families, as well as the impact on future intelligence operations. The memo outlines legal considerations and operational issues related to protecting sources and proposes a policy for managing source names in the JFK Collection. The document also includes specific cases for consideration.
