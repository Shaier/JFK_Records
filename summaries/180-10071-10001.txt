This document consists of a series of payroll authorization forms and related materials for Donald A. Purdy, Jr., who worked for the Select Committee on Assassinations (HSCA) of the U.S. House of Representatives between 1976 and 1978. It includes information about his social security number, position, salary, and the dates of employment actions such as appointments, salary adjustments, and termination. There's also a memo about a payroll certification process.
