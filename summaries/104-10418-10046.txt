This document discusses a contact between Philip Agee and Stephen M. Weissman, where Weissman claimed to have found archives with information on agency material. The document also mentions that Agee offered to arrange an introduction to Dewi Sukarno and a journalist who knew Sukarno personally for Weissman. Further investigation by JAGUAR revealed that the journalist in question may be William L. Oltmans, and they showed copies of letters mailed to "Phil and Angela," with one letter signed by Oltmans and two by Dewi Sukarno. The letters express interest in the Senate investigations regarding Indonesia. The document requests traces on Oltmans.
