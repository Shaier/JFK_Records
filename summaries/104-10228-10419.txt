This document is a classified message from Panama City to the Director, dated December 16, 1961. It requests a POA (Proof of Authority) for Vicente Herrero Diaz, a Cuban national born in Havana. It mentions his family history, his work in Cuba, and his association with Conte Aguero and an anti-communist front. The message states that Herrero Diaz has worked at market and audience research in Panama and was involved with a now-defunct magazine. It also indicates he is one of fifteen members of a local revolutionary chapter and produces radio programs. The message requests any derogatory traces and desires to use the subject for local public opinion research, monitoring, and supporting local JMRAKE activities.
