This document is a request for personnel security action concerning David Reed Fromer, a registrant. The request, dated May 5, 1967, comes from USAPSG and is directed to DCS-OPS USAINTC. The purpose is to conduct file checks at Headquarters and Local FBI on Fromer's parents to expedite a determination under AR 604-10. The document also mentions previous investigation information and asks that further investigation be considered. The document has been partially declassified.
