This document is a Central Intelligence Agency Information Report, dated May 18, 1963, concerning plans for a coup d'etat in Central Vietnam by the reformed VNQDD. The report details the organization's plans to seize control of Central Vietnam and subsequently negotiate with President Diem. The report also discusses the potential dangers of such a coup without simultaneous action in Saigon, warning of possible exploitation by the Viet Cong. A trusted follower of the leader, Luc, was sent to Saigon to discuss the plan with an oppositionist sympathizer and get his viewpoint. The report states that this was considered too dangerous.
