This document is an identification form for a record related to the JFK assassination. The record itself is a report titled "Fidel Castro's Coming Visit to Moscow and Algiers," dated April 23, 1963, and consists of one page. The document contains information regarding Fidel Castro's plans to visit Moscow for May Day celebrations and also visit Algeria. The report claims that Castro intended to communicate to Khrushchev that the USSR needed to assist Cuba while understanding that Cuba would not be a "docile satellite." The report indicates the information about Castro's plans came from a reliable source with excellent contacts in Cuban government circles.
