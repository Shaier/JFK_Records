This document is a journal addendum from the Office of Legislative Counsel dated July 6, 1978. It discusses:

1.  A liaison accompanying others to brief Thomas K. Latimer on the issue between the House Select Committee on Assassinations and the Agency, concerning sensitive information exposure.
2.  A call received from Spencer Davis regarding a former Agency employee, Newton S. Miler, and the Agency's position on retired employees like Raymond Close who took positions with foreign governments.
