This document is a memorandum for the record of a meeting between John McCone and the President on February 20, 1964. The topics discussed include a meeting with General Eisenhower, a sensitive operation in Mexico City, the interrogation of Nosenko, the question of aid to Spain, and the President's desire to distance the CIA from its "cloak and dagger" image. Action items include contacting Noel and Ambassador Woodward regarding Spanish actions related to trade with Cuba.
