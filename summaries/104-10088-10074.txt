This document is a CIA handbook chapter from 1974 describing the 201 system, a method for identifying and managing information on individuals of interest to the agency's operations. It outlines the components of the system (201 number, dossier, master record), the contents of the dossier, maintenance procedures, restrictions, information requests, dossier cancellation, machine listings, and the automatic opening of 201 files for specific categories of individuals. Annex B also outlines procedures for assigning 201 and IDN numbers to personalities within the Clandestine Services records system.
