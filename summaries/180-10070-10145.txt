This document is a collection of payroll authorization forms and related memoranda concerning Marlin Risinger's employment with the U.S. House of Representatives, specifically the Select Committee on Assassinations. The documents detail his appointment, salary adjustments, position titles (Document Clerk, Researcher), leave records, and payroll certifications, spanning from December 1976 to July 1978. The documents also include administrative information, authorizations, and approvals from various House officials.
