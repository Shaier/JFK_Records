This document is a collection of payroll authorizations and related paperwork for Edwin J. Lopez, who worked for the HSCA (House Select Committee on Assassinations) as a researcher in 1977 and 1978. The documents include forms for salary adjustments, a payroll certification, and a memo regarding new research employees. One document is a determination notification indicating that the social security number is redacted due to privacy concerns, and that this information is up for review in 2017.
