This document is a memorandum from the CIA regarding Karel van het Reve, founder of the Alexander Herzen Foundation in Amsterdam. The memorandum mentions that van het Reve is publishing the writings of Soviet dissenters and was granted a POA in June 1969 for use as an informant in Western Europe under Project AEEGGHEAD. The memo references attached clippings from the New York Times and Newsweek about van het Reve's activities.
