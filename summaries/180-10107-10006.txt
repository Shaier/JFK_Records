This document is a report of an interview with Anthony William Atzenhoffer conducted on October 16, 1978, by investigator Robert Buras. Atzenhoffer, who was a platoon sergeant in the Civil Air Patrol Cadets at Moisant Squadron in the 1950s, remembers Lee Oswald's name being on the roll but does not recall training or speaking with him. He also remembers Ed Voebel and that Ferrie taught some courses and that he took his first plane flight with Ferrie. Atzenhoffer reviewed photos and recognized Clay Shaw and Melvin "Pinky" Seeling. He also provided information about a party and Ferrie's plane.
