This document is a memorandum of a DCI presentation to the President's Foreign Intelligence Advisory Board on December 7, 1962. The presentation covered several topics including:

*   The status of the OXCART project, an aircraft project.
*   Budgetary matters, including the military intelligence budget and the CIA budget, with the DCI expressing concerns about the Defense Department's control over CIA funds.
*   The MONGOOSE project, a program to unseat Castro in Cuba.
*   The NRO agreement, concerning fiscal control over CIA projects, and a disagreement with the Secretary of Defense over withholding funds.
