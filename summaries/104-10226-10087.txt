This document is a CIA information report classified as SECRET. It discusses a planned uprising in Cuba by opposition groups, specifically the Unida Revolucionaria (UR), the Directorio Revolucionario Estudiantil (DRE), and the Movimiento Revolucionario del Pueblo (MRP). The report indicates that the French Embassy in Havana advised the UR not to participate. The report also mentions that the American Embassy in Paris reported information from the French ambassador in Havana on this agreement for a general uprising.
