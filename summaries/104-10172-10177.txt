This document is a classified message from JMBAR to the Director, dated November 24, 1961, concerning information related to BARR 0134. It references DIR 2543 and BARR 0173, discussing individuals including "Mrs. Geddes," Antonio Cuesta, and George Roppino. The message indicates that Cuesta continues to make inquiries about a boat, and reports that he may return to Prumen soon. It also mentions that A-4 is not aware of a Bark interest involving "Mrs. Geddes." The message ends with a comment about requesting details regarding Amabend-2 contacting a boat dealer on behalf of two Cubans attempting to purchase a high-speed boat.
