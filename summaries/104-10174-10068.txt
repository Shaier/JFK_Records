This document contains a series of classified messages, memorandums, and reports related to the activities and management of Edward G. Tichborn and Henry Lopez, contract agents working for the CIA, primarily in Mexico, and occasionally in other parts of Latin America. The documents cover various aspects of their work, including travel authorizations, financial matters, security clearances, project proposals, contract renewals, and assessments of their political leanings and suitability for different types of operations. The memos also detail their interactions with other agents and informants, as well as concerns about security, cover stories, and the effectiveness of their work.
