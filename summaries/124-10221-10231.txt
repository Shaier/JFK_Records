This document is an FBI record related to the JFK assassination. It is a report concerning Pedro Luis Diaz Lanz, Renaldo Blanco Hernandez, and Alberto Eusebio Mojena, all linked to Cuba and Miami. It references previous communications and notes that some information was provided by the United States Customs Office and classified "Secret" due to its source from the CIA. The document lists several informants and their respective dates of activity and sources of information.
