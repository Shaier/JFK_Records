This document is a collection of US intelligence records, including cable messages and reports, from the mid-1950s, concerning American communists and their contacts, particularly in Mexico. The records mention individuals like James Leigh White, Maurice Halperin, Noah Seborer, and others, and detail their activities, associations, and relationships with organizations and individuals of interest to US intelligence. The documents reference investigations, affiliations, and potential connections to espionage, the Trotsky murder, and other activities deemed subversive. The documents also include internal communication regarding the handling of certain individuals who may have previously worked for the agency and are seeking employment elsewhere.
