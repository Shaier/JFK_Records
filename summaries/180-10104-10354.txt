This document contains a collection of fact sheets and reports from the Department of Defense, primarily the Navy, in response to requests from the Select Committee on Assassinations regarding investigations related to Lee Harvey Oswald, Naval Investigative Service activities from 1960-1964, and other individuals and topics. The documents indicate that the Navy was unable to identify many of the requested records or individuals, and some records were referred to other agencies like the CIA and FBI. The documents also detail the structure of the ONI, its relationship to the CIA and FBI, and NIS activities regarding anti-Castro Cubans. Additionally, it provides information on Oswald's military service and addresses specific inquiries regarding photographs and other evidence related to the assassination.
