This document is a collection of internal payroll records and forms pertaining to James K. Conzelman's employment with the House Select Committee on Assassinations (HSCA) during the late 1970s. The documents include payroll authorization forms, salary adjustments, appointment dates, and a memo concerning payroll certification. The records also reflect Mr. Conzelman's position as a researcher with the HSCA, his salary increases, and the authorizing signatures from committee chairs such as Louis Stokes and Thomas Downing. Notably, there is a certification form asking employees to disclose if they are related to any current member of Congress.
