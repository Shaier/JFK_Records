This document is a request for PCS (Permanent Change of Station) overseas evaluation for Calvin W. Hicks, an OPS Officer with a GS-13 grade, planning to depart in September 1966. The proposed station is La Paz, Bolivia. The request includes information on seven dependents to accompany him and requests that medical examinations be scheduled. The request was submitted on May 10, 1966, and approved on June 2, 1966, with security approval granted. A Form 89 is attached in duplicate.
