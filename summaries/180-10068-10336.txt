This document contains personnel records related to Jane L. Downey's employment with the House of Representatives. It includes payroll authorization forms documenting changes in her position, salary, and employing office between 1977 and 1979. It also includes a memorandum from I. Charles Mathews informing the budget officer and chief clerk that Jane L. Downey had accepted the position of secretary for the John F. Kennedy Task Force, and a payroll certification form. The document has been reviewed by the Assassination Records Review Board and postponed in part to protect social security numbers.
