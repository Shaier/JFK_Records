This document is a "Notification of Establishment of Official Cover Backstop" dated August 15, 1961. It concerns an official cover backstop utilized for Joseph Piccolo. The document outlines that information regarding the cover arrangement is available in Military Cover, CCG, and requests notification when the cover arrangement is no longer required. It also stipulates that action must be taken to ensure that the subject employee is issued an Army W-2 Form for tax purposes. The document is signed by Robert E. Young, Chief of Military Cover, CCG.
