This document is a JFK Assassination System Identification Form from INSCOM/CSF, concerning a record related to DOD-affiliated personnel incident investigations. The record, dated November 22, 1968, includes information from the FBI regarding Ivan Marti, brother of Manuel Marti, and his connection to "New Horizons for Youth." The document also notes the subjects' addresses and references a characterization of "Horizons for Youth" as an exhibit.
