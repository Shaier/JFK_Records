This document is a notification from the Assassination Records Review Board regarding a record concerning HSCA staff payroll. The document, dated December 8, 1995, states that the document is postponed in part, with 2 postponements, due to the presence of Social Security numbers that were redacted to protect personal privacy. The Board based the determination on the historical interest of the document. The next review date is set for 2017. The board review was completed 10/24/95.

The document also includes payroll authorization forms for Charlotte Green, hired as a summer intern with the House Select Committee on Assassinations. Included is a "Certificate of Relationship/Nonrelationship" that declares that she is not related to any current member of Congress. A memo is included advising that Charlotte Green joined the committee staff on May 12, 1978, with a starting salary of $6240 per annum.
