This document is a memo from the FBI to the CIA, dated March 13, 1963, regarding Angela Prince Moutsos. The FBI requests information from the CIA regarding Moutsos's current residence in Cuba and any related activities. Reference is made to prior communications on this matter. The New York field office is also involved in this inquiry. A note indicates that Moutsos traveled to Cuba in December 1960 to join her husband, Pedro Moutsos, who was previously a CIA informant.
