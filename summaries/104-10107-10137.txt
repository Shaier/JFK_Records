This document is a memorandum from a CIA attorney regarding Herbert Itkin. It summarizes discussions held to investigate the ramifications of a confrontation with the New York FBI regarding Itkin. Additional information was requested and reviewed concerning Thomas L. Roberts, and Robert Delson. The memo outlines Roberts' possible affiliations with the FBI, CIA, and Itkin, as well as Delson's potential involvement in operational projects. Concerns are raised regarding Itkin's knowledge of Roberts' CIA affiliations, Roberts' financial dealings and potential prosecution for absconding with funds. Delson's connection to individuals with Communist affiliations is also highlighted as a concern.
