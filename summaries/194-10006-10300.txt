This document is an identification form from the JFK Assassination System related to a message regarding a potential counterintelligence (CI) interest involving Hans D. Kaminski. The message, dated March 20, 1972, discusses an incident where an unidentified person, "John the Dee Jay," attempted to elicit official information from members of a US Army artillery detachment in West Germany. The document details the incident, the individuals contacted, a description of "John the Dee Jay," and ongoing investigative efforts. It also notes that "John the Dee Jay" claims to have learned English in Moscow and has connections to the German Democratic Republic (GDR) army.
