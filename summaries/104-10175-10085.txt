This document is a collection of dispatches, memoranda, and other records relating to the CIA's interactions with a man named Alfonso Rudolph Wichtrich from the late 1940s through the 1950s. The records detail the CIA's assessment of his background, skills, potential for recruitment as an agent, and his various roles in intelligence operations, primarily in Panama and with connections to China. It also covers administrative aspects such as salary, budget, security checks, and coordination with other agencies.
