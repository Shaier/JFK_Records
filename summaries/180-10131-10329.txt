This document is a transcript of the testimony of Robert T. Shaw, given on May 16, 1978, before the House Select Committee on Assassinations (HSCA) regarding the JFK assassination. The testimony focuses on Shaw's work with the CIA in Mexico City, specifically concerning surveillance of the Cuban Embassy, telephone taps, and knowledge of Lee Harvey Oswald. The interview covers details of operations, personnel, and procedures within the Mexico City station in 1963.
