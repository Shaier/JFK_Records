This is a SECRET intelligence report summarizing the activities of a MINSK station for October 1996, covering liaison operations, general operational support, requirements/support from headquarters, embassy-related activities, and administrative matters. It includes details on interactions with foreign intelligence services, demarches received, referendum-related information, support for events, and general reporting. The report also touches on travel support, situational awareness reporting, updates on nuclear ICBMs, construction issues at the embassy, personnel matters, and TDY officer visits.
