This document is a Central Intelligence Agency (CIA) information report dated June 28, 1963, concerning Cuban exile plans. It details the claims of Amaury Fraginals Alonso, a leader in the alliance between several anti-Castro movements, regarding the continuation of attacks on Cuba and the launch of "Plan Omega." The plan involves reorganizing and arming guerrilla groups, incessant attacks on Russian bases and the Cuban army, and coordination for a general insurrection. The report also mentions that the alliance is receiving blueprints of major Cuban plants and describes a new adherent to the alliance, an anti-communist group within the Cuban army. Finally, it emphasizes the alliance's determination to fight Castro's regime, even at the risk of a nuclear conflict.
