This is a classified message from the Director to Santiago de Cuba, dated January 13, 1959. The message concerns a subject under ODENVY investigation for possible registration act violations. The message instructs that the subject should be dealt with under strict consular cover and that no commitments that might involve the US government should be made. It also mentions that the subject should not be construed as having ODYOKE approval for his activities. A comment mentions Frank Fiorini of Norfolk, VA, who claimed to be a 26 July arms buyer in Miami and is willing to cooperate 100% regarding information on rebels.
