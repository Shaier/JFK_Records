This document is a memorandum regarding Federico PALMIERI Guillermo, a former CIA agent who is writing a book titled "My Seven Years with CIA." It details his current exile from Guatemala and employment in San Salvador, as well as his statements about a publisher in Mexico City being interested in his book. It also mentions a memorandum from the Philadelphia office of Domestic Contact Service stating that PALMIERI had worked as a CIA agent for Roger RUSS. Copies of the memorandum are being placed in the OS files of individuals known to be affiliated with the Agency.
