This document is a routing and record sheet from the CIA dated 13 January 1961, regarding the transmittal of documentation for use of Manuel Artime y Buesa. It references a previous memo and includes comments about the documentation. It also contains a memorandum from the Intelligence Branch to the Chief, WH/4, and Lou Napoli regarding documentation, specifically a passport with a U.S. Non-Immigrant Visa, an Exit Permit, and an International Certificate of Vaccination.
