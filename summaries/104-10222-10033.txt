This document is a collection of personnel actions, fitness reports, and disability claims related to Robert M. Zambernardi, who worked for the CIA, specifically within the DDP/TSD and foreign field operations, primarily in Mexico City. The documents span from the late 1950s to the mid-1960s and detail his promotions, transfers, resignations, salary changes, and evaluations of his performance as a Photographic Technician. There are also notifications of a military cover backstop and disability claims for himself and his dependents.
