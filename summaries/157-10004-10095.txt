This document, dated March 17, 1964, is a report from the FBI in New Orleans regarding an alleged Cuban plot to assassinate the President of Panama. Orlando Piedra, a former Cuban Federal Police member, relayed information from a Cuban source that Castro was planning the assassination to incite unrest in Panama. Piedra shared this information with the Chief of the National Guard of Panama. The document states it contains no recommendations or conclusions from the FBI and is not to be distributed outside the agency. The information was orally furnished to representatives from the State Department, CIA, Army, Air Force, and Navy.
