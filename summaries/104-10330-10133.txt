This document consists of a memorandum and attachments related to questions posed by the Assassination Records Review Board (ARRB) concerning the CIA's commercial cover program, specifically regarding its connection to the publications industry, the number of participating companies, and the secrecy agreements used. The documents include responses to these questions, detailing the connections, numbers, and providing copies of the secrecy agreements used during different time periods. The memorandum requests that the material be returned to the CIA after the ARRB's review. The final document contains a listing of distribution recipients for the memo and attachments.
