This document is a transcript of a hearing before the Select Committee to Study Governmental Operations with Respect to Intelligence Activities (SSCI) on May 21, 1975. The hearing includes testimony from DCI William Colby, and addresses topics such as Operation Mongoose, assassination attempts on Fidel Castro and Patrice Lumumba, the ZR Rifle project, and the relationship between the CIA and criminal elements like Robert Maheu. The document contains redactions and references to classified information.
