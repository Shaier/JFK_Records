This document contains information regarding William Hamilton Martin and Bernon Ferguson Mitchell, two American defectors living in the USSR, who are attempting to leave the country. The information comes from a report recently received through Canadian liaison channels about a conversation between a Canadian professor, Dr. Hans Kunov, and Martin in Moscow. The Canadian Embassy was contacted regarding the defector's status. There is also an assessment of a host, Yuriy Igoryevich TorGOV. The Canadians advised that the FBI has received the information contained in the attachments.
