This is a contact report from January 25, 1963. The meeting's purpose was to inform Kulpa about Headquarters' agreement to his Congo trip and to continue instructions to Meinturff. Instructions from Holden had not yet been received, but were expected soon. Sarge Klohel agreed that the first six covers on the new Afrique Revolutionaire would be shot by K.K. Another development involved Dave Katts requesting photos from Gibson, who will purchase and forward negatives. The meeting also involved straightening out Tiptaft's accountings. The next meeting is scheduled for Tuesday, January 29, 1963.
