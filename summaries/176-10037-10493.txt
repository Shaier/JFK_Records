This CIA document, dated January 28, 1962, is an Information Report regarding comments made by Tran Van Ly concerning the desirability, problems, and consequences of a coup d'etat in South Vietnam. The report, sourced from a Vietnamese National Army officer, details Ly's views on the necessity of a coup, his concerns about the methods involved, and potential consequences. It also outlines the actions Ly believes should be taken in the event of a successful coup, including the establishment of a revolutionary committee composed of general officers.
