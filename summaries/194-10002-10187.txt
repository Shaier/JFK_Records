This document is an FBI report concerning the Vietnam Day Committee (VDC) in Berkeley, California, dated April 8, 1966. It contains information about the VDC's headquarters, members, activities, finances, and affiliations with other organizations, such as the Communist Party, Socialist Workers Party, Progressive Labor Party, and W.E.B. Du Bois Clubs of America. The report details protest demonstrations, anti-draft activities, and information on individuals associated with the VDC.
