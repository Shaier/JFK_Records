This document contains information related to the investigation of Luis Posada, tracked by the U.S. Drug Enforcement Administration (DEA) and its interactions with the Central Intelligence Agency (CIA), as recorded in various memoranda and cable communications between CIA headquarters, and field offices in Miami, Caracas, Paris, Rome, Buenos Aires and other locations from March 1973 through April 1976. The collection includes details about allegations of his involvement in narcotics trafficking, counterespionage activities, termination of his relationship with the Agency, immigration status, and other individuals of interest, such as his family, associates, and contacts. The documents also reference internal security concerns and requests for verification of certain information from other agencies, as well as summaries of intel from other internal and external sources.
