This document pertains to a request for reconsideration of the release of information concerning "Warrenton" in a specific report related to the JFK assassination records. It includes memorandums arguing for the protection of the Warrenton Training Center (WTC) as a covert CIA facility due to potential damage to national security, personnel safety, and operational capabilities if its affiliation with the CIA is revealed. It emphasizes the sensitivity of the activities conducted at WTC, its role in communications and intelligence, and the potential consequences of compromising its cover. The document also includes attachments such as annotated views of the WTC facility.
