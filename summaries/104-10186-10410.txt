This document is a classified message from the CIA station in San Salvador, dated January 20, 1965. It discusses a variety of topics related to intelligence operations and the surfacing of a Cuban defector named Rivera. The message details follow-up approaches, the need for discretion in handling information, concerns about surfacing names of trainees, and recommendations regarding a draft case paper. It also mentions a potential operation involving arresting individuals, knowledge of Salvadorans receiving GAN training in Cuba, and the possibility of catching individuals red-handed with evidence. The document includes various codenames and references to other CIA communications.
