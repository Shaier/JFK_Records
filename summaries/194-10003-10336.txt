This document is a JFK Assassination System Identification Form for a US Army document. The document concerns Edward Kaczmarczyk, born in West Germany in 1947, who immigrated to the US with his family in 1950 and was naturalized in 1969. The document contains information about his relatives, residences, employment, education, and security clearances. It also notes his travels abroad, including a period of detention in the USSR and a visit to England. The document is unclassified and was released with deletions.
