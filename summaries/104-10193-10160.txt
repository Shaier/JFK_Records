This document is a collection of intelligence reports and analysis concerning the National Democratic Party (NPD) in West Germany from the 1960s and early 1970s. The reports cover a range of topics, including the NPD's electoral performance, internal divisions, leadership, finances, and connections to neo-Nazi elements. There are also discussions of government efforts to monitor and counter the NPD's activities. The documents also contain analyses of broader social and political trends in Germany, such as reactions to student protests and the stability of the grand coalition government.
