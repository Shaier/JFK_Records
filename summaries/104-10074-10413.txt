This document is a CIA operational and source cover sheet, accompanied by a field information report concerning Jose Xavier Cortes Hernandez in Mexico and Cuba, dated December 1960.

The report discusses Cortes Hernandez, who represents himself as a priest, conveying information to the Cuban Ambassador to Mexico, Jose Antonio Portuondo Valdor. The information pertains to covert operations underway in Cuba, reactions to them, and sentiments regarding the Knights of Columbus. The report also mentions a church bombing and related political discussions, as well as Cortes Hernandez's interest in visiting Cuba.

The field comments section mentions doubts about Cortes Hernandez being a legitimate priest and his possible involvement in establishing a schismatic church in Cuba. The information is sourced from individuals with connections to the Cuban Embassy and the Roman Catholic Church, with varying degrees of reliability.
