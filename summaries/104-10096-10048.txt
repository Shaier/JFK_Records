This document consists of interoffice communications from the CIA regarding the release of information to the Senate Select Committee on Intelligence (SSCI). It details the review of Office of Security (OS) files, specifically concerning potential disclosure of DDO (Deputy Directorate for Operations) information and sensitive sources/methods. The memos discuss specific files and outline which information should be withheld or redacted before release to the SSCI, citing concerns over protecting intelligence sources and methods as well as potential third-agency rule violations.
