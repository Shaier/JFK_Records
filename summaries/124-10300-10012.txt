This document is an FBI communication from the Miami office to the Director of the FBI, concerning Jorge Raul Roblejo Lorie and Sergio Oswaldo Betancourt Collazo. It includes information about Roblejo's possible involvement in intelligence activities and his contacts with individuals connected to Cuban intelligence. The document also mentions the U.S. Immigration and Naturalization Service interviews of Cuban refugees and the distribution of FBI advisories. A limited dissemination of the information has been made due to the sensitivity of the information concerning the activities. The document includes enclosures of a LHM (Letterhead Memorandum) and indicates the document is classified as SECRET.
