This dispatch reports on the progress of AMDENIM-1, an individual working with various exile groups in the JMWAVE area. AMDENIM-1 is focused on developing maritime reception points along the northern coast of PBRUMEN, attempting to contact fishermen and establish communication systems. He is also working to identify radio operator and infiltration candidates, and to help the UR join the AMBUD-1 organization. Intelligence production is noted, and plans for future operations, including Operation Antonico III, are discussed. Funds expended for various purposes are also detailed.
