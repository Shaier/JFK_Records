This document is an identification form for a record related to the JFK assassination. It pertains to an incident investigation involving personnel affiliated with the Department of Defense. The document originated in the USA from the 771st MI DET and is dated September 24, 1968. The subject of the document is Manuel Marti. The record is currently released with deletions and was last reviewed on March 7, 1995. It also includes an exhibit cover sheet related to Manuel MARTI. It also includes a birth certificate.
