This document is a status report on a project focusing on the Institutional Revolutionary Party (PRI) in Mexico. The report details actions taken in February 1968, including the publication and distribution of a political newspaper and a magazine supplement, both related to the PRI. The report also mentions information gathered from various sources regarding personnel changes within the PRI and government expectations of the new leadership. The report also includes cultivation of political contacts, support for youth activities, media involvement through a student organization, and information gathering from various meetings and channels. The report also notes pending actions, effectiveness indicators, administrative and security situations, and ongoing efforts to maintain contact with student politicians due to ongoing political problems.
