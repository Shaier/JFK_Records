This document is an FBI report containing information related to the JFK assassination. The report includes details about FBI investigations into Cuban exile groups, specifically Cuban Power and the Movimiento Insurreccional de Recuperacion Revolucionaria (MIRR), and their suspected involvement in bombings and other activities in Miami and elsewhere. It also contains information about individuals associated with these groups, including their contacts, activities, and possible connections to the CIA. The report includes details about anonymous calls, informant reports, and various sources providing information to the FBI.
