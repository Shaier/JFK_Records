This document is a memorandum dated August 5, 1977, from the HSCA files. It contains information about the CIA station in Mexico City during the time of Lee Harvey Oswald's visit, including a list of individuals known to have been connected with the station. It also discusses the possibility of E. Howard Hunt's involvement with the CIA in Mexico City, along with organizational structure of the station. The information is primarily based on the testimony of David Phillips and interviews with Boris Tarasoff. The document includes a skeletal organizational chart of the CIA in Mexico City.
