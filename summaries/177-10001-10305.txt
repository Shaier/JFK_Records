This document is a CIA report from March 27, 1963, analyzing Cuban training of Latin American subversives. It estimates the number of Latin Americans trained, discusses travel routes, and details the types of training received in Cuba. It also assesses the employment of these trainees and countermeasures taken by various Latin American countries, as well as Cuban propaganda efforts in the region. The report includes country-by-country breakdowns and focuses on incidents, arrests, and influential figures involved.
