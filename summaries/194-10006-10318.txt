This document is an agent report from February 6, 1972, concerning an incident of possible counter-intelligence interest. The report details an interview with a security guard, Robert M. Creed, about his interactions with an individual named "John the Dee Jay" in Datteln, Germany. The report includes details of a phone conversation and a meeting at a bar, where John expressed interest in the Military Police presence, their vehicles, and a previous conversation. The report indicates that the security guard was to meet John again but declined, indicating he would be working. John replied he would be calling again to confirm the availability of the security guard.
