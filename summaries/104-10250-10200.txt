This office memorandum, dated October 21, 1953, addresses a request for information regarding Balmes Nieves Hidalgo, Jr. (#65077) and his potential availability for testimony before the Subversive Activities Control Board. It details an interview with William B. Caldwell, Chief, WH/FI, regarding Hidalgo's current employment status as a Contract Employee under the Travel Control Project in (Panama), his availability for testimony in the US, and potential FBI interviews. The memo also confirms Hidalgo's true name through a records review. Caldwell prefers that questions for Hidalgo be submitted via the CIA. The memo concludes that no further investigation is planned unless otherwise advised.
