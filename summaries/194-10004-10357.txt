This document is an identification form for a record related to the JFK assassination. The record pertains to an investigation of DOD-affiliated personnel. The document itself is a subject interview with Manuel Marti, a PFC in the US Army, conducted in Vietnam on March 25, 1969. Marti was questioned about his political views and potential communist affiliations. The interview covered his stance against communism, lack of involvement in communist activities, support for the US, and willingness to undergo a polygraph examination.
