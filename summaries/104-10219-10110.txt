This document is a memorandum from the Central Intelligence Agency regarding William H. Martin, a former NSA employee who defected to the Soviet Union in 1960. It discusses Martin's desire to see his parents in Canada and his concerns about potential legal ramifications. The memo outlines communications with the Canadian Department of State, the Department of State, and the Federal Bureau of Investigation regarding Martin's situation and legal status. It also discusses the potential for media attention and the need for a coordinated response.
