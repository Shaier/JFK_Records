This document is a directive from the Cuban General Directorate of Intelligence (DGI) to its intelligence representatives in the Dominican Republic, dated January 11, 1965. It outlines specific priorities and objectives for intelligence gathering, focusing on collective action against Cuba, the status of leftist forces, the Dominican Armed Forces, counterrevolutionary organizations, repressive bodies, political groups, and relations with the United States. The directive also includes guidelines on important and necessary information to be collected regarding the national economy, government structure, and key figures.
