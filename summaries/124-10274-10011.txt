This document is a memorandum discussing the expansion of foreign liaison efforts. It addresses concerns about personnel requests, particularly in relation to existing narcotics investigations and the President's instructions. The memorandum also provides comments and recommendations for specific areas, including Rio de Janeiro and Kuala Lumpur. In Rio de Janeiro, the agency suggests reopening their office while reducing staff in Buenos Aires. In Kuala Lumpur, they propose exploring the possibility of opening an office in Singapore instead due to the large number of CIA personnel already present.
