This document is an FBI report dated September 20, 1968, regarding YURI NOSENKO and his knowledge of various KGB operations and personnel. It compares information provided by NOSENKO with information from CIA records and investigates discrepancies in his accounts. The report details NOSENKO's involvement with different operations, including those targeting American Embassy personnel and tourists, and highlights inconsistencies in his recollection of events and individuals. It includes information obtained from interviews, internal reports, and other sources to assess the accuracy and credibility of NOSENKO's claims.
