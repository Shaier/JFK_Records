This document is a CIA dispatch from the Chief of Station in Rio de Janeiro, dated June 20, 1962, regarding SMEDIFY information concerning Tadeusz Szulc, including SMEDIFY's traces on him. It also includes information regarding Ignacy Sachs, who is mentioned in the attachment. The dispatch includes source information from 1961, outlining Sachs' reassignment within the Polish Ministry of Foreign Trade and his new role after a period of disgrace. It also includes a memorandum consisting of selected entries from Szulc's card, with information from 1947 to 1952, detailing his work, connections, activities, and relationships with various individuals and organizations.
