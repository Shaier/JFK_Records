This document is a historical staff report from the Central Intelligence Agency, specifically from the Clandestine Service Historical Series. It details counterintelligence operations of the Soviet Russia Division from 1946-1965. Volume I contains information regarding the CIA's assistance to the Warren Commission. This includes providing information, preparing chronologies, and answering questions about Lee Harvey Oswald's time in the Soviet Union. Specific attachments include items related to Oswald, such as the "Oswald Chronology," name lists, and photographs. Additionally, the document contains source references used in the report.
