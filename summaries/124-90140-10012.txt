This document is an FBI record related to the JFK assassination investigation. It's a memo from S.J. Papich to D.J. Brennan, dated September 3, 1963, concerning the "Permanent Student Committee for Travel to Cuba." The memo contains information from a CIA source about individuals involved with the committee, including their political leanings and potential affiliations with communist ideologies or organizations. The document is classified as Secret and is being directed to the attention of the Internal Security and Nationalities Intelligence Sections.
