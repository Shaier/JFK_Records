This document is a dispatch from the Chief of Station, JMWAVE, forwarding a debriefing report about Santiago SANCHEZ de la Torre. The report details SANCHEZ's activities with the MRR (Movimiento Revolucionario del Pueblo) in Cuba, his interactions with other members, his evasion of authorities, and his eventual escape to the US. It also contains information about other individuals connected to the MRR, including those who were arrested and those who may be working for the government. Attachments include lists of SANCHEZ's friends working in power plants and a report on Angel BENCOMO, whom SANCHEZ believes was responsible for the MRR's downfall.
