This is a memorandum summarizing the Senate Select Committee's testimony on February 4, 1974 regarding Presidential Campaign Activities. The memo details the events of the day, including the order of witnesses, agreements made prior to the hearing, and key topics discussed during the testimonies of Mary McGillen, Robert Ritchie, and Martin Lukoskie. It also mentions concerns about the Committee's line of questioning, the information they were seeking, and the potential need to involve oversight committees.
