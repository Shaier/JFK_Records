This document contains correspondence and reports from the CIA's Los Angeles office regarding Alpha 66 activities in Los Angeles during 1970 and 1971. The reports detail information obtained from a freelance reporter, Johnny O'Donnell, who had gained the confidence of Alpha 66 members. The documents discuss the organization's structure, key members, fund-raising efforts, training camps, and planned raids into Cuba. The correspondence also indicates headquarters' interest in these activities and a desire for further elaboration and amplification of the information. The document also includes information such as newspaper clippings, propaganda, notices of meetings and photographs of people affiliated with the organization.
