This document is a collection of Central Intelligence Agency (CIA) records related to Manuel Ray y Rivero, a Cuban exile leader. It includes cable traffic, memoranda, intelligence reports, and other notes spanning from 1960 to 1978. The records detail Ray's activities, relationships, and the CIA's involvement with him and his organization, JURE, including their plans to infiltrate Cuba, efforts to unify the exile community, and concerns about Ray's political orientation and reliability.
