This document is a memorandum from Rob Roy Ratliff to General Scowcroft regarding a request from the Department of Justice for information on the use of mail in operations related to the 40 Committee and its predecessors. The memo details findings regarding mail techniques, including propaganda mailings, testing mail within denied areas, and special covert operations such as forged letters, black letters, and operations designed to appear as if items were mailed from denied areas. It also mentions the potential for mail intercepts to be used as a source of intelligence and references to Mongoose files suggesting potential mail intercept operations related to Cuba.
