This document is a JFK Assassination System Identification Form for a report of investigation (dated 07/29/69) regarding Paul Arnold Marriott. The investigation concerns customs violations, specifically the importation of captured enemy weapons. The document details agencies involved, subjects, classification, restrictions, and comments about the investigation.  Attachments include photographs of the weapons and relevant documentation like Bills of Lading and shipping documents.  The document was reviewed by AFOSI for public disclosure on 14 AUG 95.
