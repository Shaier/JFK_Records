This document discusses a plan to deliver a special airdrop map to Miami for "Clarence" after a previous map was deemed unsuitable. The new map was prepared by an engineer. The message details communication efforts, including contacting Robert M. Geddes, Ralph Diaz Hanscom, and potential contact with Hector Febles. It also mentions the requested supplies, including arms and ammunition for 423 men plus 100 pistols. The drop signals would consist of one red light, one white light, and another red light.
