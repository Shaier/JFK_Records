This document is an identification form related to the JFK assassination, specifically concerning the investigation of DOD-affiliated personnel incidents. The document references a local agency check conducted by the FBI regarding Manuel Marti. The FBI in New York has no file information on Manuel Marti other than what may appear in reports on Marti's father that were furnished to the 108th Military Intelligence Group. The report was submitted on November 22, 1968, by Harold Bloom of the 108th MI Group.
