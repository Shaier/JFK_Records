The document is a transcript of a hearing held on May 6, 1976, before the Senate Select Committee to Study Governmental Operations With Respect to Intelligence Activities. The testimony is from Robert G. Halley, a former CIA Chief of Station, accompanied by John Rigall and Seymour R. Bolten. The hearing covers topics such as the CIA's operations in Miami targeting Cuba, Operation Mongoose, relationships with Cuban exile groups, investigations related to the Kennedy assassination, and contacts with other agencies such as the FBI and Customs.
