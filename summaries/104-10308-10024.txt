This document is a memorandum concerning the Cuban Revolutionary Council (CRC). It describes the CRC as an organization formed in New York City in 1961, under the guidance of the U.S. government, to unify Cuban exile groups. The document details resignations, coalitions, agreements, in-fighting, political aspirations, organizational structure, and financing related to the CRC. One finding mentions exile unrest in 1963 due to travel restrictions. Another finding involves a dispatch from JMWAVE to Headquarters regarding restructuring the CRC and concerns about non-CIA support to Cuban exiles and the underground.
