This document is a memorandum regarding the payment to Rivard G. Tichborn (P). It details the processing of Tichborn's accountings for an advance given to him in connection with his Cuban trip. The remaining half of his salary, $650.00, was processed through Finance and Cover channels, with the check drawn on Ashmead, Haniford Associates. Cover Division said the check would be forwarded to Ashmead, Haniford Associates in Philadelphia, who would then mail it to Tichborn's (P) California bank.
