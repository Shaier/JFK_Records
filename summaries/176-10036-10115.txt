This document is an identification form for a JFK assassination record. The document is a CIA information telegram dated July 16, 1963, consisting of two pages. The subject matter pertains to the coup d'etat plans of Tran Kim Tuyen's group in South Vietnam. The report is based on information from a high-level Vietnamese official with extensive contacts among government leaders. The document has been sanitized and reviewed as of September 10, 2000.
