This document is a dispatch from the Chief of Station in Helsinki regarding Tabe Maria Ingeborg SLI's immigrant visa interview. SLI was interviewed on July 7, 1965, about her association with Soviets. She stated she had met Soviet diplomats, but had not been approached for cooperation. She was given her visa after the interview. The dispatch also notes that SLI's traces show she was acquainted with Grigoriy GOB and Georgiy STEINING. There are several attachments included: a translation of the traces on SLI, a copy of her visa, her birth record, and her marriage certificate. Her U.S. address is listed as Fort Hood, Texas. The document includes distribution information and a reference to the dispatch symbol.
