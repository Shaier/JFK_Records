This document is a CIA report dated January 18, 1996, detailing suspected Cuban security penetrations within the Cuban human rights/dissident movement. The report, classified as SECRET and WNINTEL NOFORN, summarizes information collected between 1992 and mid-August 1995, focusing on potential security risks posed by individuals within dissident groups. It identifies individuals suspected of being Cuban security sources or provocateurs based on specific incidents and behaviors that raised concerns among dissident leaders. The report includes descriptions of interactions, observations, and reported statements that suggest these individuals might be working to undermine the movement. It also mentions reported connections to Cuban government officials and intelligence figures.
