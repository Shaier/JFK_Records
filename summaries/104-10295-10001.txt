This document is a memorandum for the record summarizing the S/W (secret writing) training and issuance of materials to agent AMLASH/1 in Paris, France, from August 17-22, 1962. The training was conducted by Richard M. Fallucci. The document outlines the training process, the materials provided, accommodation addresses, and re-supply arrangements. It also details the required and optional indicators for communication between the agent and the organization, including control indicators.
