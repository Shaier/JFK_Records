This document is a memorandum from the CIA to the FBI and the U.S. Secret Service, regarding information received about a potential link to the assassination of President Kennedy. The information was provided by an individual in Rio de Janeiro named Laszlo Kovacs, who claims to have information about a man named Vlasenco who allegedly stated the assassination was ordered by "Brezhnev and Fidel Castro." The CIA representative in Rio de Janeiro interviewed Kovacs, who provided a contradictory story and alluded to his need for money. The CIA advises no further contact with Kovacs. They have no identifiable traces on Kovacs or Vlasenco.
