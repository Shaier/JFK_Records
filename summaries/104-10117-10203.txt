This document is a memorandum detailing the CIA's use of Brown and Root, Inc. and associated individuals and organizations. It lists Brown & Root employees approved for contact use, mentions the Brown Foundation, and discusses the Vernon Fund, including its board members and funding of organizations like the World Confederation of Organizations of the Teaching Profession (WCOTP). It also explores other foundations like San Jacinto, M.D. Anderson, and Marshall Foundation, along with individuals linked to these organizations and their connections to CIA projects. The document includes covert security approvals and clearances for various individuals and mentions the scrutiny these entities faced from publications like Ramparts and the New York Times.
