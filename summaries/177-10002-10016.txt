This document, dated February 18, 1963, outlines Cuban financing of subversive operations in Latin America. It describes instances of cash couriers, including a Venezuelan politician with currency in a suitcase and a Nicaraguan exile carrying cash for rebels. It also mentions Ecuadorean troops raiding a guerrilla training camp and a Guatemalan defector detailing how Cuba sends cash to Mexico for weapons. The document further describes bank transfers for political activity, a Brazilian with a suitcase of ammunition, and waves of communist bank robberies in Venezuela, Peru, and Argentina.
