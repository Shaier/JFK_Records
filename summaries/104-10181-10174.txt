This document, dated March 1978, is a secret communication from the CIA regarding William Gaudet and Antonio Veciana. It provides biographical information on Gaudet, including his date of birth, past addresses, and past associations with the CIA. It mentions his knowledge of Latin America and his claim of being an "operative" for the agency, which the document suggests is exaggerated. It notes Gaudet had previously received a Mexican tourist visa in New Orleans, and the number preceded Lee Harvey Oswald's. The document also provides Antonio Veciana's full name and date of birth. It originates from an office in Europe with routing information indicating it's directed towards specific CIA departments and individuals.
