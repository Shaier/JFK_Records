This document is a memo from the FBI to Brennan regarding Levi Laub's unauthorized travel to Cuba. It references a CIA letter and information received from the British Intelligence Service about Laub being in Cuba during a specific period. The memo directs the information to the Subversive Control Section and mentions the possibility of interviewing Gavin Young, a British newspaperman who was also in Cuba. The CIA has no objection to the declassification and/or, release of CIA information, with some exceptions, and it is marked as "SECRET".
