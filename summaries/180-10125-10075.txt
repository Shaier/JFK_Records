This document is a U.S. House of Representatives certification report for the Select Committee on Assassinations, covering the accounting period from February 1st to February 28th, 1979. It lists employees, their positions, annual salaries, gross pay earned during the period, and any remarks, such as termination dates. The report also includes a payroll summary with the number of employees and payroll expenditures for clerical, professional, and investigative categories, and a certification statement regarding employee duties and relationships with members of Congress.
