This document is a memorandum from the Domestic Collection Division to the Miami Field Office regarding Case 57845. It requests the Miami Field Office to show attached photographs of an unidentified man to a subject for identification. The man in the photograph was published in the Warren Commission report on the Kennedy assassination and has been mentioned in news media critical of the Warren Commission. The man visited the Cuban and Soviet Embassies in Mexico City during October 1963 and is described as approximately 35 years old, of athletic build, about 6 feet tall, with a blond receding hairline and balding top, and wearing khakis and a sport shirt. The document also includes a confidential memo identifying a KGB officer who helped the Cuban group, about 15, attending the course.
