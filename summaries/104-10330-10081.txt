This document is a classified fax transmission from Michelle Combs to Barry Harrelson, dated March 31, 1997. The subject is DS&T and DO Briefing Questions related to the JFK assassination. Attached are the final versions of the DS&T and DO briefing questions with additions to the DO list reflecting conversations held on March 19 and 20. It includes questions about HTLINGUAL project, Castro Assassination plots, CIA relationships to journalists and publishing companies, searches for relevant records at the CIA Records Center, queries regarding personnel, operations, and funding related to Cuba and the Soviet Union. There are also specific inquiries about Project QKENCHANT and Clay Shaw's involvement. Additional pages discuss previous testimony and documents released related to the JFK assassination.
