This document consists of several letters and related materials. They detail the attempts of an individual named Sergyj Czornonoh to share information about Lee Harvey Oswald's potential involvement in the assassination of President John F. Kennedy. Czornonoh claims to have shared this information with American officials in Bulgaria in 1963, prior to the assassination. The correspondence includes communications with British and Russian embassies, US government officials, and legal aid services, revealing Czornonoh's efforts to share his story and concerns about government surveillance and manipulation.
