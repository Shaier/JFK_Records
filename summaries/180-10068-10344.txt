This document is a collection of records related to Jeffrey Facter's employment with the House Select Committee on Assassinations (HSCA). It includes a final determination notification from the Assassination Records Review Board, a form identifying the document as part of the HSCA staff payroll records, Facter's personal leave record, a memo regarding payroll certification, and payroll authorization forms showing various salary adjustments and his termination date. The payroll authorization forms also show the HSCA Chairman was Louis Stokes.
