This document contains the final determination notification from the Assassination Records Review Board regarding staff payroll records. The document outlines the postponement of information, the reasons for the board's actions, and the date of the next review. It also includes various payroll authorization forms for Ann Furnald Taylor from 1976-1978, detailing her employment with the U.S. House of Representatives. It includes job titles such as "Researcher" and "Senior Researcher". There is also a memo regarding payroll certification relating to the relationship of staff employees to current members of congress.
