This memorandum discusses a conversation between the President of Mexico and Mr. Winston Scott, the Chief of Station in Mexico. It outlines the President of Mexico's request to inform President Eisenhower about his views on their friendship, the invitation to President Dorticos of Cuba, and his intention not to invite Fidel Castro to Mexico. Additionally, it mentions the President of Mexico's delight in the decision to eliminate Castro and hints at CIA intentions shared with the Mexican President regarding Castro.
