This document is a collection of reports, memos, and telegrams, primarily from 1962 and 1963, concerning Cuban involvement in subversive activities throughout Latin America. The subjects covered include arms smuggling, electronic communications, training of guerrillas, propaganda efforts, and financial support for pro-communist groups. The documents originate from a variety of US intelligence agencies, and assess the extent and impact of Cuban subversion across numerous Latin American countries. The collection also touches upon differing views within the Communist world on how to approach revolution in the region.
