This document is an annex to a chapter of a CIA handbook detailing the 201 system, a method for identifying and managing information on persons of interest to the Operations Directorate. It covers topics such as 201 numbers, dossiers, master records, and indexes, as well as procedures for opening, maintaining, purging, and restricting access to 201 files. Machine listings of 201 personalities and requests for information are also described. Finally, there is an old document from 1960 concerning the CS's (Clandestine Services) procedures for the 201 numbers and IDN numbers.
