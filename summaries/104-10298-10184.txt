This document is a classified message from JWAVE to the Director, with information for DCI, DDP, ADDP, CA GA/PRG, CA/PROP, S/C. It discusses a Miami Herald article by James Buchanan about a change in leadership among Cuban exiles, with Manuel Ray replacing Miro, backed by various Latin American leaders and with the alleged blessing of the United States. The article also mentions Adolf A. Berle Jr.'s involvement and implies a new US-sponsored Cuban effort. The message further notes the resulting furor within the local Cuban community and the need to determine the truth of the story. The message concludes with checks revealing mixed opinions about the accuracy of the article and plans to assess the impact and potentially discredit the story.
