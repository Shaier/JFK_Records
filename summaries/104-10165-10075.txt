This document contains various reports, letters, and memoranda related to Clemard Joseph Charles, detailing his alleged involvement in political plots in Haiti, his relationship with Duvalier and US officials, and concerns about his business activities and associations. The documents span a period from 1969 to 1976 and involve communications between various US government agencies including the CIA, the State Department, and the FBI. They also document efforts to evaluate Charles's reliability as an informant and the security implications of his contacts.
