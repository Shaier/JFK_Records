This document is a collection of payroll authorization forms and related documents for Patricia M. Orr, an employee of the U.S. House of Representatives Select Committee on Assassinations from 1976 to 1978. It includes information such as her social security number, employing office, position title, gross annual salary, and dates of salary adjustments. There is a memorandum regarding payroll certification related to the staff employee's relationship to any current member of Congress. There are also notifications and records of review regarding postponement of social security numbers and subsequent release of the information under JFK Assassination Records Collection Act.
