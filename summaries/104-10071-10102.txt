This document is a dispatch from the CIA station in Oslo, Norway, to CIA headquarters. It transmits untranslated clippings from Norwegian newspapers regarding the visit of Bobby Seale, a leader of the Black Panther Party, to Oslo in March 1969. The clippings include information about Seale's views on the struggle between races in the U.S., the Black Panther Party's goals, and their cooperation with other revolutionary groups. The dispatch also includes the CIA's translation of one of the newspaper articles.
