This document is a memorandum discussing the AMWORLD project, specifically related to influencing the political landscape in Latin America. It outlines strategies involving key figures such as Figueres, Uduber, Artime, and Betancourt, as well as organizations like the MRR and publications like "Combate." The goal is to promote the acceptability of AMWORLD and AMBIDDY-1 by the moderate left and to neutralize potential opposition. The memorandum details plans to influence the student body of the School for Inter-American Development, and to manage the perception of certain political figures.
