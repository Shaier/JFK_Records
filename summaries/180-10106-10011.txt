This document contains an Identification Form, Investigation Interview Schedule, and a synopsis of an interview with Charles Steele, Jr. conducted by the House Select Committee on Assassinations on April 13, 1978. Steele admitted to passing out Pro-Cuba leaflets with Lee Harvey Oswald but refused to answer questions about his prior knowledge of Oswald. He stated he was offered $5.00 to pass out the leaflets and quit after reading them. He also described a second male involved and identified photos of Clay Shaw and Oswald. The interview occurred at Steele's residence in New Orleans, and Steele requested a copy of his Warren Commission testimony.
