This document is an FBI memorandum dated August 17, 1961, regarding Ismael Rodriguez, comptroller of Cubana Airlines in New York City. He is identified as a source of information for the Bureau and has been used by the CIA as a courier for transmitting material between the U.S. and Cuba related to CIA operations. The memorandum directs this information to the attention of the Nationalities Intelligence Section. The document is classified as Secret.
