This is a memorandum for the record regarding the release of stations and bases related to JFK documents. It details a phone conversation about how to treat German and Swiss locations not specifically addressed in a previous memo, instructing that they be treated the same as the primary station/base listed in that memo, referencing Bern, Lausanne, Geneva, Frankfurt, Hamburg, Munich, Stuttgart, Berlin, and Bonn. The information was relayed to ARRB staff via voice mail.
