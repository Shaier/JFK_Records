This document is a U.S. House of Representatives payroll certification report for the Select Committee on Assassinations. It covers accounting periods from 10/01/77 to 10/31/77 and 8/1/77 to 8/31/77, detailing employee names, positions, salaries, and gross pay. The report also includes an office payroll summary with information on expenditures, number of employees, and salary projections.
