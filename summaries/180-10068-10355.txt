The document is a collection of records related to Dominick K. Giangrasso's employment with the House Select Committee on Assassinations (HSCA) in 1977. The records include payroll authorization forms, memoranda concerning his hiring and termination, and his resume. The records indicate he was hired as a Staff Investigator, assigned to the Martin Luther King, Jr. Task Force, at a salary of $24,000. However, he resigned shortly after and was granted administrative leave until the end of October 1977. His resume details his previous experience as a Baltimore City police officer, specializing in homicides and undercover work.
