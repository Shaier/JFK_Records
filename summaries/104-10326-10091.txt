This document summarizes information obtained from NOPIGGIN on retired KGB Colonel Oleg M. Nechiporenko, known for his involvement with Lee Harvey Oswald. Nechiporenko was in the U.S. promoting his book. He is described as lonely and potentially approachable under a commercial guise.

The document details Nechiporenko's past assignments, including his time in Mexico and Cuba, his involvement with Vietnamese affairs, and his later role as an instructor at the KGB school. He retired in 1991 and has since been involved with a counter-terrorist training institute and writing his book.

NOP attempted to elicit updated CI information from Nechiporenko but was unsuccessful. The document also notes that Nechiporenko knows no one at the Russian Mission in New York. Nechiporenko's current address and contact information in Moscow are provided.
