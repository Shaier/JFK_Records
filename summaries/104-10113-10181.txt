This document is a memo from the Chief of the Security Branch, OS, regarding several individuals, including WARNILOW, Cook, QUINE, HESY, HUSZ, and BIMBOTY. The memo indicates that a review of the subjects' files reveals they do not meet the requirements set forth in recent instructions. It requests that the office be furnished with copies of current Personal History Statements of each of the subjects so that appropriate security checks may be initiated. The memo is dated August 5, 1947.
