This document is a JFK Assassination System Identification Form containing an Agent Report dated November 19, 1968. It concerns Manuel Marti, a Private First Class in the U.S. Army, born in San Juan, Puerto Rico on May 17, 1946. The report details a character reference interview with Ramona Rodriguez, a neighbor of Marti's family in the Bronx, New York, from 1951 to 1965. Rodriguez describes Marti as a quiet, friendly, serious-minded young man from a respectable home with strict parents, suggesting he would be financially responsible and is a loyal United States citizen. She recommends Marti for a position of trust and responsibility with the United States Government.
