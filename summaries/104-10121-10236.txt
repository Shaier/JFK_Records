This document is a memorandum regarding foreign awards and decorations presented to individuals. It outlines the process for retaining these awards under a new public law, requiring approval from the Head of the Agency and concurrence from the Secretary of State. The Central Cover Staff is handling liaison for State Department concurrence. The memorandum requests a review of the attached roster for the Director of Personnel's approval, stating that memorandums are not required for the awards, and the approval is for record-keeping purposes. The document includes a list of individuals, their awards, the country of origin for each award, and the date they were received.
