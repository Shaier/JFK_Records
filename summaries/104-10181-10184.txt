This document is a message regarding Antonio Carlos Veciana Blanch, a former member of the People's Revolutionary Movement in Cuba and founder of Alpha 66 Group. He was granted POA for use with the Revolutionary Movement of People but no record exists if he ever contacted. He was also registered to LNGLUE but terminated without prejudice. At the time the message was written, he was employed with Officer of Capital Development at the U.S. Agency for International Development (AID), La Paz, Bolivia. An additional comment states that Veciana was mentioned and had been denounced as a CIA agent.
