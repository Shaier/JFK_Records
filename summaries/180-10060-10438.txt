This document includes several pages of payroll authorization forms and related memos. The forms pertain to Dan L. Hardway, who worked as a researcher for the House Select Committee on Assassinations. They show changes in his salary and position between 1977 and 1978. The document also includes a memorandum regarding the committee's monthly payroll certification, requiring employees to disclose any relationships with current members of Congress.
