This document pertains to a file about the "KD Factor" photographic surveillance operation conducted by the CIA, specifically Folder 11, covering activities from 1958-1973, primarily in Mexico. It documents the file's history, revisions, coordination activities, and related intelligence communications about the KDFACTOR operation, its connections with other projects and agencies, and the circumstances surrounding its termination. Documents include memoranda, telegrams, and records of review, detailing subjects as photograph analyses, equipment, personnel, activities, meetings, expenses, and internal disputes.
