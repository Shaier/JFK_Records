This document is a final determination notification from the Assassination Records Review Board regarding staff payroll records related to the HSCA. It indicates that parts of the document were postponed due to privacy concerns related to social security numbers. A review board completed a review and the document was partially released on December 8, 1995. There are 7 pages total, some of which contain payroll authorization forms and internal memos regarding Ms. Gale Lynn Oppenberg, who was employed by the Select Committee on Assassinations as a Research Attorney. The documents show her starting date, salary, and termination of employment. There is also a form certifying that she is not related to any current Member of Congress.
