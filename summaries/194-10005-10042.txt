This document is a JFK Assassination System Identification Form containing information about an investigation into Robert Scheer. The record originates from the INSCOM/CSF agency and has the record number 194-10005-10042. The document itself is a confidential paper. The document summarizes a review of investigative files and notes that the case was adjudicated favorably and forwarded with a recommendation for the subject to be deemed acceptable for military service. The review of the file includes agency checks with the FBI, CSC, I&NS, HCUA, and OSI. The document was originally classified but was regraded as unclassified on April 18, 1963.
