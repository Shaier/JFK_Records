This document is a dispatch from the Chief of Station in Tokyo to the Chief of the Soviet Bloc Division and the Chief of the Far East Division regarding the Rybat Mipasha Redtop operation, which concerns Beheiren (Japan "Peace for Vietnam" Committee) and four U.S. Navy deserters. The dispatch includes a memorandum detailing the origin and development of Beheiren, their activities, and their involvement in the case of the four U.S. Navy deserters, including their escape to Moscow. The memo also lists Japanese individuals identified as Beheiren supporters and sympathizers, as well as Americans and other foreign individuals who have been in contact with or cooperated with Beheiren. The dispatch is classified as SECRET TRYBA and marked particularly sensitive information.
