This document is a transcript of a hearing held before the Senate Select Committee to Study Governmental Operations With Respect to Intelligence Activities on June 19, 1975. The hearing involves the testimony of James Angleton, a former high-ranking CIA official, who is questioned about various topics, including his involvement with Israeli intelligence, potential breaches of security within the Agency, and the Kennedy assassination. The document also touches on issues such as Soviet intelligence activities, the Watergate scandal, and the effectiveness of U.S. counterintelligence.
