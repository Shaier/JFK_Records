This document is a memorandum for the record dated June 1, 1964, detailing a conversation between members of the SR Division and the Chief of Station (COS) in Helsinki. The discussion focuses on the Warren Commission's inquiry concerning the timetable of Lee Harvey Oswald's stay in Finland in October 1959, including his contact with the Soviet Consulate there. It includes information about travel times and the retention period for passenger lists in Helsinki. The document also mentions a U.S. consular official named Robert Fulton and suggests cabling the Helsinki Station for additional information not covered by the State Department's inquiry.
