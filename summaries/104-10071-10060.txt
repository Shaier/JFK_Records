This document is a record sheet from the John F. Kennedy Assassination Records Act of 1992. It details the routing of a dispatch from the Chief of Base, Washington, regarding WOMUSE "Peace" Demonstrations, to the Chief, DO/I. The dispatch encloses literature from the Catholic Association for International Peace (CAIP) and materials concerning its 40th Annual Conference. The items were received by Oliver H. CORBUSTON, who had joined the CAIP for cover purposes.
