This document pertains to the 2025 release of information under the President John F. Kennedy Assassination Records Act of 1992. It involves a request from headquarters for station assistance in contacting a former employee residing in Caracas to deliver information related to complying with the Act. The document also informs the recipient that their name may appear in documents being reviewed for public release and explains the process and potential implications of such a release, including the possibility of requesting that the name be redacted if specific criteria are met. The letter also provides some information about the concerns raised and questions answered about the issue. It covers grounds for postponing public disclosure of records, actions stations can take, and why the station's name would be in the JFK Assassination records.
