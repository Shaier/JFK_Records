This document is a JFK Assassination System Identification Form. It pertains to Robert Scheer, a pre-inductee, and his employment history at Repeat-O-Type Stencil Manufacturing Company. The document is an Agent Report from the FBI, dated October 19, 1958. The report details Scheer's employment dates and pay rate, as revealed from payroll files examined on October 15, 1958. The document has been released with deletions after a review by the FBI.
