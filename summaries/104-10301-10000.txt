This document is the chronology section of a draft history of John A. McCone, the sixth Director of Central Intelligence. It covers the period from November 1960 to June 1964. The chronology details McCone's activities, meetings, and decisions related to key events like the Bay of Pigs, the Cuban Missile Crisis, and the escalating situation in Vietnam. It includes his interactions with Presidents Kennedy and Johnson, other government officials, and foreign leaders, as well as his views on intelligence matters, covert operations, and national security issues.
