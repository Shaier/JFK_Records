This document is a JFK Assassination System Identification Form for a record related to investigations of incidents involving Department of Defense-affiliated personnel. The document concerns Manuel Marti, along with local agency checks on Ivan and Blanca Marti. It is a paper document that has been declassified with deletions and was last reviewed in 1995. The associated agent report details that files from the San Juan Field Office, Federal Bureau of Investigation, Hato Rey, Puerto Rico, were examined concerning Ivan Marti, brother of the subject, and Blanca Marti, sister of the subject. The review of these files found no records.
