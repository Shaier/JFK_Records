This document is a SECRET internal memorandum from the CIA, dated June 25, 1975, concerning a project called ZRRIFLE. The memorandum outlines the project's purpose, objectives, background, and operational assets. It details security protocols, potential problems, and procedures for handling assets and operations. The document also includes budget information and guidelines for selecting personnel and handling potential blackmail situations. There are heavily redacted portions of the documents, but these are the major points.
