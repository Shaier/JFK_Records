This document is a collection of paperwork related to the employment of Kenneth G. Grant by the House Select Committee on Assassinations (HSCA) in 1977. It includes payroll authorization forms, a note about his status, a forwarding address and a memorandum on payroll certification. Additionally there is a Final Determination Notification from the Assassination Records Review Board, dated December 8, 1995, regarding the release of these records. This notification states that the documents were "Postponed in Part" due to the presence of Social Security numbers, and that a review would be conducted in 2017.
