This document is a memorandum from the Chief of the Special Security Division to the Director of Security. It concerns a request for a recommendation regarding granting Operational Clearance for the Subject's use as an Agent in Costa Rica. The memo provides information to aid in determining whether the Subject should be used and notes that no field investigation was scheduled due to the Subject residing outside the US since 1945. The memo requests that the office be informed of the decision and that attached materials be returned in a sealed envelope marked "Eyes Only."
