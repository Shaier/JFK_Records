This document is a Central Intelligence Agency (CIA) information report, dated January 11, 1965, concerning policy guidance directives for Cuban intelligence representatives in the Dominican Republic. It outlines intelligence objectives and priorities in Santo Domingo, focusing on collective action against Cuba, the status of leftist forces, the Dominican Armed Forces, the Dominican Communist Party, training camps for Cuban mercenaries, counterrevolutionary organizations, repressive bodies, relations with the United States and the Church, and various aspects of Dominican government and society. The information is based on an unevaluated source, a former Cuban intelligence officer who served until April 1964.
