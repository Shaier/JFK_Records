The document consists of multiple pages of CIA files related to Nikolai Sergeyevich Leonov. These include personal information such as date and place of birth, physical descriptions, family, languages, and past career. There are also reports on his activities in the Soviet Embassy in Mexico, his connections with Cuban exiles (specifically Fidel Castro), and his involvement in potential Soviet subversion in Latin America. The documents consist of both intelligence reports, communications, and photocopies of documents, and cover the period from the 1950s to 1960s.
