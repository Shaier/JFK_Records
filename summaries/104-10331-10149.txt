This document is a memorandum from a JFK Project Officer to a Chief of IRG/OIM, outlining several issues pending before the Assassination Records Review Board (ARRB). These issues include concerns about covert actions, alias documentation and accommodation addresses, requests for organizational materials, Mexico City tapes, and files related to LITAMIL-9 and Richard Gibson. The document also addresses a major ARRB request for CIA officials and officers files. For each issue, the document explains the background, current status, and proposed resolutions, including the ARRB staff's recommendations and potential concerns.
