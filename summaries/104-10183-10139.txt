This document is a SECRET message from the Director, sent on November 5, 1964, to PARIS, regarding KEYWAY PBRUMEN AMWHIP MHVIPER. The message instructs the recipient to obtain a study and forward it to headquarters by any means, and to advise headquarters on methods before undertaking a clandestine approach if unable to obtain the information openly. The message refers to a two-volume study on the Cuban transport system. The message was coordinated by several officers.
