This document is a memorandum summarizing an interview with John Stein conducted on March 18, 1975, at CIA Headquarters in Langley, Virginia. The interview was conducted by James B. Weidner as part of the Commission on CIA Activities Within the United States. The interview covered Stein's review of alleged domestic activities of the CIA, specifically the counter-intelligence staff, excluding activities related to the CI/SO project and mail intercept project. The discussion included topics such as domestic breaking and entering, physical and electronic surveillance, files on Americans (specifically the "American Targets Program"), defectors, and the CIA's relationship with other government agencies.
