This document is a dispatch from the CIA's Task Force W station, JUWAVE, detailing the debriefing of three recent Cuban legal travelers in Miami. The debriefing focused on their experiences and observations in Cuba, particularly regarding the reception of anti-Castro propaganda and radio broadcasts, opinions on various political and social issues, and knowledge of internal Cuban resistance movements. The subjects provided insights into the effectiveness of different propaganda efforts and the attitudes of the Cuban populace toward the Castro regime and opposition groups.
