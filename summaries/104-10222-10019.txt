Here's a summary of the documents:

This is a collection of documents from the official personnel folder of David S. Morales, primarily consisting of fitness reports and requests for personnel action from the Central Intelligence Agency (CIA). The documents span from 1951 to 1975, and include information on salary, performance evaluations, position titles, organizational affiliations, and comments regarding his strengths, weaknesses, and career potential. A few documents also reflect changes made to the file for review under the President John F. Kennedy Assassination Records Act of 1992.
