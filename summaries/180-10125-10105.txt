This document is a payroll certification report from the U.S. House of Representatives, specifically for the Select Committee on Assassinations. It covers the accounting period from February 1, 1977, to February 28, 1977. The report lists employees, their social security numbers, job titles, annual salaries, gross pay, and the period for which they were paid. It also includes information about the number of authorized employees, salary limits, and a summary of payroll expenditures.
