This document is a series of memoranda regarding the ARRB's meeting on November 17th, detailing decisions on requests regarding JFK assassination records. It includes information on topics such as the release of Warrenton as a CIA Records Center, the CIA's use of state cover, the release date of Nosenko NBR records, Cuban Revolutionary Council financial records, a Ramparts Damage Assessment document, book cable dissemination lists, and a specific document request, as well as other documents. It also outlines the process for reconsideration and appeals.
