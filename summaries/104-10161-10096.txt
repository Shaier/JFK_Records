This document is a message sent to Madrid and Lisbon from the CIA. It concerns Dave Phillips' son, Chris, traveling in Europe and Chris' contact with someone named "Kalbings." The message reports that Dave had instructed Chris to contact Kalbings, who may have been in a position to offer help. Chris neglected to advise Dave of his travel plans. Chris was born on December 28, 1956. The message also states that this may explain a previous incident.
