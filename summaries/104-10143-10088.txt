This document is an annex to a chapter of a CIA handbook detailing the agency's 201 system, a method for identifying persons of interest and managing information about them. It outlines the key features of the system, including 201 numbers, dossiers, master records, and indexes. The annex describes how OI codes are used to categorize individuals and how to open and maintain 201 dossiers, covering topics such as requesting file openings, contents, maintenance procedures, and security restrictions. It also discusses procedures for requesting information on 201 personalities and generating machine listings. It lists categories of people whose 201 dossiers are opened automatically, including diplomats and intelligence officers from certain countries.
