This is a Top Secret - Sensitive memorandum from November 27, 1962, outlining an operational plan for the CIA to continue operations against Cuba. It details assumptions, objectives, and specific actions, including intelligence gathering, potential sabotage, economic warfare, and psychological operations, while acknowledging limitations due to political assurances against invasion. The plan emphasizes covert actions and the exploitation of Cuban exiles, with recommendations for organizational changes and policy approvals. It's signed by William K. Harvey, Chief of Task Force W.
