This document is a deposition of William C. Sturbitts taken by the Rockefeller Commission on April 16, 1975. The deposition covers a range of topics including Anti-Castro activities, assassination attempts on foreign leaders, clandestine operations, CIA activities, Cuban exiles, and individuals like David Ferrie, Frank Sturgis, E. Howard Hunt, Jim Garrison and others. The document is classified as "Secret" and contains 113 pages.
