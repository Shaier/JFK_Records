This document is a dispatch from a CIA Chief of Station concerning the termination of an agent, AMHINT-56. It includes copies of the agent's Form 2457, Service Record, and Damage Report. The termination was due to budgetary considerations leading to a reduction in special operations assets. The dispatch requests that the agent's OA (Operational Approval) and MOC (Memorandum of Conversation) be canceled. Attachments detail agent information, service history, and potential exposure of locations, personnel, and assets.
