This is a memorandum from H. Clark Schor, Chief of the Plans and Review Group at the Central Cover Staff, to SA/DO/O. It responds to a House Select Committee on Assassinations (HSCA) request for information, supplementing a previous memo and providing additional details per paragraph 6 of the referenced memo, as requested in a letter from the Chief Counsel/Director of HSCA. The memo details CCS files containing information on specific individuals and entities cited in the HSCA's request, including George DeMohrenschildt, Leo Cherne, William Harvey, the International Rescue Committee, Alexander Rorke, Richard Helms, James Angleton, Raymond Rocca, Southern Research Corporation, and Wackenhut Corporation. It outlines the number of documents, their date ranges, and the general subject matter they cover, often related to cover establishment, maintenance, use, travel orders, documentation, and related clearances.
