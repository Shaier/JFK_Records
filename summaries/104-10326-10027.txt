This document pertains to the JFK Assassination Records Collection Act of 1992. It is a communication from the CIA Director regarding the ARRB release, specifically requesting station assistance in contacting a former employee who retired under cover and resides in Costa Rica. The document also informs individuals about the potential release of their names in publicly available documents related to the assassination and outlines the grounds for requesting postponement of such disclosures. Furthermore, it addresses common concerns and questions about the implications of having one's name appear in the JFK assassination records and the process for requesting name redaction.
