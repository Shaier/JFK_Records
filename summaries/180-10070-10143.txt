This document contains payroll authorization forms and related documents for Gina I. Resnick, an employee of the House Select Committee on Assassinations (HSCA), from June 1977 to March 1978. The documents include her appointment, salary adjustments, and termination of employment, along with a personal leave record and a memorandum from Tom Lambeth recommending her hire. The document also includes a payroll certification stating that Resnick is not related to any current member of Congress.
