This document is a CIA intelligence report from April 20, 1963, regarding statements made by Ngo Dinh Nhu, a key figure in the South Vietnamese government. The report suggests Nhu discussed a possible withdrawal of American aid to South Vietnam, indicating that the U.S. was using aid as leverage. He expressed a desire for the country to become self-sufficient, even suggesting citizens produce their own weapons. The document also includes the reactions of Vietnamese Air Force officers, who believed that any withdrawal of American support would lead to the collapse of the regime.
