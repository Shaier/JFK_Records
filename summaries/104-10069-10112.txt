This is an Office Memorandum from the CIA dated August 31, 1962, concerning Robert Pratchett, a US citizen with extensive experience in Cuba. Pratchett is in contact with a Cuban counterrevolutionary group called Alpha-66 and is willing to provide information on their activities to the US government. The memo requests a background check on Pratchett and details his personal and professional history. It also mentions Alpha-66's plan to sabotage power plants in Cuba.
