This document is a memorandum from S. J. Papich of the FBI to Mr. L'Allier, dated September 14, 1960. The subject is Petros Moutsos and internal security in Greece and Cuba. The memo discusses Moutsos' past use by the CIA as an informant in a communist-dominated Greek Maritime Organization, and his desire to leave that atmosphere. The CIA advises that Moutsos is currently a crew member of the SS Radnor and that CIA is working out an arrangement whereby he can travel to Cuba. To arrange the travel, Moutos will jump ship in New Orleans. CIA will then hold conferences with Moutos and subject will proceed to Havana under a cover being established for him. Prior to leaving for Havana, Moutos will endeavor to have contact with his wife who has a communist background.
