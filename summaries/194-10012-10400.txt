This document is an identification form related to the JFK assassination system, agency information, and document information. It concerns possible US Army deserters in West Berlin in December 1967 named Peter Simons and Roman Arnold. The document outlines information received from police in Dusseldorf and West Berlin regarding their location and potential assistance from a student organization. It also describes actions to be taken by West Berlin and US military police if the subjects are located, including their apprehension and immediate transfer to Rhein Main.
