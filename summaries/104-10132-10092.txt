This document is a memorandum regarding the Lee Harvey Oswald case, dated January 29, 1964. It details information about Abram Chayes, a Legal Officer of the State Department, who was reportedly involved in Oswald's return to the United States from the Soviet Union. The memorandum also mentions Llewellyn Thompson and Samuel Wise, Counsel of the American Embassy in Moscow, as potentially involved in the handling of Oswald's case. The document further provides details about Chayes' security clearance, a White House namecheck on his wife, and a HUMAN EVENTS report referencing Chayes' role in State Department regulations on passports. Additional information is provided about Samuel Wise, including his previous employment history, application for CIA employment, and association with William Orville Munsell.
