This is a 1977 CIA memorandum about Nestor Antonio Moreno Lopez. It details his background as a Cuban lawyer involved in anti-Batista movements, his association with figures like Tad Szulc and Jorge Volsky, and his involvement in the AMTRUNK operation aimed at overthrowing Castro. The memo highlights Moreno's indiscretions, distrust of the Agency, and his tendency to leak information, ultimately leading to his termination from the operation.
