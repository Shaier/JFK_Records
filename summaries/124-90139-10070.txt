This document is a memorandum from the FBI to the CIA, dated December 11, 1961, regarding Herbert Levy and miscellaneous information concerning Nationalities Intelligence. It includes a copy of a memorandum from Salt Lake City, Utah, dated November 28, 1961, concerning Cuban economic matters and furnished to other interested agencies. The source of the information is Herbert Levy, who was contacted by the CIA. The document also mentions concerns about possible export regulation violations regarding shipments to Cuba by a large firm.
