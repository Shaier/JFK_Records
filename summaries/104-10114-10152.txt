This document is a report dated March 18, 1961, concerning an individual named Court Wood and his activities. The author spent hours with Wood discussing his future plans and his participation in the "FRAY FOR CUBA COMMITTEE." The author believes Wood is naive about their true affiliation and is candid. The author is confident they can gather substantial information about Wood's associates. The report notes Wood and his father have an interest in communist publications, and Wood is described as an extreme leftist and a fanatic believer in Castro's Cuba. Wood is planning to enter Cuba illegally in June and wants to be a tenor for the Castro government. Members of the "26th of July Movement" are involved in this. The author anticipates gathering more information about Wood's plans soon.
