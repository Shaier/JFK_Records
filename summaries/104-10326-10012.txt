This document is a message from the CIA to MINSK regarding the Assassination Records Review Board (ARRB) meetings with the BKGB. The message discusses the agency's policy of facilitating the meetings without participating in substantive discussions. It also provides information and traces on BKGB officials who met with ARRB members in October 1996, including Aleksandr Ablameika, Gennadiy Narkevich, Lev Pimenau, and Aleksandr Veras.
