This document is an FBI report dated May 24, 1966, concerning the Vietnam Day Committee (VDC) and their activities. It details the committee's involvement in distributing a leaflet specifically targeting soldiers, attempts to undermine the morale of US troops, the identities and affiliations of key VDC members, and the distribution of leaflets to military personnel serving overseas. The report also includes information from various sources, including informants and records from educational institutions, police departments, and other organizations, as well as characterizations of various groups mentioned in connection with the VDC.
