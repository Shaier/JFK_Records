This document is a collection of meeting minutes from the Special Group (5412), a committee that oversaw covert operations during the Kennedy administration, from March to September 1963. The meetings covered topics such as reconnaissance flights over Cuba, Indonesia, Laos, and North Vietnam; covert actions against Cuba; operations in British Guiana; Italian elections; and the internal defense of Bolivia and Colombia. The meetings also addressed intelligence requirements, security concerns, and coordination with other government agencies, including the State Department, Defense Department, and the CIA.
