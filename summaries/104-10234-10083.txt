This document is a CIA message from January 15, 1965, classified as "Secret." It reports information acquired from an asset named ARMANDO (MARTINEZ) Y VIRELLESI, detailing potential plans for an uprising against Castro.  The asset is described as being very active and supported by the CIA. The report suggests that Castro's overthrow could happen any time after January 5th, involving many people within the Cuban government and Cuban agents infiltrated by Castro's own people. The information suggests that a high-ranking person in Castro's government might lead the uprising.
