This document is a classified message from the Mexico City office to the Director, concerning potential access assets related to a Soviet individual named Valentin Vasilyevich Bakulin, who is involved in supplying propaganda films. It mentions a source named Seymour D. Trinnich and identifies Arturo Carrillo Esparza as a possible access asset. The document requests traces on Carrillo and his girlfriend, Cynthia Chlebicki.
