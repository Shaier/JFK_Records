This document is an identification form from the JFK Assassination System archive, pertaining to a CIA record about the potential severance of diplomatic relations between Cambodia and South Vietnam. The record originates from the CIA, is dated September 17, 1962, and consists of a single page. The document has been reviewed as recently as September 10, 2000. The document itself is a telegram reporting that the Royal Cambodian Government might be planning to sever relations with South Vietnam, based on information from a senior official. The decision was allegedly to be formalized in a cabinet meeting around September 19-20, 1962. The report was disseminated to multiple US government and military agencies.
