This document is a memorandum from Jack Moriarty, an investigator for the JFK investigation, to Surell Brady, Counsel, JFK. It summarizes Lawrence Huber Florer's U.S. Air Force file. Florer enlisted in the Air Force in 1962 and was honorably discharged in 1968. He was a member of the Texas Air National Guard. The file reveals his personal information, including his date of birth, education, marital status, and physical characteristics. The memorandum notes that his attendance record shows he typically pursued his military career on weekends.
