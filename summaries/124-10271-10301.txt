This is a memorandum from the FBI regarding inquiries made by Elliff. Elliff was interested in the operational status of legal attaches, CIA's technical developments shared with the FBI, and FBI documents related to surreptitiously entering the Chilean Embassy. The FBI is researching the latter and will contact Elliff. The memo also outlines recommendations to examine materials related to the FBI's legal authority and sensitive investigative techniques, and to have Elliff submit specific requests in writing regarding the Chilean Embassy episode.
