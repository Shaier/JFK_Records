This document is a message sent to Priority Vienna from the Director, requesting station assistance regarding a former employee who retired under cover and now resides in Vienna. The request is being made in compliance with the President John F. Kennedy Assassination Records Collection Act of 1992, and specifically asks the station to contact the former employee and deliver certain information. The document also includes a notification to the former employee that their name appears in documents being reviewed for public release and provides contact information for them to express any concerns regarding this release.
