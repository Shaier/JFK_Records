This document is a classified message sent on May 7, 1960, from Havana to the Director, regarding a report about Cuban youth planning sabotage on April 30th. The source is Hans Arturo Bodewig Leuenberger, who gave information to the Havana station. The message references a previous report, HAVA 4414, and details the subject as Cuba setting off bombs by Juventud Accion Cubana Anticomunista.
