This document consists of 17 pages related to the payroll authorizations and related correspondence of S. Jonathan Blackmer, who was an employee of the House Select Committee on Assassinations (HSCA). The records include payroll authorization forms detailing salary adjustments, appointments, position titles, and social security number. There is also a memo to all staff employees regarding the requirement to certify any relationships to current members of congress. A Final Determination Notification from the Assassination Records Review Board (ARRB) states that portions of the document have been postponed due to redaction of Social Security numbers to protect personal privacy.
