This document is a notification of the establishment of a military cover backstop, dated August 7, 1962. It was issued by the Chief Records and Services Division for INCE: TORING, and it applies to a military cover backstop established for the U.S. Army Elemont, Joint Operations Group (8739)32. The document indicates actions taken concerning block records, continuing effectiveness, Form 642 submission, W-2 issuance, and Forms 1322 and 1323 submissions.
