This document is Annex B of Chapter III from CSHB 70-1-1, dated November 15, 1974, regarding the 201 System used by the Operations Directorate to identify and file information on persons of specific interest. It details the system's components, including the 201 Number, Dossier, Master 201 Record, and the Main Index Master 201 Reference. The annex outlines procedures for opening, maintaining, purging, and restricting 201 dossiers, requesting information on 201 personalities, and obtaining machine listings based on various criteria. It also lists categories of individuals for whom 201 dossiers should be automatically opened. The document also includes information about IDN numbers, file number assignment, and the handling of Top Secret documents.
