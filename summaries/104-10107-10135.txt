This document is a memorandum discussing Herbert Itkin and ramifications related to a confrontation with the New York FBI office. It details requests for and review of security files on two individuals, Thomas L. Roberts and Robert Delson, as well as meetings held to discuss Roberts' operational use and his affiliations with the FBI, CIA, and Itkin. The document raises questions about the accuracy of Roberts' account, his potential knowledge of Itkin's CIA connections, and inconsistencies in his explanations. It also discusses a potential prosecution against Roberts and prior projects involving Delson, including Project DTPILLAR and Project WUBRINY. The memorandum includes a chronological rundown of Roberts' affiliation with the FBI, CIA, and Itkin, as well as a statement from the Assistant General Counsel.
