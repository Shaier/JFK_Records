This document is a Central Intelligence Agency dispatch outlining a program for Project JMATE in Cuba. The dispatch discusses the objective to add to the knowledge of objectives, operations, and assets of the Castro services. The dispatch goes on to state the intentions of helping with the deflection of selected personalities in Cuba.

The dispatch then goes on to state the program will supply intelligence regarding the BRIGADE its assault on Cuba, and this information will be used to help the people who may go into double agent operation with the United States.

The dispatch then discusses supporting and directing intelligence and security services and supporting officers and agents to collect information on Cuba.

The dispatch concludes with the fact that they are going to intensify the project's communist party penetration program, increase counter intelligence efforts, and counter espionage operations directed by Castro services within the Western hemisphere.
