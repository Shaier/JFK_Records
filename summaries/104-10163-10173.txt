This document is a collection of records and memoranda pertaining to Manuel Artime Buesa (also known as AMBIDDY-1) and his organization AMWORLD. They detail activities, meetings, plans, financial transactions, and interactions with individuals and government entities. The records span from January 1965 to August 1965 and cover topics ranging from phasing out AMWORLD's paramilitary operations, concerns about Communist subversion, financial improprieties, discussions for future operations, meetings with President Schick, and investigations into various individuals connected to AMWORLD.
