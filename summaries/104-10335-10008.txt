This document contains correspondence and memoranda related to the Assassination Records Review Board's request for additional information and records, specifically regarding CIA and Intelligence Community serial publications, analytical materials, and compliance with the JFK Assassination Records Collection Act. It details the search efforts, identification of relevant documents, review process, and coordination between various agencies and individuals. It includes the designation of Agency Compliance Officials, the description of steps taken to locate assassination records, and the provision of materials to the ARRB. Additionally, it mentions specific requests, such as those related to CIA-9 (Publications and Analytical Materials), and references to the LBJ Library and its relevant documents.
