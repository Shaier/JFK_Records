This document contains a collection of administrative records, memorandums, letters, and personnel action requests related to David E. Murphy. The documents span from 1947 to 1976 and detail various employment actions like assignments, promotions, commendations, retirement, and security clearances within the CIA and the Department of State. There are also references to travel arrangements, salary adjustments, and expressions of appreciation for his service. A memorandum also indicates a compromise of his cover as a CIA employee to the North Vietnamese Intelligence Service.
