This document is a chronological review of Operation EMDEED/ENSLEW, a US government operation to replace the Trujillo regime in the Dominican Republic, focusing on US government involvement with persons plotting the overthrow of Trujillo. It details communications, meetings, plans, and actions from November 1959 through May 1961, including support for dissidents, requests for and approvals of weapons, policy shifts, and ultimately, the assassination of Trujillo. It outlines the U.S. government's internal debate and contradictions regarding providing assistance to those who sought to assassinate Trujillo, and the resulting investigation and exposure of U.S. involvement.
