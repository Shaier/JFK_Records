This document is a dispatch from the Chief of Station in London to the Chief of the Western Europe Division of the CIA, concerning a prepared list of identified and suspected Cuban intelligence officers. The London Station requests consideration of distributing this list to friendly Latin American and Western European liaison services, and asks whether it would be appropriate to share it with ODENVY. They also request a cabled reply regarding this further distribution. The dispatch includes an attachment of three copies of the "Cuban Identified and Suspect Intelligence Officers" list and mentions distributions to different parties. It also references other documents and provides information on specific individuals suspected of intelligence activities, including their roles, descriptions, and connections to Cuban security and diplomatic missions.
