This document is a collection of reports and correspondence between US intelligence agencies regarding Valentin Sergeyevich Loguinov, a Soviet Third Secretary and KGB officer stationed in Mexico City during the mid-1960s. The reports detail his activities, contacts, and suspected intelligence operations, as well as efforts to cultivate him as an asset and track his movements. It also includes information on those associated with him, as well as Loguinov's physical characteristics.
