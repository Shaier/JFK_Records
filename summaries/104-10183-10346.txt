This document is a memorandum regarding a proposed training curriculum for a group of trainees from an unnamed organization. It requests approval for covert training to be given to these trainees, outlining the purpose of the training in relation to improving the efficiency of a relationship with the Venezuelan Armed Forces Intelligence Service (SIFA).

The document includes biographical data on four specific trainees, including their backgrounds, education, language abilities, and previous intelligence training. It details the training requirements, including the location, language, and specific skills to be taught, such as clandestine activities, security, surveillance, agent acquisition, counterintelligence, and intelligence research and analysis.

The memo also requests assistance from various CIA departments and personnel, including Colonel Valeriano for counterinsurgency orientation and TSD/Training for courses in flaps and seals and audio surveillance. It seeks permission for the trainees to enter the R & S building for training purposes.
