This document is Annex B of a CIA handbook, titled "The 201 System." It describes the 201 system, a method for identifying persons of interest to the Operations Directorate, controlling and filing information about them, and generating lists of 201 personalities by category. It outlines the 201 number, dossier, Master 201 record, and Main Index Master 201 reference. It also defines Ol codes, procedures for opening and maintaining 201 dossiers, restricted access, cancellation, and requests for information. Includes examples of records. The document also references automatic dossier openings for certain individuals based on their nationality, affiliation, or role.
