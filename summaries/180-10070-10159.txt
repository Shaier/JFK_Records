This document is a collection of payroll authorization forms and related information concerning Johanna Lynn Smith. These forms document Smith's employment with the House Select Committee on Assassinations from January 1977 to December 1978. There is also a memorandum to all staff employees regarding payroll certification and relationship to current Members of Congress, completed and signed by Smith. It also includes a Final Determination Notification of Assassination Records Review Board that it has postponed some information in this document because of privacy concerns.
