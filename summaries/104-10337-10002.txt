This document is a collection of memos, briefing outlines, and records of conversations related to the CIA's declassification efforts for JFK assassination records, specifically for the House Permanent Select Committee on Intelligence (HPSCI). The discussions concern the CIA's process for reviewing and releasing documents, the challenges posed by the JFK Assassination Records Review Board, the criteria for redaction, and the agency's interactions with Congress and the White House regarding sensitive information. The documents also touch upon the Board's authority, appeal procedures, specific instances of disagreement, and requests from the Board to review additional records.
