This document is a top-secret message from McGeorge Bundy at the White House to Ambassador Lodge, dated October 30, 1963, concerning U.S. policy and actions related to a potential coup in Vietnam. It discusses the U.S.'s position on delaying or discouraging a coup, clarifies instructions for coup-group money, and outlines the U.S.'s posture in the event of a coup, including the rejection of direct intervention.
