This document is a memorandum detailing the CIA's handling of intelligence concerning the Soviet build-up in Cuba from July 1 to October 16, 1962, leading up to the Cuban Missile Crisis. It covers a timeline of events, intelligence gathering efforts, the dissemination of information, and the decision-making processes within the US government during this period. It also covers the limitations of intelligence publications. It further discusses the different points of view from some officials and the progression of the September Overflight Program.
