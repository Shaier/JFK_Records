This document is a memorandum summarizing the results of an ARRB (Assassination Records Review Board) meeting held on November 17, 1997, regarding the release of JFK assassination records. It details decisions made on several specific items, including the release of records related to Warrenton (CIA Records Center), CIA's use of State Cover, the release date of Nosenko NBR records, Cuban Revolutionary Council (CRC) Financial Records, and a Ramparts Damage Assessment Document. The memo also covers book cable dissemination lists and a specific document (#104-10072-10232), and provides details on how the Agency can seek reconsideration or appeal Board decisions.
