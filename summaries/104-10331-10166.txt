This document is a memorandum summarizing the results of an ARRB (Assassination Records Review Board) meeting held on July 20, 1998, concerning CIA documents related to the JFK assassination. It details the ARRB's decisions on several CIA requests regarding the handling of sensitive information in these documents, including requests to protect crypts/project names, retype an Australian document, use specific language for a document description, and deny a JMWAVE org chart. The ARRB accepted some requests but rejected others. The document also mentions an upcoming Board meeting and the possibility of appeals or reconsiderations.
