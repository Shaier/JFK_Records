This document is a U.S. House of Representatives certification report for the Select Committee on Assassinations. It covers the accounting period from February 1st to 28th, 1979. The report lists employees, their job titles, annual salaries, and gross pay earned during the period. It also indicates if any employees were terminated and their termination date. The report includes an office payroll summary detailing the number of employees paid and the total payroll expenditures for the month, along with a certification statement.
