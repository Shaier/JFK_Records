This document is a personnel file on Thomas B. Casasin, reviewed by Betsy Wolf on August 29, 1978. The file contains pertinent information and documents from Casasin's personnel folder for the period 1955-1962, which was kept by the House Select Committee on Assassinations staff members. In mid 1962, Casasin went overseas. The file includes his career preferences, qualifications, employment history with the CIA, and other employment history. The file also includes his personal history statement, information on his family, financial background, residences, education, and club memberships. Various personnel actions such as promotions, reassignments and transfers, and resignations are also documented. Several fitness reports and performance evaluations for Casasin are included as well. A memorandum notes cover arrangements for the subject, as well as records being properly blocked.
