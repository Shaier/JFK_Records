This document is a report on actions taken in July and August 1963 to combat Castro-Communist subversion. It covers enhancement of intelligence on Cuban subversion, strengthening counter-insurgency capabilities, control of travel to and from Cuba, control of transfer of funds, surveillance of Cuban missions, control of movement of guerrillas and arms, and control of movement of Cuban propaganda. The report includes actions by the Central Intelligence Agency, Department of State, Department of Defense, and United States Information Agency. It also includes an intelligence annex with details on travel to and from Cuba, movement of Cuban propaganda, and movement of guerrillas and arms in various Latin American countries.
