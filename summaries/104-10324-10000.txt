This document is an interview with Sam Halpern conducted by Brian Latell and Michael Warner on April 7, 1998. The interview focuses on Halpern's experiences with Operation Mongoose and his thoughts on the Bay of Pigs operation, particularly in relation to an IG report on the latter that Halpern recently read. He discusses the governmental structure of Mongoose, its management, and the lessons learned from the Bay of Pigs. He also touches on personnel issues, coordination between Miami and Headquarters, and the involvement of key figures like Bissell, Helms, and the Kennedy brothers. Halpern offers opinions on various aspects of these operations, including assassination plots, the quality of personnel, and the dissemination of intelligence.
