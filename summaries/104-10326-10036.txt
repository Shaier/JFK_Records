This document is a request from the CIA to its Paris station, seeking assistance in contacting a retired former employee residing in Paris. The request is made in order to comply with the President John F. Kennedy Assassination Records Collection Act of 1992, which mandates the public release of information in these documents. The memo explains the act's requirements and the potential for an individual's name to be included in the public release. It provides guidance on how to respond if the individual wishes to prevent the release of their name, outlining the criteria for postponement of public disclosure. The document also addresses common concerns and questions about the implications of the release.
