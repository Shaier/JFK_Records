This document is a memorandum discussing a letter received by the CIA from Mrs. Lourdes Cuesta, ex-wife of Antonio Cuesta Valle, who is imprisoned in Cuba. Mrs. Cuesta requested verification that her ex-husband was not connected to the CIA or involved in certain raids. The document outlines the Agency's response, including discussions with the DC/LAD and the FBI, and a proposed reply to Mrs. Cuesta. The memorandum also details Antonio Cuesta's past associations with the U.S. government and exile groups, as well as information on Mrs. Cuesta's contacts and activities.
