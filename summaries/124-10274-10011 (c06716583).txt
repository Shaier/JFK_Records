This document is a response from the FBI to a request from the United States Senate Select Committee (SSC) regarding intelligence activities. It pertains to further documents related to the FBI and the Department of Justice, specifically regarding the organization, structure, and jurisdiction of legal attaches. The document references a previous letter from the SSC and provides enclosed copies of 23 documents in response to the request. A note explains the scope of the request and the information included in the enclosures.
