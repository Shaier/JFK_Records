This document is a Top Secret Daily Summary, dated December 6, 1963. It covers military, international relations, internal situation, and economic development aspects of Cuba, with a section on external subversive activities. It mentions Cuban military training in chemical warfare defense, the OAS meeting on Cuban arms shipments, apparitions reported in Oriente Province, reactions to Kennedy's assassination, and dissatisfaction within the Cuban government with agricultural policy. The summary also details Chinese Communist relations with Cuba and bloc economic relations. Finally, it delves into Cuba's support for external subversive activities in various Latin American countries.
