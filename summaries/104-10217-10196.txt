This document is a classified message regarding Richard Gibson. It references a magazine article accusing him of misusing his former position with the "Fair Play for Cuba Committee". The message also mentions a previous consideration of approaching Gibson, during which an inquiry was made about whether he was an agent for VIRK JERVAY, and the response was negative. The document also includes a reference to a file containing information on Gibson, including his position as editor-in-chief of the English language edition of "Revolution Dispatch".
