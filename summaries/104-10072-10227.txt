This document is a dispatch from a CIA operative to several addresses, primarily concerning the transmittal of contact reports detailing the first two meetings between Peter J. DI GERVENO and an agent codenamed "AMCLOCK/1" in New York in September 1967. The reports cover operational details, discussions about the agent's situation in PBRUMEN, his contacts, and other intelligence matters. The dispatch also outlines the distribution of the contact reports and identity sheets.
