This document is a collection of dispatches, memoranda, and reports related to the LIRING-3 operation, a CIA project in Mexico and Guatemala. The documents detail communications, contacts, and activities of LIRING-3 and related individuals, focusing on their interactions with FAR (Fuerzas Armadas Rebeldes) contacts, involvement in subversive activities, assessment of their reliability, and efforts to recruit or debrief them. There are also details about finances, security concerns, and the operational objectives in relation to the Cold War. The reports highlight the challenges and complexities of managing agents and monitoring their connections with potentially hostile groups.
