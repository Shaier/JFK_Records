This document is a 165-page compilation of memoranda, affidavits, letters, testimonies, and reports related to the alleged assassination plots against Patrice Lumumba and Fidel Castro during the Eisenhower and Kennedy administrations. The documents include details of CIA involvement, meetings discussing assassination possibilities, the use of biological agents, and the potential involvement of high-ranking officials. The documents also cover other covert operations against Cuba. There are mentions of Special Group meetings, NSC meetings, and testimonies from various individuals such as C. Douglas Dillon, Robert Johnson, James Lay, and alleged CIA operatives.
