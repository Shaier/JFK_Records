This document is a CIA information telegram regarding discussions between President Diem of South Vietnam and Vice President Chen Cheng of Nationalist China in March 1963. The discussions, which lasted over twenty hours, were private and possibly covered topics such as increased military support for South Vietnam, using South Vietnam as a base for military operations against mainland China, ceasing the drafting of overseas Chinese in Vietnam, publicly supporting a counterattack on mainland China, granting airfield and coastal bases for military operations, joint operations into North Vietnam and Laos, Thailand's neutrality movement, and efforts to unseat Cambodia's leader. The visit was considered successful in strengthening ties between the two countries.
