This document contains payroll authorization forms for Lance W. Svendsen, an employee of the House Select Committee on Assassinations (HSCA). The forms detail his various positions, salary adjustments, and start dates within the committee from August 1, 1977, to December 1, 1978. The documents also include a payroll certification indicating that he is not related to any member of Congress. Some information has been redacted from the documents, specifically Social Security numbers. The records were released under the John F. Kennedy Assassination Records Collection Act of 1992, with a review date set for 2025.
