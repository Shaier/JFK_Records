This document is a classified fax transmission from Michelle Combs to Barry Harrelson, dated March 31, 1997, regarding briefing questions for the ARRB on DS&T and DO matters. It includes a list of follow-up questions related to CIA records, specifically concerning operations in Mexico City and connections to journalists and publishing companies. The questions delve into matters like destroyed records, archival searches, crypt analysis, personnel lists, technical operations, funding processes, and specific projects like QKENCHANT and WUBONBON. The document also shows some review and comments on earlier drafts of the briefing questions.
