This document is an information report from the CIA dated August 1, 1962, regarding views on a possible coup d'etat in South Vietnam. It details warnings of a potential coup attempt against President Diem on the night of August 1-2, 1962, originating from various sources including an American observer, Lieutenant Colonel Nguyen Van Chau, and Major Long. The report notes discrepancies and uncertainties regarding the information, including the involvement of senior ARVN officers and possible VC activity. Ultimately, the coup did not occur as described, and the report is disseminated for potential future developments.
