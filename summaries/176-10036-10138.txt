This document is a CIA report, dated October 9, 1963, concerning potential plans by the South Vietnamese government to stage a demonstration in front of the American embassy in Saigon. The report suggests that government agents would infiltrate the demonstration, attack the embassy, assassinate Ambassador Henry Cabot Lodge and other high-ranking officials, and then set the embassy chancery on fire. The report also mentions plans to assassinate Thich Tri Quang. The source of this information is described as a medium-level government official with extensive contacts among government leaders. The report expresses some skepticism about the reliability of the information.
