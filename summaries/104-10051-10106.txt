This document is a memorandum from M. D. Stevens concerning the Lee Harvey Oswald case. It discusses Abram Chayes, Legal Officer of the State Department, and his involvement in Oswald's return to the United States from the Soviet Union. It details Chayes' security approvals and liaison contacts. It also mentions Samuel WISE, Counsel in the American Embassy in Moscow, and his potential connection to Oswald's case, as well as his history as an applicant for CIA employment.
