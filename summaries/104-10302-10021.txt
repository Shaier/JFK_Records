This document is a Top Secret Daily Summary from the CIA dated November 25, 1963, concerning Cuban reactions to the assassination of President Kennedy. It summarizes Fidel Castro's speech on the matter, noting his apprehension about a tougher US policy toward Cuba. It also mentions Cuba's defensive military alert and other reactions from Cuban officials, including statements from Carlos Lechuga and Che Guevara. Furthermore, it touches on Cuban support for external subversive activities, specifically the planned Venezuelan press conference tying an arms cache to Cuba.
