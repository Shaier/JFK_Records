This document is a report on the status of implementing actions designed to counter Cuban subversion. It details actions taken by various US government agencies, including the Department of State, Central Intelligence Agency, and Department of Defense, during September 1963 in areas such as controlling travel to and from Cuba, movement of propaganda, clandestine movement of guerrillas and arms, transfer of funds, strengthening counter-insurgency capabilities, and exchanging intelligence on Cuban subversion. The report also includes actions taken by the United States Information Agency.
