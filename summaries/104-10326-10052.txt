This document is a secret staff communication from Paris, dated March 12, 1996. It relays information that Ambassador Harriman received a postcard through the APO from an individual in Minnesota. The postcard's message, stamped on the back, alleges that the CIA assassinated President Kennedy on orders from the military-industrial complex headquartered in New York City, naming David Rockefeller as the "Chairman" of this group. The postcard is unsigned but has a printed address label affixed to the back.
