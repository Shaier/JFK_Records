This document is a JFK Assassination System Identification Form containing agency information and document information related to Robert Scheer. It includes details about his associations with various organizations such as the Vietnam Day Committee, Communist Party, Socialist Workers Party, Progressive Labor Party, and the Berkeley Chapter of the W.E.B. DuBois Clubs of America. It recommends that Scheer be rejected for service due to credible derogatory information. The document also details Scheer's refusal to complete security questionnaires. It is a disposition form with a request to return the file through the Director of Security, OACSI, to the Chief, USAPSG, Fort Holabird, Maryland 21219, upon completion of action.
