This document is an identification form for a record related to the JFK assassination. The record itself is a memorandum from Arthur Schlesinger Jr. to the President, dated June 10, 1961, with the title "CIA Reorganization". It consists of 15 pages and discusses topics such as covert action, paramilitary warfare, Cuba, and policy control. The document type is a paper and textual document classified as "S" with restrictions and a current status of "P". The last review date was September 30, 1993. It is part of Box 244, Folder 7, and was Exhibit 5 to R. Goodwin's testimony on July 18, 1975. Some items are restricted.
