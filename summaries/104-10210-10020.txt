This document, marked Top Secret, is part of the John F. Kennedy Assassination Records Act of 1992 release. It concerns a study entitled "The Examination of the Bona Fides of a KGB Defector - Yuriy I. NOSENKO," dated February 1968. Certain portions have been deleted due to ongoing CIA operations and related security. The document lists specific page numbers included in an attachment. It also contains a warning about sensitive intelligence sources and methods involved. The pages include contents related to the seventh department of the KGB, KGB operations, investigations, arrest, information, intelligence sources and assessments of KGB activities among American tourists. The summary included in this document lists reasons and evidence in support of and against the authenticity of Yuri Nosenko's statements and position within the KGB.
