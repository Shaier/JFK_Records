This document is a journal from the Office of Legislative Counsel, dated March 9, 1978. It details liaison activities with various congressional committees, including the House Select Committee on Assassinations, the House Permanent Select Committee on Intelligence, the House International Relations Subcommittee, and the Senate Select Committee on Intelligence. The entries cover topics such as reviewing files, discussing hearings on FBI/CIA dealings, setting back hearing dates, discussing acceptable paragraphs for public release, scheduling committee meetings, and discussing requests for information.
