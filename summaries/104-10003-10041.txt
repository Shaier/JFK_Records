This is a routing and record sheet regarding a clipping of an article titled "The Kennedy Murder and the Secret Services of the USA". The document indicates it was sent from WE/4/INT Arthur P. Iorio on March 26, 1964, to CI Staff Birch O'Neil, in Room 2603. The attached article appeared in the March 7 issue of the Italian Communist Party weekly Rinascita and was written by Gianfranco Corsini. The document notes that rumors mentioned in red pencil suggest the Agency organized the murder of President Kennedy. It is marked as SECRET.
