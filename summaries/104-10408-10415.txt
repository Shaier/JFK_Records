This document is a memorandum to the Director of Central Intelligence from the Deputy Director for Plans concerning a letter from Sir Charles Spry (Director General of the Australian Security Intelligence Organization, ASIO). The letter recommends against the declassification of Warren Commission document CD-271.
The memo writer agrees with Sir Charles' points and recommends against declassification, with concurrence from the C Staff. The document also lists several attachments including letters to and from Sir Charles Spry, a FAMA cable regarding declassification, a copy of CD-271, and a request for release of Warren Commission documents.
