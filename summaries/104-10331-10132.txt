This document is a memo detailing a meeting between CIA and ARRB (Assassination Records Review Board) staff regarding the CIA's compliance with the ARRB's request for JFK assassination-related documents. The ARRB wants to assure the public that all possible attempts have been made to locate relevant files and that no secret files remain. The memo outlines specific record categories, offices, and files the ARRB is interested in, as well as questions and concerns raised during the meeting. The CIA explains the challenges in guaranteeing all records have been located due to the lack of a central filing system.
