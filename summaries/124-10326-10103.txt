This document is a memorandum from the FBI to W.C. Sullivan regarding Bureau Informant 100, which is a source of information attributed to James Angleton of the CIA. This information comes from a Liaison arrangement the CIA has with the Israeli Intelligence Service, and other sources under Angleton's control. The document suggests reiterating instructions regarding this source to the field offices, as the informant has been developing information on US citizens traveling to Russia via Israel. It includes a recommendation for a SAC letter, and mentions that the CIA has no objection to declassification and/or release of CIA information.
