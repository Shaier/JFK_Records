This document is a memorandum for the record detailing a meeting on October 6, 1961, between the author and Dr. Miro Cardona. The meeting covered topics such as propaganda efforts, underground activities, commando plans, requests for arms to the Escambray group, information on arms received by the Castro regime, and a proposal for the Revolutionary Council to establish a government on Cuban soil. It also discusses members of the Revolutionary Council, possible Argentine rupture of relations with Cuba, and MIRO's views on various individuals and groups. It further mentions telephone conversation between the author and Miro on October 7.
