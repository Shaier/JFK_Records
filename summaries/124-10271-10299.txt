This is a collection of memoranda and documents related to the USIB Ad Hoc Coordinating Group on Congressional Review, focusing on the release of information to Congressional Select Committees investigating intelligence activities (specifically related to the JFK assassination, among other things). The documents detail meetings, task assignments for preparing papers, discussions about the scope of information requests from Congress, and concerns about handling sensitive intelligence information. Key topics include assassinations, CIA and FBI cooperation (or lack thereof) on "notional" organizations, security regulations, and liaison with various government agencies.
