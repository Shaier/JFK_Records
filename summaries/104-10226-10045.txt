This document is a memorandum for the record detailing a briefing between MI-6 and CIA representatives regarding the case of Robert Morten Geddes, a British subject arrested in Cuba. It outlines Geddes' background, his alleged involvement with a Cuban resistance group, and potential suspicions of him being a CIA agent or a provocateur. The memo includes a chronology of Geddes' activities, questions from MI-6 regarding the CIA's involvement, and the CIA's responses. The document reveals concerns about the validity of Geddes' claims, the security of CIA contacts, and the potential for a Cuban operation against the British Embassy.
