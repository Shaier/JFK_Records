This document is a memorandum from the Central Cover Staff to the House Select Committee on Assassinations (HSCA) providing additional information related to a previous request. It details the contents of CCS files regarding individuals and entities mentioned in the HSCA's request, including George DeMohrenschildt, Leo Cherne, William Harvey, the International Rescue Committee, Richard Helms, James Angleton, Raymond Rocca, and the Wackenhut Corporation. The memo specifies the number of documents, the time periods they cover, and the general subject matter of the information contained within those files.
