This is a CIA dispatch from August 16, 1965, regarding the identification of Manuel ESTEVEZ Perez.  A request was made to AMMUG/1 for any information he might have, but he was unable to identify the name. It is suggested that if a photograph of the subject is available, it should be sent to WAVE to show AMMUG/1 for possible identification.  The distribution list includes WH/C, COSIMADR, COL Barcelona, and C/WE.
