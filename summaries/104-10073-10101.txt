This document is a debriefing report on AMFAUNA-14, a person involved in intelligence activities in Cuba, following her detention by the DSE (Cuban State Security) in November/December 1963. The report details her interactions with DSE officers, their interrogation techniques, and the information they sought. It also outlines her cooperation with the DSE, the messages she transmitted under their control, and her efforts to protect other individuals involved in clandestine activities. Additionally, the report covers her exfiltration from Cuba and provides assessments of other individuals mentioned in the debriefing, including their potential connections to intelligence networks and their reliability.
