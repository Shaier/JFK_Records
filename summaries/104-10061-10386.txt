This document is a memorandum from the Chairman of the Information Review Committee to Mr. H. G. Bean, Assistant for Information, regarding a FOIA appeal by John W. Donovan. It summarizes recommendations related to the release of Agency documents, including sanitizing certain portions, referring a Department of Army name check form to the Army for action, downgrading the classification of some documents, and explaining the difficulty in providing legible copies of some microfilm records. Mr. Strickland of OGC has concurred in the recommendations. Attachments include references, full text and sanitized versions of documents, and the Army document for referral.
