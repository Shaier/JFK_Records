This document is a 142-page report from the Montgomery County Department of Police concerning Eugene Michael Carrafa, dated August 3, 1976, with continuing investigations reports up to March 31, 1977. It details an investigation into Carrafa's activities, associates, possible murder, and connections to organized crime. The report includes information from various sources, including interviews, toll records, NYPD, FBI, other law enforcement agencies, and confidential informants. It covers subjects such as stolen vehicles, gambling, prostitution, and potential links to the Kennedy assassination.
