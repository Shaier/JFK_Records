This document is a memorandum regarding Samuel Adason Jaffe, focusing on his alleged recruitment by the KGB in Moscow, his relationship with KGB officers, and his connections to intelligence agencies. It details conflicting information about his involvement with the KGB and the FBI's assessment of his activities. The memo also discusses his concerns about his FBI association affecting his employment and his interactions with British Intelligence and the CIA in Hong Kong. Finally, it recommends the memorandum be referred to the SENSTUDY project.
