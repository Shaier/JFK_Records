This document is a communication from the FBI regarding Cuban exile leader Orlando Bosch Avila. It contains information from both the CIA and the Centro Nacional de Informaciones (CNI), formerly known as DINA. The document discusses Bosch's contacts in Chile, including individuals referred to in a previous cable. It mentions Carlos Ashton Ugalde and Manuel Fuentes Wedling and their connections to various organizations. The document also mentions Jaime Rojas Brugues, a cryptology instructor and security chief, and Jorge Davanzo Cintolesi, the commander of the Chilean naval training ship "Esmeralda". Finally, it notes that Bosch re-entered Chile with a false Chilean passport under the identity of Hector Davanzo Cintolesi. The Bureau requests Miami, Newark and New York be advised that Bosch is armed and dangerous.
