This is a classified intelligence document from March 1978 concerning potential damage to U.S. security interests and the CIA following an NBC program containing revelations about operations against a Soviet target in Mexico City. It discusses the exposure of sources, the impact on future recruitments, the revelation of operational methods, damage to liaison with the Mexican government, potential damage to existing sources, embarrassment to the President of Mexico, and station security. The document also requests more specific information about the locations mentioned in the program to assess the damage.
