This document is a notification of establishment of a military cover backstop. It indicates that the backstop is being established for the Chief, Records and Services Division, as well as the Chief, Operating Component. The backstop is continuing and effective. It includes instructions to submit Form 642 to change the limitation category and Form 1322 for any change affecting the cover. The document also mentions ascertaining that Army W-2 is being issued. There is a section for remarks and a distribution list.
