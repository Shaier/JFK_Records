This document contains payroll authorization forms and related memoranda for Brenda L. Welburn, an employee of the HSCA (House Select Committee on Assassinations) from December 1977 to December 1978. The forms detail her position as a Researcher, her annual salary, and changes in her employment status. The documents also include a certification of her relationship to current members of Congress. Some information, specifically Social Security numbers, has been redacted for privacy reasons.
