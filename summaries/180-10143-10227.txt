This document is a summary of an interview with Thomas Casasin, a retired CIA officer, conducted on August 17, 1978, in Paris, France. The interview focuses on Casasin's knowledge and involvement with Lee Harvey Oswald and related CIA activities. Casasin discusses his career, including his roles in Paris and Japan, his work in Soviet operations, and the functions of various CIA sections. He addresses his 1963 memo regarding Oswald, his observations about Oswald's defection and return, and his assessment of Oswald's potential connections to the KGB. The interview also delves into CIA procedures, cryptonyms, and the agency's handling of defectors.
