This document outlines a request for an ordinary Nicaraguan passport in the name of Juan Hidalgo ELIECIDO Romano for use by ACCID-117 to travel between the US and Argentina. The request includes specific details such as a forged US tourist visa, a false entry date into the US, and a fabricated permanent address in Managua.
