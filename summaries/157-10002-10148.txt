This document is a transcript of the testimony of James P. O'Connell before the SSCIA on May 30, 1975. The document includes information on Robert Maheu, Castro, the assassination, Cuba, Hughes, Bissell, Verona, Varona, William Harvey, Rosselli, Giancana, the Mafia, Trafficante, the CIA, and Dan Rowan and Phyllis McGuire.
