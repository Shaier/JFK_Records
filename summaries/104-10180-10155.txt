This document is a memorandum from the Chief of the Finance Division regarding Emilio Americo Rodriguez. It states that Rodriguez has been recruited by the Habana Station at a rate of $300.00 per month, effective June 1, 1960. The memorandum requests that the Finance Division deposit Rodriguez's monthly salary into a specific account at the Whitney National Bank of New Orleans, account number 44-335-104. The first deposit, for the month of June, should be made on June 30, 1960, and monthly thereafter until further notified. The memo also specifies that no taxes are to be withheld from Rodriguez's salary. It also contains routing and distribution information.
