This document is a collection of payroll authorization forms and a related memorandum from the U.S. House of Representatives concerning Clifford A. Fenton, Jr.'s employment with the Select Committee on Assassinations. It includes information about his salary, position, and other employment details from 1976 to 1978. The documents also contain a memorandum regarding payroll certification requirements, specifically related to employee relationships with current members of Congress. The document is a Final Determination Notification from the Assassination Records Review Board and concerns STAFF PAYROLL RECORDS. The document has been postponed in part because it contains Social Security numbers, which are redacted to protect personal privacy. The next review date is scheduled for 2017.
