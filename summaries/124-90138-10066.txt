This document is an FBI memorandum dated December 2, 1959, regarding Paul Hickman and information concerning the Communist Party-Cuba. It relays information from a CIA liaison agent about Hickman's alleged involvement in business dealings to sell arms to the Castro government, his contact with a Soviet Embassy representative regarding a potential sugar deal, and the CIA's decision not to cover his activities without a specific request from the Bureau. The memo is directed to the Nationalities Intelligence Section.
