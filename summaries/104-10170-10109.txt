This is a collection of memos and letters related to Freedom of Information Act (FOIA) requests to the CIA and other agencies. The requests are from individuals and groups seeking information about themselves, their organizations, or particular topics of interest. The documents include internal reviews of the requests, recommendations for release or denial of information, justifications for withholding information, and communication with other agencies like the FBI and the Department of State regarding related documents. Some documents also detail modifications of reports for partial release by redaction.
