This document is a collection of records related to the payroll and employment of G. Robert Blakey at the House Select Committee on Assassinations. It includes forms authorizing payroll actions, a memo on payroll certification requirements, and administrative information for the documents. The records show adjustments to Blakey's salary and the committee's need to certify any relations employees have to a current member of Congress. Some Social Security numbers have been redacted due to privacy concerns.
