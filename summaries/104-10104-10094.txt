This document is a memorandum estimating the potential damage to the CIA in Miami that could result from the prosecution of the Rolando Masferrer Haitian Invasion group. It outlines several key areas of concern, including negative publicity arising from student and foundation topics, the potential exposure of CIA activities and personnel through the trial, and the possibility of disgruntled former agents or Cuban exiles using the situation to harm the CIA's reputation and influence US policy. The document expresses apprehension about maintaining the cooperation of local institutions like the University of Miami and emphasizes the delicate balance between protecting CIA equities and preventing unwanted attention to its operations.
