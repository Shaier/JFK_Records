This document is a classified message dated December 10, 1962, from JMMAVE to the Director. It requests PRITY POAS (priority personalities) information on several Cuban individuals: Edda (Hernandez) Gonzalez, Benicia (Gonzalez) Gomez, and Adelaida (Martinez) Andreades. It includes personal information such as birthdates, locations, and family details. It also mentions that WAVE and ODENVY traces are negative for some of the individuals. The message indicates that POA was formerly GRAS ED PER REF B.
