This is a memorandum from the Chief of Alien Affairs Staff dated April 5, 1968, regarding Miguel Angel DIAZ Isalgué. It mentions Robert Fambrini's report that DIAZ Isalgué, a paroled Cuban, is scheduled for temporary duty travel to Argentina and will be traveling under an alias. A request is being submitted to TSD for an Argentine passport with a B-2 visa for DIAZ Isalgué's travel in South America. An I-512 Reentry Document will also be requested to permit his reentry as a parolee.
