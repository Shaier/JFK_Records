This document is a request for station assistance in contacting a former employee residing in Hong Kong, in order to comply with the President John F. Kennedy Assassination Records Collection Act of 1992. It discusses the potential public release of the former employee's name and provides information about the review process, the grounds for postponement of public disclosure, and answers to frequently asked questions. It also includes instructions for communicating with headquarters, if desired, and mentions the agency's efforts to protect cover status.
