This document is a collection of scanned pages pertaining to an FBI file, number 62-116395, related to Senate Select Committee (SSC) requests for information regarding intelligence activities, particularly concerning Executive Order 10450, and investigations of various individuals and organizations.  The documents span from the 1940s to the 1970s and include memoranda, letters, government orders, organizational charts, and interview summaries. It includes information related to FBI procedures, investigative policies, security concerns, and relationships with other government agencies and Congress.
