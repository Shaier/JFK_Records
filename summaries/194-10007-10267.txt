This document is a JFK Assassination System Identification Form, containing information on Henry S. Beer, Henry Beer, Joachim F. Beer, and Joachim F. Pehl. The document is a cable, dated March 10, 1971, and originated from the USA. It was released with deletions. It concerns an investigation and was regraded unclassified on October 18, 1994. The document also discusses stepsons, birthdates, SSAN numbers, and military service.
