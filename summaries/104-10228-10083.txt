This classified message details an invasion of Cuba by a strike force of Cuban revolutionaries at Cochinos Bay, Las Villas Province. Initial beachheads were secured, and air superiority was established. However, one beachhead was lost, and the remaining foothold was compromised due to resupply issues, tank assaults, and enemy manpower. The main rebel effort occurred between April 16-20. Data on casualties is unavailable. Police actions in Cuba prevented civilian support for the invasion. The message requests urgent input. Further psychological guidance will follow.
