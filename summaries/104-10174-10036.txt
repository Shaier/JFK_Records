This document is a memorandum for the record of a meeting with Henry P. Lopes at the Carleton Hotel on January 15, 1960, regarding a PP/PA subject. The memo details the meeting's discussion, including a staff report on opposition groups and an Indian political action operation, Lopes' briefing on the situation in Cuba, instructions and a check given to Lopes, his familiarity with the Cuban situation, security precautions, and arrangements for further briefings and recording of his remarks.
