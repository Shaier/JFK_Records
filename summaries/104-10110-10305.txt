This document is a memorandum for the record, dated May 31, 1972. It details Elsie L. SCALETTI's recollections about Phillip Agee's involvement with Cuban operations at the Mexico City Station. The memo covers Agee's discussions regarding the Olympics, his lack of assistance to the Cuban Section, his possible knowledge of station operations, social encounters, and his future plans. It also touches upon an encounter with John A. PROBERT and the circumstances surrounding Agee's resignation. Additionally, it notes Agee's awareness of SCALETTI's tourist cover in Mexico.
