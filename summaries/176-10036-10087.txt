This document is an intelligence report from the CIA, dated August 23, 1962, concerning Vietnamese Communist (VC) activities in the Saigon area. The report details information regarding the infiltration of unarmed VC members into Saigon, the movement of armed platoons to the city for special operations, and the setting up of bases among laborers. It also mentions the presence of young VC guerrillas in Saigon for sabotage and kidnapping purposes. Furthermore, it includes an unconfirmed report about the appointment of a commander for the South Vietnam Liberation Army to launch a large-scale attack. The report is classified as confidential with "No Foreign Dissem."
