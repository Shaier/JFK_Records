This document is a classified message from the CIA, dated April 22, 1964, originating from W. Wainwright, Unit WH/SA/EOB, to Rome, from the Director. It concerns an individual named AMWHIP/1, who is arriving in Europe on April 26 for a three-week business trip, including errands in Paris and Geneva. He will arrive in Rome around May 5 and contact the station via the usual channel to ascertain any services Rome may wish. The message is marked "Secret."
