This document is a report from the CIA on Charles William Wiley, dated April 1, 1974, with revisions up to 1993. It details his recruitment, activities (including involvement with the World Youth Festival, trips to Cuba, and work with Cuban exiles), relationships with individuals like James McCord and Lee Pennington, utilization, and eventual termination. The report draws on Office of Security files to paint a picture of Wiley's work as a lecturer, photographer, journalist, and covert operative.
