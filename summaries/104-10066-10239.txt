This is a secret document dated May 191115Z 78, deferred teletype from the Brussels station, addressed to Washington, concerning the "Vlaamse Militantenorde." It references a note received from "Nigale" and mentions the lack of known VMO-AMCIT contact between 1968-70. The message states that it's inopportune to prepare a report on the VMO for the House of Representatives Committee and that they are opposed to passing articles from their quarterly or semi-annual reports to the committee. The file number is 008-002-009/3.
