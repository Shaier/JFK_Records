This document is a classified message from Mexico City, dated October 16, 1963. It advises that cartridges were delivered to Corpus Christi on October 1. The message also mentions a box of fifty pounds of cargo, identified as 64-M51104 DIF. The message ends with a request for advice taken on Regn S59-5-64. The message is signed by William E. Brooks, Chief, WH Support, and Charles F. Reimer, Acting Chief, WH Logistics.
