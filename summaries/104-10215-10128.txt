This document contains various intelligence reports from the Central Intelligence Agency (CIA) concerning Cuba in the late summer and early fall of 1960. The reports cover a range of topics related to the oil industry in Cuba, including: the shortage of lubricating oil, the operations of refineries, the effect of using Soviet crude oil, the attitudes of employees, and the desire of technicians to flee the country. Additionally, a personnel security file pertaining to Winfred C. Barnes is included.
