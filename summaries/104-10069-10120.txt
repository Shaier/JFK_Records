This document is a memorandum from the Chief of the Minneapolis Office to the Chief of the Contact Division, ATTN Support Branch (Crowley), dated August 31, 1962. The subject is a Cuban Refugee Group. The memorandum discusses three names provided by George Hauer of Cleveland, Ohio, who are identified as the leaders of a Cuban group involved in activities such as establishing reception groups in Cuba, transporting medical supplies, ammunition, weapons, and bringing Cubans out of the country. The memo suggests that the group is seeking help in the form of supplies, but the Agency would require some control in return. The memo also requests that any on-the-street investigation be handled with great care. It asks for an expedited response regarding interest in the matter. An enclosure is included, and the document is marked as SECRET and SENSITIVE.
