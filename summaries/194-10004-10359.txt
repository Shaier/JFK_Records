This document is a sworn statement taken on March 26, 1969, from Manuel Marti, a member of the US Army Intelligence Corps stationed in An Khe, Republic of Vietnam. He was questioned about his views on communism, his associations, and his attitude toward military service and US policy in Vietnam. Marti states he thinks the communists are trying to take over the world and he does not go for it. He affirms his support for the United States Constitution and allegiance to the United States.
