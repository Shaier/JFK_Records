This document is a JFK Assassination System Identification Form related to DOD-affiliated personnel incident investigations. It concerns an investigation request for David R. Fromer's induction, originating from the USA and sent from Myron K. Hayashida to the USA Intelligence Command on November 25, 1966. The document has been released with deletions and was last reviewed on March 7, 1995. It involves a paper document that has been reclassified as unclassified, and was initiated by the USAINTC, and includes reports from US Army and the USACRF.
