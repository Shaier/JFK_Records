This document is a memorandum for the record dated March 4, 1969. It discusses a 1955 memorandum by Mr. George Carey regarding individuals referred to as T. and H. It identifies T. as Juan Trippe and H. as a General Hardin. The memo also mentions that General Thomas O. Hardin, an Air Force Major General, is likely the person being referenced. It discusses Mr. Carey's interest in placing Hardin with Pan Am for operational reasons, and clarifies that Hardin is not synonymous with Philip Harbin.
