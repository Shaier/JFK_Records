This document is a CIA intelligence report, classified as SECRET, concerning observations of truck traffic and tube-like objects entering the Torrens base in Cuba between October and November 1963. The source is a former Cuban government employee. The report details nightly truck and trailer truck activity covered with canvas traveling toward Torrens, as well as instances where large trailer trucks entered Torrens. One report mentions tube-like objects protruding from the bed of one of the trailers. The report also notes that the Torrens surface-to-air missile assembly area was active during this period, according to a reliable source.
