This document is a Central Intelligence Agency information report regarding conditions in El Salvador, as reported by a special representative of the Cuban National Revolutionary Council. The report was written on June 28, 1962, and consists of one page. The information was gathered from a Cuban national exiled in the US who recently returned from a trip to Central American countries, and the report was developed by an Army representative assigned to the office of preparation. Attached is a copy of a paper in Spanish regarding general conditions in El Salvador and specifically dealing with Cubans in that country. The author of the report was sent to El Salvador for a specific purpose and spent 45 days in El Salvador and other Central American countries. The report is classified as CONFIDENTIAL.
