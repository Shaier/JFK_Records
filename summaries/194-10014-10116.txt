This document is an information report from the Central Intelligence Agency (CIA) concerning Clemard Joseph-Charles' release from prison in Haiti in exchange for a US $605,000 ransom. It details the conditions of his release, his financial resources, and his agreement to use the ransom to pay off legitimate claims of depositors in his Banque Commerciale d'Haiti. The report also mentions President Duvalier's interest in reorganizing the bank and his desire to improve his image and attract foreign investment. Additionally, it touches on a possible reorganization of the Haitian government. The document is classified as confidential and is marked for CIA internal use only.
