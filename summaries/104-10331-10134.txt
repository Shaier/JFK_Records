This is a memorandum regarding the agenda for the ARRB (Assassination Records Review Board) meeting on January 22, 1998. It covers several items including the reconsideration of the "State Cover," Office of Personnel Files in the Sequestered Collection (Microfilm), Non-related files/documents in the Sequestered Collection (Microfilm), and an ARRB request concerning DO (Directorate of Operations) documents. It also mentions a Crypt LINLUCK issue and a 1967 IG report on "Castro Assassination Plots."
