This document is an identification form regarding the JFK assassination system. It pertains to records from the Department of Defense concerning affiliated personnel and incident investigations. The specific file number is AC640653W, pages 7-9. The document itself is a message from USA to DCSI, USAREUR regarding Hans D. Kaminski and possible CI interest. The document has been released with deletions and was re-graded unclassified on September 2, 1994. The message details an incident where an unidentified person, known as "John the Dee Jay," attempted to elicit official information from members of a US Artillery Detachment in Germany, and is extremely knowledgeable about the military. He had also mentioned SSG (USAF) FNU Alexander.
