This document is a report from the FBI in Newark, New Jersey, dated August 10, 1960, regarding Lazaro Eddy Espinosa Bonet, a Cuban national involved in espionage activities. The report details contact between Espinosa and a confidential source (NK T-1), a Cuban refugee, in New York City. Espinosa sought information from the source about CIA activities, individuals connected to the CIA in Miami, and infiltration tactics into Cuba. The report also mentions concerns about the source's loyalty, potential connections to Cuban intelligence, and the identification of specific individuals and operations.
