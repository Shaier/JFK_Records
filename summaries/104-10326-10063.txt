This document is a heavily redacted CIA message relaying an agent's vehement objections to the potential release of his name under the JFK Assassination Records Collection Act of 1992. The agent, based in Vienna, cites personal security concerns, potential legal repercussions under Austrian law, disruption of his and his wife's established life, and disillusionment with the US government's actions. He emphasizes his past service and claims he is unwilling to accept the associated risks. The message details the agent's background, the reasons for his concerns, and his intended course of action, including legal representation. Additionally, it relays that the agent attempted to disguise the message in a code before becoming too tired to complete it.
