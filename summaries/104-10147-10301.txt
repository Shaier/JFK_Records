This document is a secret cable from The Hague, dated November 29, 1977. It discusses Willem Oltmans' meetings with Cuban and Soviet diplomats, his planned travel to Cuba, and his past travel to the USSR. It mentions Oltmans' contact with Cuban and Soviet diplomats and his association with George de Mohrenschild, who was of interest to Senate and House intelligence committees. It suggests disseminating information to the House Select Committee on Assassinations.
