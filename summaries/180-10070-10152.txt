This document is a collection of forms and memoranda related to the employment and termination of Jan Richard Schlichtmann with the U.S. House of Representatives, specifically within the Assassinations subcommittee. It includes a personal leave record, payroll authorization forms, a memorandum confirming the lack of familial relationship with current members of congress, and a memo indicating the acceptance of the position and initial salary. The record also includes a final determination notification from the Assassination Records Review Board indicating that some information has been postponed due to privacy concerns.
