This document is a CIA dispatch from April 16, 1962, concerning operational matters in Santiago de Cuba. It transmits CIA reports numbered 134 through 149, and 156, and includes an index. The dispatch involves the Chief of Task Force W and the Chief of Station, JM/WAVE. Attachments include the CIA reports and the index in duplicate. The document contains information related to a source who was interviewed and the DRE in Santiago de Cuba.
