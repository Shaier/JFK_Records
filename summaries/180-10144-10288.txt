This document is a letter from the House Select Committee on Assassinations (HSCA) to Patrick Carpentier of the Central Intelligence Agency (CIA). It requests access to all CIA files and references pertaining to a list of 59 named individuals, along with their approximate birthdates, last known addresses, and last known employment information. Most of these individuals have connections to Dallas, Texas, particularly the Dallas Police Department, and some were employed at the Texas School Book Depository.
