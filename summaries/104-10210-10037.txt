This document is the first volume of a study on the bona fides of Yuri Ivanovich Nosenko, a Soviet defector. It covers his case from 1962 to 1967. It includes a summary of operational developments, circumstances, and the motivations behind Nosenko's contact with American intelligence and eventual defection. The study details his life before joining the KGB, his career within the organization, evaluations by specialists, and analysis of his production as an intelligence source. Certain Soviet personnel such as Anatoliy Mikhailovich Golitsyn and Aleksandr Nikolayevich Cherepanov are referenced throughout the document.
