This document is a secret message from the CIA Director to the Immediate Madrid Station, dated January 8, 1980. It discusses a Cuban asset named AMLASH/1 who arrived in Madrid and requested a meeting with a CIA representative. The document also mentions that AMLASH/1 has been contacted by numerous media representatives but has refused to grant interviews. The message requests the name and telephone number that IDEN B can use to contact the station upon arrival in Madrid.
