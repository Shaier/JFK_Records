This document is a memo from the CIA to the FBI, concerning Richard and Susan Frank. It discusses information received from a Swiss source about the Franks, including their travel to Switzerland and contact with the Chinese Communist Embassy. The memo also mentions plans for the Franks to travel to Communist China and the possibility of them flying via Prague. The CIA requests permission to share a summary of the information with their Swiss source, without revealing that the FBI supplied the information. The FBI Director sends this information to various legal attaches with instructions on dealing with the Franks.
