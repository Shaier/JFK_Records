This document is a classified message from San Juan to the Director, dated November 8, 1962. It discusses interactions between IDEN A, KARNLEY, and ARTECONA regarding assistance in obtaining miniature cameras and establishing communication channels with IDEN B. The message also mentions DRE/KUBARK contacts and a suggestion to present a request for assistance to a KUBARK contact during a visit to Miami. The message suggests handling the matter to avoid crossing a specific channel and advises on the situation. It concludes with information about DRE, a Cuban exile student group with ties to KUBARK.
