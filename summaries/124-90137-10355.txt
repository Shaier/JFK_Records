This document is a memo from the FBI Director, John Edgar Hoover, to the Director of the Central Intelligence Agency (CIA). The memo concerns Eduardo Espinosa Y Prieto and CIA liaison material. The subject of Espinosa writing bank drafts to active communist workers is discussed, with the FBI stating that the CIA's summary of information about Espinosa should not be included in the summary sent to a German intelligence agency because the FBI does not have evidence to support that claim. The memo is classified as Secret.
