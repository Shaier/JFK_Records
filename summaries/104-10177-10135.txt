This document is a collection of records related to a project called PBGROVEL, also known as FURIOSO-C, a covert operation run by the CIA in Chile from 1952 to 1954. The project's aim was to establish an anti-communist group and influence local media. The records include project outlines, financial data, memoranda, and correspondence between CIA personnel and individuals involved in the operation. The documents detail the recruitment and payment of agents, funding of a Chilean media outlet, and efforts to control the project and maintain security.
