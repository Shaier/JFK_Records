This document is an identification form related to the JFK assassination system. It concerns agency information and a sworn statement (affidavit) from October 3, 1982. The subjects mentioned are Vladimir Slovoda, Joseph Timothy White, and a restricted individual. The document is unclassified, and has been released with deletions after a review on March 20, 1995. The sworn statement authorizes the release of Agent Report, DA Form 341, dated 22 September 1982, concerning Joseph Timothy White. Henry D. Glaister subscribed to the affidavit.
