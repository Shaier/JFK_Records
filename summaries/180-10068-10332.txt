This document is a collection of personnel records related to Clarence J. Day's employment with the House Select Committee on Assassinations (HSCA) in 1977 and 1978. It includes payroll authorization forms, leave records, memoranda regarding his hiring and salary, and a payroll certification form. Some information, such as Social Security numbers, has been redacted due to privacy concerns. The redaction decision was reviewed on 10/24/1995 and set for next review in 2017.
