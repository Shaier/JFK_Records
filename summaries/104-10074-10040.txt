This is a CIA memorandum from Frederick C. Randall to the Chief of Information and Privacy Staff, dated August 5, 1976, regarding Edwin Anderson Walker. The subject of the memo is a request (P-76-1140) and states that the CIA does not maintain a file on the subject. A search revealed one FBI report mentioning the subject, originating in Boston, Massachusetts on December 6, 1972, pertaining to the Boston Scientists and Engineers for Social and Political Action (SESPA). The bureau file is 100-459865 and the field file is BS-100-42304.
