This document is a memorandum from the Director of Security to the External Activities Branch regarding a manuscript by David Phillips. It details concerns from various Agency components about the manuscript's content, focusing on potential breaches of security, revelation of sensitive information, and negative implications for the Agency's reputation. The memo outlines specific deletions requested, reasons for withholding certain information from Mr. Phillips, and advice from the Office of General Counsel on how to approach the author with these proposals.
