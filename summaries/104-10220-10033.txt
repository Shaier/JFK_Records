This is a partially redacted document classified as SECRET, concerning an individual named Nagell. Nagell appeared at the Zurich consulate on the morning of March 7, and threatened to expose the U.S. government on radio and television if his demands weren't met. He disappeared afterward. Zurich consulate advised on March 10th that Nagell made similar threats from US consulate in Barcelona. The file number is 201-746537. The document is dated March 17, 1969.
