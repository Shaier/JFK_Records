This document is a memorandum from the Director of Central Intelligence regarding an audio operation (STESCALADE) against the New China News Agency (NCNA) in Havana. It details the installation of microphones and tape recorders in apartments adjacent to and above the NCNA office, the involvement of Embassy personnel, and the subsequent arrest of TSD technicians involved in the operation by Cuban authorities. The memorandum also outlines efforts to manage the situation, including providing cover stories for the technicians and their families.
