This document is a memo from the FBI to the CIA regarding the shipment of ten safes from a US company to the Soviet Embassy in Havana, Cuba. The memo provides information about the safes, including model numbers, serial numbers, combinations, and dial key numbers. It also mentions that the safes are equipped with key-locking dials and have two inside compartments. The memo includes a set of dial keys and a set of keys for the inside compartments for the CIA's use. The memo also requests that the information be handled on a need-to-know basis and that no further dissemination be made without prior clearance from the Bureau.
