This document is a dispatch from the Chief of Station in Ciudad Trujillo, dated June 12, 1958, to the Chief of the Western Hemisphere Division (WHD). It concerns the business interests of William D. Pawley in the Dominican Republic. The dispatch mentions Pawley's investments in mineral exploitation, negotiations for the purchase of the Hotel Hamaca, and a concession for the procurement and sale of Dominican postage stamps managed by his brother, Edward F. Pawley. The information about the stamp business was provided by Nora Britzius, an American citizen employed by Pawley.
