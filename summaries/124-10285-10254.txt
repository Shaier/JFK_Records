This FBI document, dated March 23, 1967, concerns an FBI operation involving a Cuban alien code-named "FISHFACE" who is acting as a double agent. The document details that the alien is providing anti-Castro information to his Cuban principal at the behest of the FBI. The document requests authorization for the alien to continue passing information to his Cuban principal. It also discusses a meeting of anti-Castro leaders in Miami, where the possibility of a committee traveling to Cuba to discuss political prisoners and relations with the United States was discussed.
