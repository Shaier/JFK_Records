This document is a memorandum for the record, detailing a meeting between the Director of Central Intelligence (DCI) and Chairman Boland of the House Permanent Select Committee on Intelligence (HPSCI) on December 5, 1978. The meeting covered several topics, including the Kampiles Case, a personal matter, the Iranian situation, Charter questions, the Authorization Bill, and involvement with SAVAK. The DCI also addressed a question regarding information received from Guyana about Jonestown.
