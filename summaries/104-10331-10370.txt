This document is a collection of memos and related documents concerning the inadvertent destruction of records related to the JFK assassination. It details how the Office of the Inspector General (OIG) discovered the destruction, the steps taken to reconstruct the file, and the legal considerations regarding the destruction in light of the JFK Assassination Records Collection Act of 1992. The documents also include the initial request for authorization to destroy the records, shelflists, and handwritten notes about the destruction.
