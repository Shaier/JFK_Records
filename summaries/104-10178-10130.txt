This document is a CIA message from April 19, 1968, concerning James Earl Ray, the accused assassin in a high-priority case. It provides details about his identification, physical description, background, and criminal history. The message states that Ray has been positively identified, describes his appearance, and notes his past military service, mental health history, and employment. The document also mentions his criminal record, escape from prison, and that he is considered armed and dangerous. It concludes by stating that photographs of Ray are being sent for liaison purposes.
