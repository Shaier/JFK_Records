This document is a memorandum for the record summarizing a meeting with AMBANG-1 in Miami on July 27, 1963. The discussion covers various topics including AMBANG-1's request for high explosives to sabotage a Havana power plant, the possibility of contact in Guantanamo, AMBANG-1's political plans, the isolation of Cuba, his phasing out of employment, a JURE radio link, aerial maps of Cuba, JURE organization and strength, questions about ARTIME, a list of Cuban diplomats, his relation with Jorge Volsky, underwater craft, simple scorch system training, writing an article for Cuadernos, and communication between AMBANG-1 and his case officer. The document also includes general observations about AMBANG-1's plans to return to Cuba, his assessment of Castro's support, and his views on the JURE's role in Cuba's future.
