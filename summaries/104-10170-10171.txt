This document is a memorandum from the CIA regarding William Wayne Dalzell. It details requests for information and traces of Dalzell, who was in Addis Ababa in 1956 and who has a connection to the Garrison investigation of the John F. Kennedy assassination. The Addis Ababa Station reports that their files contain no traces of Dalzell. Desk traces also reveal no additional information beyond what is contained in the attached 201 file.
