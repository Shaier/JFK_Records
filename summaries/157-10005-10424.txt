This document is an identification form for a sanitized CIA document related to the JFK assassination. It's a letter with attachment from Walter Elder of the CIA to William G. Miller, Staff Director of the Select Committee To Study Governmental Operations With Respect To Intelligence Activities, dated September 9, 1975. It concerns information on DD/O liaison contacts in Miami, related to JMWAVE contacts with state, county, and local police organizations and individuals between 1960-1973. The document includes details of cooperation with various law enforcement agencies including state level organizations like the Department of Public Safety and the Florida Highway Patrol, as well as county level organizations like the Dade, Broward, Collier, and Monroe County Sheriff's Offices and the City of Miami Police Department.
