This document is a collection of sanitized notes from the Office of Legislative Counsel at the Central Intelligence Agency, forwarded to Mr. Robert Morrison of the House Select Committee on Assassinations in June 1978. The notes cover various subjects, including Leslie Wiselman, William Alexander Morgan, Beth Lichtenfels, Bob Genxman, Daniel A. Parsons, Norman Greenspun, Gordon McLendon, Jim Wolf, a Deryabin file, and David W. Ferrie. There is also a note indicating that the copy is "NOT FOR RELEASE." The documents include correspondence, memoranda, reports, and personal details related to individuals of interest.
