This document is an Information Report Re: regarding an offer of supplies and men to the Governor of Rio Grande do Sul by the governments of Communist China and Cuba. The information is dated September 3, 1961, and originated from the CIA. It mentions Chairman Mao Tse Tung and Premier Fidel Castro of Cuba, who offered material support to Leonel Brizola during the week of August 27, 1961. Brizola reportedly did not accept the offer, fearing international implications and potential US intervention. The source is identified as a Brazilian professor with contacts among communist student leaders. The document is classified as SECRET, and includes dissemination controls and restrictions.
