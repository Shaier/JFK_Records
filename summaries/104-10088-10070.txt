This document is a revised Clandestine Services Instruction regarding covert approvals and related matters within the CIA, outlining responsibilities of headquarters components, investigation protocols, and approval processes for various categories including operations, security, liaison with US officials, and dealing with foreign services. It details procedures for handling investigations, operational approvals, security clearances, and actions involving sensitive individuals, ensuring sensitive data remains within relevant departments. It covers policies regarding the use of various categories of personnel, including those associated with foreign governments, US officials, and the academic community. The overall aim is to regulate sensitive operational data and maintain control over covert activities.
