This document is a memorandum for the file regarding Boris Tarasoff. It mentions that about two weeks prior, Mr. Sounitza called stating they needed an interpreter for a Soviet Delegation and that Subject had been selected. Mr. Kuhn had recently approved the Subject. Mr. Sounitza also said that a new exchange program is under negotiation. The Subject will be under State Department cover as a contractor for the language school. No security objection was interposed to Subject's use. The memorandum is dated February 13, 1962, and signed by R. C. Svendiman.
