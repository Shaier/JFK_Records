This document is a secret message from the Madrid station to the Director and the Lisbon station, dated January 26, 1976. It reports on a phone call received by an individual named Kalbing from a young man identifying himself as Chris Phillips, son of Dave Phillips. The caller requested tourist information for Madrid and inquired about the political situation in Portugal. The Madrid station finds the call suspicious for various reasons, including Kalbing not knowing Chris Phillips personally. It asks for confirmation that Chris Phillips is traveling in Iberia and that Dave Phillips gave him names of people to contact. It also indicates that Lisbon may receive calls from Phillips.
