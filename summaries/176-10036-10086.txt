This document is an identification form for a record related to the JFK assassination. It originated from the CIA and pertains to "Views... Possible Coup D'Etat" with a date of June 15, 1962. The record consists of two pages and includes comments about National Security Files, Vietnam, and Box 2.

The record itself is a telegram from the CIA regarding the views of Colonel Ton That Xung on the possibility of a coup d'etat in South Vietnam. The telegram reports that Xung stated that the people of South Vietnam were tired of the current situation and were going to take action, but would not inform the Americans due to their opposition to unlawful acts. The information is considered unevaluated and the appraisal of the content is tentative.
