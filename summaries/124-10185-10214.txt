This document consists of several memoranda and letters related to Senate Select Committee on Intelligence (SSCI) oversight of US counterintelligence and security programs. The SSCI requests briefings and testimony from the FBI, CIA, Department of State, and Department of Defense, focusing on current and emerging threats, resource allocation, and the impact of political changes in the Soviet Union and Eastern Europe. Specific topics include unauthorized technology transfers, counterintelligence efforts in West Berlin, and coordination between intelligence agencies.
