This document is a memorandum regarding possible retaliatory actions by the Cuban government. It outlines existing contingency plans for various scenarios, including actions against U.S. targets in Cuba, U.S. aircraft and shipping, and Cuban exiles. It also discusses actions against targets in the U.S. and the potential for increased support of subversive forces in Latin America by the Cuban government. The document includes comments on the level of intensity of these actions and the appropriate U.S. response.
