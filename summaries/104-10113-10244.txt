This document is a request from an agency to the Commissioner of Immigration and Naturalization for assistance in facilitating the travel of a contract employee. The employee is scheduled to travel to Argentina and other South American countries and will be using a Republic of Nicaragua passport and a specific Arrival/Departure Record Card under a different name for operational reasons. The agency requests the issuance of a Reentry Document under the alternate name to allow the employee's return to the United States. The agency promises to provide travel information in advance and notify the Service before the employee's return. The document is marked "SECRET" and includes a warning against further dissemination without permission from the Central Intelligence Agency.
