This document is a JFK Assassination System Identification Form containing agency information and document information. It pertains to the investigation of DOD-affiliated personnel and incidents. The document is a message from USA to SSO Meade, dated September 7, 1982, and focuses on Vladimir Slovoda and Joseph T. White, specifically their military personnel files. The document details the findings of an investigation into Joseph Timothy White's ROTC file at Kemper Military College, including his enrollment, attendance at basic camp, an incident with military police, enlistment in the Army National Guard, academic record, and discharge from the ROTC program. The document also outlines attempts to contact individuals associated with White, including Professors, former instructors and a retired general.
