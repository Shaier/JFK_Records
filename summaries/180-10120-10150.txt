This document is a summary of an interview conducted on February 24, 1978, by the HSCA with Sgt. Lewis Wesley Leber regarding Captain William C. Stanley. It includes Leber's identifying information, physical description, personal history, and additional personal information. Leber recalled Captain Stanley and mentioned he had worked under Major Rudolph Gobel. The interview was conducted with DOD approval and the presence of military observers. Leber stated that Stanley was an ex-enlisted man and may have been "r.i.f.'ed" from the service. It was believed Stanley studied law at the University of Miami in 1971. Two servicemen, Larry F. Caison and Charles Fischer, were identified as having served under Stanley. Addresses for Gobel and Caison were provided. The whereabouts of Stanley and Fischer were unknown at the time.
