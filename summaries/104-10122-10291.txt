This document is a memorandum from the Deputy Director of Security to the Director of Security, providing information about Lyon L. Tyler, also known as "Slug." It details Tyler's background as a former FBI agent, his work on Capitol Hill for Senator Lyndon B. Johnson, his involvement in private law practice with connections to Edward P. Morgan (another former FBI agent), and his association with the Agency. The memo also touches on Tyler's income sources, his personal life, and Morgan's political activities and financial dealings.
