This document is an interview schedule and a summary of an interview with Dennis Flynn conducted on June 16, 1978, by Robert Genzman and Harold Leap, pertaining to the JFK assassination investigation. Flynn, a former FBI employee and Director of Security at the State Department, discussed procedures for State Department cover for CIA employees, mentioning that a record of CIA certification would likely be in the individual's State Department security file. He also stated that the State Department didn't keep a listing of CIA employees and that Security Officer and the head of the Bureau of Intelligence Research knew of the arrangements. He claims that during 1959 to 1963, he recalls that he remembers officers who had State Department cover in Moscow during this period.
