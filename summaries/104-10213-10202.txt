This document is an internal CIA handbook chapter outlining the 201 system for identifying, tracking, and filing information on individuals of interest to the Operations Directorate. It details procedures for opening, maintaining, purging, restricting, and cancelling 201 dossiers, as well as requesting information and generating machine listings of 201 personalities. It also defines the types of individuals who automatically qualify for a 201 file and explains the criteria and process for designating subjects for automatic inclusion in the listing system. It also details procedures to be taken when information is converted from one format to another. The document also states guidelines on identifying people of interest for coverage.
