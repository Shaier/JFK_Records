This document is a Top Secret study examining the bona fides of KGB defector Yuriy I. Nosenko, dated February 1968. It compiles statements from Soviet officials and various intelligence sources regarding Nosenko, his background, his defection, and its impact on the KGB. It includes information on Nosenko's positions within the KGB, his knowledge of KGB operations and personnel, Soviet reactions to his defection, and varying assessments of his value to American intelligence. The document contains numerous redactions to protect sensitive intelligence sources and methods. It details events spanning from Nosenko's defection in February 1964 to January 1967.
