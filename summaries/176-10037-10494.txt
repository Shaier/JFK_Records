This document is an information report from the CIA, dated February 20, 1962, regarding the strategic hamlets program in South Vietnam. The report contains information from Ngo Dinh Nhu, brother and political advisor of President Ngo Dinh Diem, and Minister of Interior Bui Van Luong. Nhu discusses the program's goals of defense, development, and democratization, as well as its potential to modernize Vietnamese life and address discontent within the government. Luong states that Nhu had given instructions that the strategic hamlets' program be conducted under the full control of the new interministerial commission and that the errors made in the initiation of the agroville program not be repeated.
