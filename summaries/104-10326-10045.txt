This document is a secret message from Tokyo to the Director regarding a news story about Lee Harvey Oswald and the CIA in Japan. It requests the name and phone number of the project office responsible for declassification of Kennedy assassination papers. A UPI reporter is about to file a story based on local research and an interview claiming Oswald was recruited by the CIA while in Japan. The embassy wants to provide the reporter with contact information for the historical office responsible for dealing with public queries to potentially manage the story.
