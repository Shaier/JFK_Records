This FBI document, dated July 10, 1967, concerns JORGE RAUL ROBLEJO LORIE and his activities related to Internal Security - Cuba. It details information provided by SERGIO OSWALDO BETANCOURT COLLAZO, including ROBLEJO's alleged interest in obtaining information about Cuban refugees processed at the Miami Airport and his possible connection with seeking the release of Cuban political prisoners. The document also mentions a trip taken by BETANCOURT and ROBLEJO to Puerto Rico and potential meetings with other individuals.
