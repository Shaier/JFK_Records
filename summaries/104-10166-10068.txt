This document is a confidential dispatch from the Chief of Station, JMWAVE, regarding the deployment sketch of battalion defensive positions. It states that on August 16, 1963, AMCORE-2 delivered a document obtained through the mails from Agent 1995 (Modesto VENTOSO Mendes) to their Case Officer. The letter containing the sketch was postmarked April 15, 1963. The sketch depicts a defensive position for a battalion-sized force, with three companies on the line and the fourth in reserve.
