This document is an agent report dated November 18, 1968, concerning Manuel Marti, a former US Army private, providing background information gathered from a co-worker. The report summarizes an interview with Anthony Gennarelli, who worked with Marti at the Mutual Insurance Advisory Association (MIAA). Gennarelli describes Marti as dependable, of excellent moral character, and believing in the principles of a free democratic society. Gennarelli recommends Marti for a position of trust and responsibility with the US government. The report also details Marti's employment history at MIAA, his family background, and lack of known organizational affiliations or foreign ties. The report is prepared by Alfonso E. Marshall of the 108th MI Group (I-SIB).
