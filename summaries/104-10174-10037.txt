This document is a memorandum for the record, dated February 1960, summarizing a meeting with Henry P. Lopez. The meeting, held at the Statler-Hilton Hotel, included discussions on Lopez's contacts in Cuba and specific recommendations on influencing Castro. These included utilizing Herbert Matthews and Edward R. Murrow, exploiting defections among Castro's officials, and contacting Padre Alberto de Castro. The possibility of a split between Fidel and Raul Castro was also mentioned.
