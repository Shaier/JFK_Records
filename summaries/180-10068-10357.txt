This document is a collection of payroll authorization forms and related memos for Jane E. Godfrey, who worked for the House Select Committee on Assassinations (HSCA). The documents span from January 3, 1977 to December 30, 1978, and detail changes in her position title and salary. It includes forms for salary adjustments, appointments, and payroll certifications. The documents also include signatures from authorizing officials like Louis Stokes and Henry B. Gonzalez. A notification states that some Social Security numbers have been redacted due to privacy concerns.
