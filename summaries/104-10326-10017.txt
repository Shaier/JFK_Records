This document is a secret communication from the Buenos Aires CIA station to headquarters, arguing against the release of information confirming the use of state cover for CIA operatives in Argentina. The document outlines potential damage to unilateral officers, relationships with liaison services, US-Argentine relations, and intelligence gathering efforts. It emphasizes the open environment of the embassy, the importance of current liaison relationships, and security threats from terrorist groups. The station requests that these arguments be used against the publication of the records in question.
