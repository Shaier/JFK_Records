This document is a collection of records related to the employment of Caryl M. Emanuel by the Select Committee on Assassinations of the U.S. House of Representatives. It includes payroll authorization forms, memoranda discussing her hiring and salary, and a payroll certification form. Some information, specifically Social Security numbers, has been redacted due to privacy concerns. The documents span from May 1977 to December 1995, with a note for a future review in 2017.
