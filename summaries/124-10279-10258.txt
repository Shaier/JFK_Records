This document is a monthly intelligence summary from the FBI Legat in Mexico, dated February 10, 1960. It includes information on a variety of subjects, including:

*   President Lopez Mateos' South American trip
*   Statements by David Alfaro Siqueiros critical of the Mexican President
*   The Caribbean political situation (specifically Cuba, Nicaragua, and Haiti)
*   The Communist Party of Mexico (PCM)
*   The Popular Party (PP)
*   Cuban exile activities
*   Soviet intelligence activities, focusing on a new Soviet principal in Mexico, Valentin Vasilevich Bakulin.
