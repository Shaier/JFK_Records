This document is a memorandum from the Plans and Review Group of the Central Cover Staff, responding to a request from the House Select Committee on Assassinations (HSCA) for information on 19 individuals. The CCS files contain information on five of those individuals: Mrs. G. Stanley Brown, David E. Murphy, Grayson (Grayston) Lynch, Robert Moore, and Bill Logay. The information pertains primarily to the establishment, maintenance, and use of cover for these individuals during their Agency careers. CCS files contain no information on the remaining individuals listed in Reference request. The memorandum provides details about each of the five individuals and the number of documents available on them. The memorandum also includes information about Robert T. Moore, Jr., not to be confused with the requested Robert Butler Moore, and his Agency employee status.
