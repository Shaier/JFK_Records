This is a memorandum from the CIA, dated January 14, 1975, regarding a name trace on Henry L. Bretton and Praeger Publishing Corporation. It details the results of checks through various CIA files (IP, OSG/OC, FR, DCD, ITG, CI, and CCS) and provides information uncovered in the IP and CCS files. It mentions Subject was a former OSS man who had used the name Henry BISMARK. It confirms a phone conversation.
