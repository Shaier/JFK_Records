This document contains letters, memos, and other communications related to the JFK Assassination Records Review Board's (ARRB) review of CIA and FBI documents, focusing on foreign government liaison issues and concerns about the release of sensitive information. The documents address topics such as the release of information obtained from foreign governments through liaison channels, protecting liaison relationships, and coordinating with foreign governments regarding the declassification of their information. The documents also discuss specific concerns related to releasing information that could compromise intelligence operations or threaten relationships with foreign services, particularly with Australia and Mexico.
