This document is a transcript of a security-classified testimony given by Bernard Hugh Tovar on June 29, 1978. Tovar was the Chief of Counterintelligence at the time and is being deposed by the House Select Committee on Assassinations (HSCA) regarding Lee Harvey Oswald and CIA policies regarding defectors. The testimony discusses the CIA's handling of defectors, internal procedures, the roles of different departments within the CIA, and specific incidents related to individuals who defected to the USSR.
