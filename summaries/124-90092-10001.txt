This document is an FBI record from 1965 related to the JFK assassination investigation. It contains information about Robert Glenn Thompson, aka Samuel Katzman, James Olson, Bob Robertson, and "Tommy", and William K. Harvey. The document includes details about Thompson's possible aliases, his knowledge of a "Captain Harvey", and his communication with Soviets regarding him. It also mentions Thompson's story in the "Saturday Evening Post" and clarifies some details related to it. The document is classified as Secret and includes references to previous reports and administrative details.
