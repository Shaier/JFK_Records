This document is a collection of declassified U.S. government records, predominantly from the CIA, concerning the agency's operational files on Ross Lester Crozier and his association with Arthur G. Vaivada, covering the period from 1951 to 1960. These records include memoranda, clearance requests, personal record questionnaires, and routing sheets, indicating actions taken, security considerations, and the roles of these individuals in various CIA projects, particularly AMPHODARCH, HUMANOID, and ESCROW, involving Cuban and Latin American operations.
