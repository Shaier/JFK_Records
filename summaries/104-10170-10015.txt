This document is a dispatch from the Chief of Station, JMWAVE to the Chief of Task Force W, concerning AMSPELL underground activities in Cuba in November 1962. It includes a copy and translation of a letter from the National Coordination Committee of AMSPELL, detailing their activities and financial needs, and a report of underground financing for October 1962. The dispatch also discusses the infiltration of AMHINT-58 and the arrest of AMHINT-140, as well as the need for money to support AMSPELL's activities.
