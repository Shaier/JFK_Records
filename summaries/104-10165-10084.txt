This document is a collection of intelligence reports and related processing sheets concerning Rogelio Cisneros, a Cuban national, and various Honduran political figures and events, including government officials, possible coups, economic conditions, and relations with the US and other countries. The reports, dating from 1967 to 1975, are based on information from a US citizen who has worked and lived in Honduras. The reports provide analysis and opinions on the political and economic situation in Honduras, as well as information on the activities and ambitions of key individuals.
