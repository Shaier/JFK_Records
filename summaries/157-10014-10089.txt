This document contains a transcript of a hearing held before the Senate Select Committee to Study Governmental Operations with Respect to Intelligence Activities on October 9, 1975. The hearing features the testimony of Bronson Tweedy regarding the CIA's involvement in the assassination of Patrice Lumumba. The document includes various cables and discussions related to the planning and execution of the operation, as well as Tweedy's recollections and interpretations of the events. The document is marked as "TOP SECRET" and includes various restrictions.
