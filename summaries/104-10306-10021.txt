This document contains minutes from various Special Group meetings from January to June 1964, along with some related memoranda. The meetings covered a range of topics including covert operations against Cuba (infiltration/exfiltration, sabotage, intelligence gathering), reconnaissance activities (DOD and NRO forecasts), and political action in North Vietnam, Kenya, Zanzibar, and Chile. The group discussed the status of covert action against Cuba, the need for higher-level policy decisions, and potential problems with exile groups. They also reviewed programs for Japan, France, Tibet, Taiwan, and China, and considered the feasibility of propaganda leaflet balloon launches against Cuba. Lastly, they discussed a report on Cuban operational climate and the current operations, along with the matter of recruiting Chinese personnel for maritime operations against 34A.
