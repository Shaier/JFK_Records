This document is a confidential intelligence report from the CIA regarding the arrest of a federal deputy, Cesar del Angel Fuentes, in Mexico on August 28, 1967. It concerns his alleged agitational activities related to a conflict between rival factions of the copra growers' union in Acapulco, which resulted in numerous deaths and injuries. The report also mentions his association with a leftist Mexican playwright, Elena Garro de Paz, and notes that he is being interrogated about his role in the "Acapulco Murders."
