This document is a memorandum from the CIA regarding a media inquiry into a Cuban exile journalist in Miami about Cuban involvement in the Watergate case and attitudes in the Miami area. It details a phone call from a journalist in Washington to a Cuban exile journalist in Miami, inquiring about Cuban involvement in the Watergate scandal and the activities of several Cuban exiles in Miami. It further elaborates on the CIA's knowledge of the individuals mentioned and their connections, as well as their relationship to the proprietary businesses. It expresses security concerns about a possible link to NorthWest Management, and suggests potential connections between various parties involved. The document ends with a note of an impending trip by the Chief of the proprietary to Nassau and then Miami. Attachments include biographical summaries of the individuals mentioned in the memorandum.
