This document is a memorandum for the record from the CIA regarding a "Defector Watch List" for the Secret Service during the Brezhnev visit in June 1973. It details a meeting to decide which defectors with legal responsibilities should be monitored due to their potential for being unpredictable, unstable, and/or violent. The memo lists specific defectors, their locations, known issues, and whether their names should be passed to the Secret Service for monitoring. It also includes input from the Psychiatric Staff, SB, and CDC. There's discussion on specific cases, like disagreement on adding GOLITZYN and recommendations regarding NOSENKO. It further mentions actions taken by individuals and involvement with various organizations.
