This is a classified message from Mexico City, dated October 16, 1963. It references previous messages (MEXI 6547 and MIDW 3207) and states that cartridges were delivered to Corpus Christi on October 1st. One box, fifty pounds of cargo, with the code 64-M51104 DIF, was involved. The message ends with a request for Headquarters advice taken on Reqn 559-5-64. It's coordinated through the OL/TD/CB department and signed off by William E. Brooks and Charles F. Reimer. Reproduction by other than the issuing office is prohibited.
