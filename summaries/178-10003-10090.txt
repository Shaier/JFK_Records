This document is a record of the minutes from a meeting of the Special Group (Augmented) on Operation MONGOOSE, held on September 6, 1962. The meeting included discussions on clandestine operations against Cuba, including sabotage, propaganda, and potential actions involving Cuban exiles and other Hemisphere governments. Key topics included the effectiveness and risks of certain activities, coordination with U.S. objectives, legal considerations, and potential counter-measures by Cuba. The group also discussed broader contingencies beyond the MONGOOSE program, like responses to a potential revolt in Cuba.
