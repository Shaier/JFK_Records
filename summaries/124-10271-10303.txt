This document is an FBI memorandum, dated June 20, 1975, regarding a Senate Select Committee inquiry. It summarizes a CIA "Secret" document from May 1973 containing information about questionable CIA activities, some involving the FBI. The memo details a briefing by a Major from the Intelligence Community Staff about the CIA document and its relevance to the FBI. It mentions specific pages within the document referencing FBI involvement in various operations and programs, including electronic penetrations, use of CIA funds, and cooperation on Soviet defector cases. The document concludes that the FBI should obtain a copy of the 693-page CIA document.
