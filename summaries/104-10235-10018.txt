This document is a classified message, dated June 8, 1962. It outlines arrangements for "AMBUD-1" visit by "PACY." The arrangements include airport reception, breakfast with various Panamanian officials, supper with "HYJULA-1" and Dulcido Gonzalez, interviews with "HYJULA-1," President Chiari, Galileo Solis, and other cabinet ministers. It also includes a tour of the University of Panama and a visit to democratic labor unions. There's a reference to station funding for local propaganda value.  Airmail clippings and photos from MANA, SALV, GUAT, SANJ and TERU are mentioned for possible replay. Finally, Marco Robles, Minister Government and Justice is also identified.
