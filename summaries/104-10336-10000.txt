This document is a collection of memorandums and letters between the CIA and the Assassination Records Review Board (ARRB) regarding the ARRB's requests for information and records related to the JFK assassination. The topics range from specific individuals who may have worked for the Agency to general information about files and documents, including the status of various CIA responses to the ARRB's requests, both formal and informal. There are also discussions about operational reports for Cuban exile groups, tapes, and Angleton's files.
