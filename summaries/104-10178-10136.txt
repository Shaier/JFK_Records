This document is a secret cable from the CIA, dated April 18, 1968, concerning the suspected assassin of Martin Luther King, Jr. It relays information about the suspect's name, aliases, physical description, and possible employment. The cable requests that recipients evaluate any information reported, provide as much detail as possible, and prioritize positive replies to headquarters and legal attaches. It also indicates that a photograph of the suspect is available upon request. The cable includes routing and dissemination instructions, as well as instructions for liaison with other agencies.
