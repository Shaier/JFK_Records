This is a secret outgoing message from MIDDAUGH dated July 11, 1963, regarding books for a new G-2 co-opted subject. The sender urgently needs books on espionage and requests help from LONDON and ZURC in finding and sending them. The desired books cover various topics related to espionage, including historical examples, and should be in English or French. A list of books already sent is provided. A phone call about the availability of the books is appreciated and they should be mailed commercially to a private address.
