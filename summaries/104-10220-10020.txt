This document is a cable regarding Richard Case Nagell, an American citizen who has come to the attention of Swiss authorities. He registered at a Zurich hotel in April 1970 and reported to police that his raincoat, which contained a document intended for American military authorities in Berlin titled "Fiscal Year 70 (USAREUR)," had disappeared. The raincoat was later found without the document. When questioned by police, Nagell provided references for Col. Francis H. Dillon, Francis X. Plant, and Bruce A. Flatlin. The cable also states that Nagell claims to be living in a hotel in Berlin and is considered a fabricator and mental case. It requests permission to pass inbreed info and asks that US Army authorities be advised of the potential missing document.
