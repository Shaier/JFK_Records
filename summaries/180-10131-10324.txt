This document contains the transcript of a closed session of the House Select Committee on Assassinations Subcommittee on the Assassination of John F. Kennedy on June 20, 1978. The witness is Yuri Ivanovich Nosenko, and the questioning focuses on his knowledge of Lee Harvey Oswald, particularly regarding his time in the Soviet Union and the KGB's potential interest in him. The discussions cover Nosenko's previous testimonies, contradictions in his statements, and the circumstances surrounding Oswald's defection, mental state, and alleged surveillance.
