This document is a message from Mexico City, classified as secret. It reports that L-3, while in New York, contacted Cuban exiles and had a chance encounter with Antonio de Souza Carbajal. L-3 suggested defection to de Souza, who replied he was merely performing his duty and was frightened. De Souza stated he would talk with L-3 again under secure circumstances. L-3 describes de Souza as "a key man." The document requests traces, comments, and advice on another trip by L-3 to New York for the purpose of talking to de Souza.
