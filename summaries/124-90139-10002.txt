This document is a memorandum from the FBI to Mr. Brennan concerning Manuel Pineiro (also known as "Petronio"), dated October 10, 1963. It discusses information obtained from a CIA analyst regarding the identity of a Cuban agent named "Petronio," who is believed to be identical to Manuel Pineiro, a high-ranking official in Cuban intelligence. The memo relays CIA's reasoning for this conclusion based on intercepted communications. The information is being forwarded to the Nationalities Intelligence Section, and the document is classified as secret.
