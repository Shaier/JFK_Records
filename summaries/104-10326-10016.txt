This document is a secret communication from the Paris station to the Director, concerning the JFK Assassination Records Review Board (ARRB) and the continued protection of cover for certain activities. It details station responses to questions posed by the Director regarding potential impact of ARRB releases on operations, particularly concerning the potential for increased scrutiny by French intelligence services and media, and the possible compromise of unilateral and joint activities. The message requests advice on whether the Paris station can provide any additional background.
