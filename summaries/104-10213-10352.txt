This document is a routing and record sheet concerning a request from the House Select Committee on Assassinations (HSCA) for information on the criteria for opening, maintaining, controlling, and closing 201 files. It includes attached copies of a November 1974 DOHB regulation and an earlier version of a 1960 CSHB regulation, answering the committee's questions. The regulation classifies this information as SECRET, and is used to identify individuals with specific interest to the Operations Directorate, for monitoring purposes.
