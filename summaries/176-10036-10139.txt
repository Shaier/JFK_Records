This document is a CIA Information Report dated October 15, 1963, concerning a recommendation by cabinet officers that Ngo Dinh Nhu be made prime minister of South Vietnam. The report, classified as Confidential with "NO FOREIGN DISSEM" control, details how on October 8, 1963, an executive secretary indicated that Vice President Nhu told him that the cabinet decided to recommend that President Diem appoint Nhu as Prime Minister, requiring a constitutional amendment. The report also notes how on October 5, Vice President Nhu stated that he called a meeting to discuss Nhu's role, ultimately recommending his appointment as prime minister to hold him accountable, and that a constitutional amendment would be required but not difficult to pass.
