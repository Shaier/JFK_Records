This document is a payroll authorization form and related memos for Richard Newcombe Billings, who was hired as Editorial Chief for the Select Committee, effective March 6, 1978, at a salary of $30,000 per year. The document also includes a payroll certification form. It has been reviewed by the Assassination Records Review Board and postponed in part to protect Social Security numbers. The document is part of the HSCA Staff Payroll Records.
