This document is a secret cable concerning Richard Case Nagell, also known as Joe Craier, Joe Crane, and Robert C. Nolan. It discusses his upcoming visit to the consulate on February 27th and a warning from Nagell that he will reveal the story of his contacts with WOFIRM to the press if promises made to him by US officials are not honored. The cable instructs that the matter be left strictly to the consulate unless otherwise advised and asks for advice to be sent to LNER GO. It also references a previous state message from the US Mission in Berlin.
