This document is a historical staff report from the Central Intelligence Agency (CIA) concerning the Western Hemisphere Division (WHD) from 1946 to 1965. Authored by David R. McLeat and approved by David A. Phillips in December 1973, it details various CIA activities in Latin America, including operations related to Cuba, such as efforts to counter Fidel Castro's influence, support for the Bay of Pigs invasion, economic warfare tactics, and propaganda campaigns. The report also covers CIA operations in other countries, such as Mexico and Chile, as well as internal issues and assessments within the WHD.
