This document contains a series of classified communications related to "Operation AMSCROLL", a propaganda campaign aimed at undermining the Cuban government. The messages detail various actions, such as:

*   Disseminating anti-Castro information through media (newspapers, radio broadcasts, leaflets).
*   Targeting key individuals and groups (sugar brokers, members of the armed forces, Latin American leaders) with specific propaganda efforts.
*   Trying to influence international events to isolate Cuba economically and politically.
* Tracking individuals connected with the program and those who present potential security risks.
*  Managing the program's finances, and addressing the need to reduce costs.
*   Working with other organizations to forward the campaign's aims
