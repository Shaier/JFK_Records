This document is a notification from the Assassination Records Review Board regarding a record related to the HSCA (House Select Committee on Assassinations). The record in question pertains to staff payroll records. A portion of the record has been postponed from release due to the presence of social security numbers, the release of which would constitute an unwarranted invasion of privacy. A future review of the document is scheduled for 2017, with a final declassification date of 2025. The document also contains information about payroll authorizations for Paul A. DiPaolo, including his effective dates of employment, salary, and position title. It includes a memo about his hiring and responsibilities.
