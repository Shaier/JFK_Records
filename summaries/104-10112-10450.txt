This is a memorandum dated December 24, 1959, regarding R. L. Crozier (#112569). It confirms that the Office of Security would not approve his utilization in a staff capacity due to his non-staff status, cover, and security considerations related to his assignment in Mexico. It also states that the WH Division will be advised that OTR cannot assign him to isolation for security reasons. The memo is signed by Ervan E. Kuhnke, Jr.
