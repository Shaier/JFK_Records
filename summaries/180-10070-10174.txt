This document is a collection of payroll authorization forms and related memos for Elizabeth Ann Wolf, employed by the U.S. House of Representatives, specifically with the House Select Committee on Assassinations (HSCA). The forms detail changes in her position, salary, and other employment information from January 1977 to December 1978. A related memo discusses payroll certification requirements. The document has been reviewed by the Assassination Records Review Board, which has postponed the release of certain information related to social security numbers to protect personal privacy.
