This document is a memorandum from the CIA to the Associate General Counsel, OGC, providing information about past Agency contacts with Mitchell Livingston WerBell III. It summarizes DDO contacts with WerBell in 1959, discusses his alleged activities related to Cuba and the Dominican Republic, details his attempts to gain CIA support and funding, and notes concerns about his reputation and activities. It also mentions Agency contacts with other former employees, their connections to WerBell, and potential involvement in activities like establishing a tax shelter and selling arms. Finally, it notes the Agency's denial of any affiliation with WerBell.
