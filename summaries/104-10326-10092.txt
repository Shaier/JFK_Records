This document outlines a meeting between U.S. representatives and Leonid Ryabchenya of the Russian Foreign Intelligence Service (SVRR). The meeting covered several topics: Ryabchenya provided documents on the Middle East peace process, aid to Bosnian Muslims, and the situation in Somalia. He also discussed Oleg Nechiporenko's upcoming book tour in the U.S., emphasizing that the SVRR had no connection to the book. Ryabchenya inquired about the status of discussions on Yugoslavia and the publication of a paper by U.S. scientists on environmental issues. He expressed the SVRR's interest in arranging an exchange of views between U.S. and Russian scientists on environmental topics.
