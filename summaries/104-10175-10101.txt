This document is a classified message dated January 24, 1963, from Mexico City to the Director. It discusses British BBC newsman, PETTIE, who is reportedly in contact with LICOOKY-1. According to reports, PETTIE reads and writes fluent Russian and Finnish, also speaks Spanish and French, and lives with LICOOKY-1 a great deal of time, being her present lover. PETTIE is also reported to have contacted Jose Arevalo, and trying to make contact with Mario Fonteforte Toledo. It mentions that L-1 introduced PETTIE to Monteforte Toledo on January 3, 1963. The message suggests that the station may be in indirect contact with L-1 regarding exile activities. The comment requests a reply regarding a British inquiry about LICOOKY.
