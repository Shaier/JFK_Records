This FBI document, dated September 5, 1968, pertains to anti-Fidel Castro activities and is categorized as IS-CUBA. It references previous communications between the Bureau, San Juan, and Miami. Enclosures include LHM reports and Defense Intelligence Agency information regarding storage of supplies on Mona Island by a Cuban exile group. The document mentions a plan by Cuban exiles to ship arms from Venezuela and the potential involvement of a CIA source, Luis Posada. It also discusses the dissemination of information to various agencies, including U.S. Customs and the Coast Guard, and outlines investigative requests for the Bureau in Miami and San Juan.
