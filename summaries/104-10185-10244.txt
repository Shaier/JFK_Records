This document is a classified memorandum regarding a covert site at an apartment complex in Silver Spring, Maryland. It confirms security approval for the site and includes a security survey report identifying potential hazards. A search of security indices revealed no noteworthy information on individuals listed in the report. The document restricts storage of classified material at the site and prohibits its use by other agencies without prior approval. It requests the return of the attached report in a sealed envelope marked "To Be Opened By Addressee Only." The report contains information about the area surrounding the site, its accessibility, fire and police protection, potential security hazards, and biographical data on the residents of the apartments above and at the site.
