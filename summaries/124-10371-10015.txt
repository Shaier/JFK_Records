This document is a CIA report from 1974 regarding Carlos Miguel KASSEL, an agent working for the Soviets in Bogota, who traveled to Miami to gather information on Cuban exile activities. The report details Kassel's cover, his contacts within the Cuban exile community, and highlights from Kassel's report to his Soviet contact, including information on Cuban exile groups, their lack of U.S. government support, and their potential involvement in terrorism. The report also offers to keep the FBI informed of Kassel's future visits to Miami and recommends participation in meetings with Kassel to gather more information.
