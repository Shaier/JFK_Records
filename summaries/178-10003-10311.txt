This document is an identification form related to the John F. Kennedy Assassination Records Act of 1992. It details a CIA document titled "CIA 'HISTORIES': BREAKDOWN OF CERTAIN ITEMS REQUESTED..." from March 1975. The document is a list classified as Top Secret and discusses Anti-Castro activities, CIA operations, and Document requests from the Church Committee. It analyzes histories, noting those requested and not requested, organized by the Eisenhower, Nixon, Kennedy, and Johnson Administrations. The document also includes a section on miscellaneous "histories" not requested but potentially relevant.
