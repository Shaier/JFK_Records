This is a classified message from the Director to the ROME station, dated October 14, 1963, concerning the AMWHIP operation. It states that AMWHIP is due in Rome the week of October 13th and will contact the station per the old contact plan. The message cautions that Rome should not expect AMWHIP prior to that week. The message references DIR 71153 from September 25, 1963, and has a cite direction number of 75462. It is classified as SECRET.
