This document is a memorandum from the CIA to the FBI regarding an investigation into allegations about CIA activities. It includes information on specific individuals, including Mitchell Livingston Werbell III and John Patrick Muldoon, as well as information about searches conducted in the Office of Security records regarding Security Defense System, Intelligence Services, Ltd., Advance Security Corporation, and Howard Gibson. The memo also discusses information related to an individual potentially named Steve Van Cleave, and his claims of working for the CIA.
