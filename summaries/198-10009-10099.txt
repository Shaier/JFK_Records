This document is a collection of papers pertaining to the John F. Kennedy assassination records, specifically the "Califano Papers," from the U.S. Army. It includes classified Congressional testimony and memoranda concerning Cuban subversion in Latin America, the Cuban Missile Crisis, and Soviet military buildup and weapons in Cuba. The collection also contains an interim report by the Preparedness Investigating Subcommittee of the Committee on Armed Services regarding the Cuban military buildup, including a review of military developments, intelligence activities, and operations in connection with Cuba from early 1962 to the present.
