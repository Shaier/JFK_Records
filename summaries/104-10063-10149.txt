This document is a secret cable from the US government in Algiers, dated October 2, 1972. It discusses Melvin McNair, a Delta Airlines hijacker, who appeared in Algiers to obtain US passports for his children and the child of George Edward Wright, and Joyce Tillerson. It also mentions William Holder, a Western Airlines hijacker, named by the Black Panthers (BP) International Section as its Algiers head. The cable goes on to discuss Cleaver's activities and relationships with other individuals, including Peter O'Neal and Donald Cox, and the living arrangements of the hijackers in Algiers. It also mentions a raid on the BPP headquarters by Algerian authorities triggered by Cleaver's letter demanding the return of the hijack money. McNair's visit to Flanik's office and his observations about Algeria are also detailed.
