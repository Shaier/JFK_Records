This document is a memorandum summarizing the debriefing of Edward G. Tichborn following his trip to Cuba in early 1960. It details operational information and suggestions Tichborn offered, focusing on the political climate in Cuba, Fidel Castro's personality and potential vulnerabilities, and individuals who might have influence over Castro. The memo also mentions administrative matters related to Tichborn's trip, including financial accounting, cover letter, and potential reimbursement for medical expenses.
