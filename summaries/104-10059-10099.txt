This document consists of several memos and attachments related to a security clearance request for Robert West Pugh, specifically for his role as a First Officer in the Far East under Project HBILKA/JBGREED. It includes the initial approval of the covert security clearance, notes on concerning information about Pugh's past, including alcohol issues, and a brother with Communist ties. The document also contains a dispatch providing this same information, as well as administrative information on Pugh's resignation from a previous job, and a request to cancel the covert security approval.
