This document is a CIA information telegram regarding a potential coup d'etat in South Vietnam. It details plans by the Tuyen group, supported by Lieutenant Colonel Pham Ngoc Thao, to remove the Ngo family from power. The telegram mentions the involvement of other military figures and outlines a possible timeline for the coup. It also indicates that Brigadier General Nguyen Khanh would be involved in a related move in Hue.
