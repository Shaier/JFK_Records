This document is an interim working draft dated February 14, 1977, regarding "AMTRUNK Operation," with attachments. It details an operation in the early 1960s involving the potential use of AMTRUNK as a political action against the U.S.G./CIA. The operation involved an on-island strategy to split the Castro regime, and the document explores the roles of various individuals and the reasons for its ultimate termination. It also questions the loyalty of some individuals involved, including Tad SZULC.
