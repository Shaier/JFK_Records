This document is a collection of memos, statements, and summaries relating to the testimony of Howard J. Osborn before the Commission on CIA Activities Within the United States in 1975. It covers topics such as taping systems in CIA offices, covert use of listening devices, physical surveillance, Watergate contacts, covert informers employed by the agency, mail intercept programs, cover operations using local police cover, training of state and local police, and unauthorized break-ins. The document also discusses Mr. Osborn's background, activities of the Office of Security, and contact with the White House.
