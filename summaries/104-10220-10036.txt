This document is a SECRET communication from March 1969, originating from the Director, with priority information for Zurich, concerning "INK MHORDER WOMUSE". It references a Director communication from June 1968 and another related to Zurich. The subject contacted Zurich CG and threatened to disclose information about alleged WOFIRM contacts unless assurances were received regarding promises made by US officials. A request is made to confirm the subject's ties to WOFIRM or other LN HARP. It also notes a file deferment, and the document is marked "SECRET".
