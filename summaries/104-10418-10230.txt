This document is a memo sent from the Hague to the Director, concerning Willem Oltmans, who is described as a sympathizer. It details Oltmans' scheduled meetings with the Cuban and Russian ambassadors, his recent travel to the USSR, and his potential travel to Cuba. The memo suggests this information is of interest because of Oltmans' role at the time of George de Mohrenschild's death. The memo also mentions the Senate Select Committee on Intelligence and the House Assassinations Subcommittee's interest in de Mohrenschild. The memo suggests conveying the information, with appropriate source protection, to proper Congressional authorities if deemed appropriate.
