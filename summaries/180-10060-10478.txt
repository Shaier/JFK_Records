This document is from the Assassination Records Review Board, providing a final determination notification for a staff payroll record from the HSCA. The document was partially postponed due to the presence of social security numbers, which were redacted to protect personal privacy. A review of this material was scheduled for 2017. This is associated with the John F. Kennedy Assassination Records Collection Act of 1992 and the document is currently restricted. It includes information about Leodis C. Matthews, his employment with the HSCA as a staff counsel and senior staff attorney, his salary, and his professional background. It also includes a payroll certification and information about internal memorandum related to his employment.
