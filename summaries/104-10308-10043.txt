This document consists of a memorandum for the record about the JMARC Program, along with related classified messages.

The memorandum describes files from 1960-61 concerning support activities for the training and supply of infiltration forces.

The classified messages discuss individuals potentially connected to the Castro regime, possible assassination plots, and concerns about trusting information from certain sources. Specifically, the messages deal with Eduardo OJEDA Camaraza and someone called MORATORI.
