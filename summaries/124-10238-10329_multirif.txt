This document is a memorandum from the Legal Counsel to Mr. Adams, reporting on a conversation with John T. Elliff, Director of the Domestic Intelligence Task Force for the Senate Select Committee, regarding the upcoming testimony of the Attorney General. Elliff requested information on several key areas of FBI operations being investigated by the Committee, including topics like COINTELPRO, use of informants, and interception of mails.
