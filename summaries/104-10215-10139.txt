This document is a SECRET message, dated July 7, 1969, concerning a priority request for isolation information, directed to EGLIN, related to projects PBRUXEN KUROAR CHAPPIE RANROD. It requests training for a six-man leader group scheduled to arrive at isolation on July 11th. The message details the type of training requested, emphasizing leadership, and lists the names of the trainees, along with information about their prior training. It also requests airlift for the leader team to ISO on the night of July 11th and the return of the AMANCHOR group ISO VAVE on the night of July 12th.
