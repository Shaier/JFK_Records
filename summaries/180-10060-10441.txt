This document is a collection of payroll authorization forms and related documents for Louis H. Hindle, who worked for the House Select Committee on Assassinations. The forms detail changes in his position, salary, and other employment-related information from 1976 to 1978. A memorandum regarding payroll certification for relationships with members of congress is also included. It also has a document from the Assassination Records Review Board, detailing a postponement of records due to concerns regarding unwarranted invasion of personal privacy for social security numbers.
