This document is a memorandum from W.O. Cregar of the FBI to W.R. Wannall, dated July 23, 1975, regarding "SENSTUDY 75". It summarizes a 693-page CIA document from 1973 that cites instances of CIA operations possibly exceeding its mandate. The memo highlights 190 pages provided by the CIA at the FBI's request, noting references to FBI-CIA cooperation, technical support provided by CIA to the FBI, and CIA activities potentially impacting the FBI. Specific topics include electronic coverage of embassies, handling of defectors, domestic surveillance, and mail interception. The document also mentions concerns about CIA's interest in domestic dissident groups and attempts to identify all references in the CIA document for possible damage assessment to the FBI.
