This document is a collection of memoranda and reports regarding actions taken by the US government, particularly the Departments of Defense and State, and the CIA, to counter Cuban subversion in Latin America during the period of 1963 and 1964. It includes information on control of travel to and from Cuba, control of propaganda, clandestine movement of guerrillas and arms, transfer of funds, strengthening of counter-insurgency capabilities, and the exchange of intelligence. It also details specific actions taken in various Latin American countries, including Argentina, Bolivia, Brazil, Costa Rica, Ecuador, Guatemala, Honduras, Mexico, and Venezuela.
