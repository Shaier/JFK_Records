This document is a memorandum from Linda A. Jasper, Deputy Chief, Office of Central Cover, to John Pereira of the Historical Review Group, dated November 19, 1996. It provides answers to questions posed by the John F. Kennedy Assassination Review Board regarding the CIA's commercial cover program. The memo includes information on the connection of businesses to the publications industry, the number of participating companies in the 1960s and currently, and provides copies of the secrecy agreements used in the program. The document indicates that the information is for background only and must be returned to the Central Intelligence Agency after review.
