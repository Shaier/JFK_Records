This is a SECRET message from Richard Ober, CIOP/DO, dated April 20, 1968. It requests addresses to construct an Identikit composite of James Earl Ray for use prior to receiving photos that are en route. It also provides a code and a note about a scar on Ray's forehead that was omitted from the composite because the exact location is unknown, and that his left ear protrudes more than his right. The message is marked with priority no night action and is addressed to various cities around the world.
