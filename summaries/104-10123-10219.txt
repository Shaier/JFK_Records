This document is a memorandum proposing key personnel assignments within the Office of Security. It suggests that Mr. Arthur C. Sheridan replace Mr. James P. O'Connell, Jr. in Okinawa, that Mr. James W. McCord, Jr. replace Mr. Sheridan as Chief, Technical Division, and that Mr. James P. O'Connell, Jr. be assigned to a GS-16 position as Chief, Investigations Division upon his return to Headquarters. The Director of Security, Howard J. Osborn, approved the assignments on February 26, 1965.
