This document is a dispatch from the Chief of Station in Havana to the Chief of the WHD regarding Arthur G. Vaivada's upcoming reassignment. Vaivada has requested reassignment upon the completion of his term in Havana in June 1958. The station chief recommends that Vaivada's request be given consideration. Vaivada also expressed an interest in a possible unofficial assignment to Spain. The dispatch also includes a memorandum from Vaivada outlining his background and preferences for future assignments. He notes his experience in Cuba, the challenges he has faced, and his willingness to travel to any area, though he expresses a desire to leave the Central America-Caribbean region if possible. Vaivada also discusses his cover status and options.
