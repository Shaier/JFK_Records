This document is a notification of the establishment of a military cover backstop. It includes details such as the recipient (Chief, Records and Services Division or Chief, Operating Component), file number, and ID card number. The form also specifies whether the backstop is temporary or continuing, and includes instructions to submit forms for changes or transfers of responsibility. Remarks section contains information about cover history including OSGR, average, and NOC, and also what to do if the target applies for a governmental position. The form is signed by the Chief of Military Cover.
