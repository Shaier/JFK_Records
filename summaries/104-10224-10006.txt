This document contains a collection of personnel records and related correspondence regarding James W. McCord, Jr., his employment and career at the Central Intelligence Agency (CIA) from the 1950s to his retirement in 1970. It includes performance evaluations, awards, training records, and documents related to his appointment, promotions, and eventual involuntary retirement from the Agency. The material also details actions to secure and maintain security clearances and documentation related to a "Watergate File Review Flag."
