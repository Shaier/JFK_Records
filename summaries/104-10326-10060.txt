This document is an intelligence report concerning suspected Cuban security penetrations of the Cuban human rights/dissident movement in Cuba, covering the period from 1992 to mid-August 1995. It focuses on the Cuban Civic Current (CCC), a dissident group, and identifies individuals suspected of being Cuban security sources or provocateurs. The report details incidents and observations that raised suspicion among CCC members regarding these individuals' activities and loyalties. The report also includes source information, warnings, and dissemination instructions.
