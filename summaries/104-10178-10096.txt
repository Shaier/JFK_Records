This document is a collection of Central Intelligence Agency (CIA) records related to an individual named Luis Clemente Posada-Carriles and his connections to Frank "Lefty" Rosenthal and other individuals involved in bombings and criminal activities in Miami during the 1960s. The records include memos, dispatches, debriefing reports, and other communications concerning Posada-Carriles's alleged involvement in supplying materials and acting as an intermediary in transactions involving explosives. The documents also touch on the FBI's investigation of Rosenthal and Posada-Carriles's employment with the Cuban Telephone Company and his suspected ties to Cuban intelligence. Additionally, the records address the Justice Department's interest in the case and their consideration of various legal and operational strategies.
