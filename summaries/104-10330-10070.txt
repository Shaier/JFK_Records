This document is a memorandum from the CIA to the Assassination Records Review Board (ARRB) regarding the release of the true names of human intelligence sources appearing in the JFK Collection. It details CIA concerns and proposes a method for addressing this issue. It outlines the differences between CIA sources, FBI sources, and covert employees. It further covers U.S. legal considerations and operational considerations with the protection of human intelligence sources. The document concludes with a proposal for protecting CIA sources within the JFK collection.
