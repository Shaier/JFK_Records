This document is a Central Intelligence Agency file, part of the collection released under the President John F. Kennedy Assassination Records Act of 1992. It contains memoranda regarding the Latin America Division's Task Force report on possible Cuban complicity in the Kennedy assassination. The report reviewed Agency operations against Cuba from 1960-1965, but found no definitive proof of Cuban involvement. The document includes a chronology of findings, a list of task force participants, and an outline of the complete LAD/JFK Task Force files.
