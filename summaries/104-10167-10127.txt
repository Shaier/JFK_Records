This document is a dispatch from a CIA officer (code name KARINZY) to the Miami JMWAVE station, reporting on a contact with a Cuban exile (code name DCHAR) and his group in August 1957. The dispatch covers topics such as the group's inactivity due to lack of funds, plans for establishing a propaganda program, knowledge of Soviet Russians in Cuba, and the possibility of re-establishing communication with contacts in Cuba to gather information on communist activities within the Cuban armed forces. The CIA officer proposes exploring connections between the group and Julio Lobo to secure funds and recommends further investigation into DCHAR's information regarding Fidel Castro's contacts and communist activities.
