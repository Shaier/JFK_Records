This document consists of FBI communications and internal memos from 1962 to 1975. It discusses investigations, briefings, and requests for documents related to several high profile topics from that era such as Martin Luther King, Jr., the Vietnam War protests, and COINTELPRO. Much of the information pertains to Stanley Levison, Gary Thomas Rowe, and other informants. It relates to Senate Select Committee to Study Governmental Operations with Respect to Intelligence Activities (SSC) inquiries into various intelligence-related matters.
