This document pertains to a letter of indemnity for Collins Radio, involving a non-governmental liaison. The document includes memoranda and correspondence between CIA personnel, including William F. Donnelly and Anthony A. Lapham, regarding contractual agreements with Collins Radio through the Liechtenstein Anstalt Electronic Products International Etablissement, an instrumentality of the US Government. These letters concern agreements, assurances, and inspection arrangements, to protect the Agency proprietary interest and guarantee agreements entered into by Collins Radio, guaranteed by the Agency.
