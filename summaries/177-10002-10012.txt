This document, dated February 18, 1963, is a draft briefing note from the CIA regarding Cuban subversion in Latin America. It states that Fidel Castro is encouraging and supporting Communist efforts to overthrow Latin American governments. The document indicates that while direct Cuban support is not always evident, Cuba provides inspiration, guidance, training, communications, and technical assistance to revolutionaries. Venezuela is identified as a high-priority target for revolution, with Cuban involvement in training Venezuelan guerrillas. Despite violence in Venezuela, it is not seen as an immediate threat to government control.
