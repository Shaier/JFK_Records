This document relays information from Miami Field Office File # 105-1391. A liaison service in Venezuela informed our representatives in Caracas that a subject was arrested upon arrival from Miami and is being held by Venezuelan authorities. The liaison service informed that the subject was in Venezuela to assassinate an unknown target for $15,000.  The liaison service added that the evidence is insufficient to prosecute and asked about FBI or INS interest, indicating intentions to deport to Miami if there is U.S. interest, otherwise releasing on the condition the subject leaves Venezuela.
