This document is a memorandum regarding a project called AMCANOE, concerning U.S. contacts for a resistance group in Cuba, the Unidad de Liberacion Nacional (ULN), and their plans to overthrow Castro. It lists the principle players in this project with their identifying numbers and notes regarding some of them. The document also summarizes two other documents. One discusses efforts by a Cuban lawyer in Chicago to unify exiles and obtain business support for overthrowing Castro. The other references a man named Cameron representing an unidentified group seeking to assassinate Castro and other Cuban leaders, with likely MAFIA involvement.
