This document is an FBI communication from the Special Agent in Charge (SAC) in Miami to the Director of the FBI. It concerns Carlos Hilario Gonzalez and an alleged plot to kill Esteban Ventura. The information originated from Ana Rosa Guerra, who works in an undercover capacity in Cuba for Bernard Labarker and reportedly for the CIA. The memorandum about the plot is classified to protect Guerra's identity. Information on the plot has been shared with local law enforcement, and Ventura will be advised of the threat, but no protection can be provided by the FBI.
