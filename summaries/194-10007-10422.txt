This document is an agent report from INSCOM/CSF about Victor L. Trinkala, a conscientious objector, who received a discharge from the Army. The report includes information about Richard C. Nagell's alleged involvement in getting Trinkala separated from military service, physical descriptions of Nagell, and a summary of an interview with Harfez Muneer, a hotel desk clerk, regarding his knowledge of Nagell and Trinkala. The report also details the circumstances surrounding Trinkala's conscientious objector status and his discharge from the Army.
