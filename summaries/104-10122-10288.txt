This document is a memorandum discussing Robert D. Peloquin, the President of International Intelligence, Inc. (INTERTEL). It details the background of INTERTEL, its association with Resorts International, and its engagement by the Hughes Tool Company to investigate Robert A. Maheu. The memo also explores potential connections between Maheu, the CIA, and organized crime, as well as concerns about Edward P. Morgan's involvement. It expresses a desire for confidentiality regarding the source of the information.
