This document is a memorandum for the record summarizing the minutes of a Special Group meeting held on June 6, 1961. The meeting included discussions on topics such as the Congo, review of political action projects, release procedures regarding Francis Gary Powers, STPOLLY flights in Vietnam, covert annexes for Vietnam and Korea, TACKLE (a program needing more information), Nicaragua, Cuba, and the distribution of Special Group meeting minutes. Key decisions and opinions were noted, and certain items were deferred for future meetings or action.
