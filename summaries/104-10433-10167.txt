This document is a collection of memoranda and teletypes related to the FBI's investigation of the assassination of Martin Luther King, Jr. The documents include information about:

*   Allegations made by Hernan Henriquez Lora and efforts to verify or disprove them.
*   Information provided by the CIA about individuals who may have had information about the assassination, including their backgrounds and activities.
*   CIA concerns about the appearance of their ex-employees at a conference commemorating the tenth anniversary of President John F. Kennedy's death.
*   Efforts to track currency that may have been used by James Earl Ray.
*   The activities of the Committee to Investigate Assassinations.
