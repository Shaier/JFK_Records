This document is a memorandum from the Chief of the Information Review Group/OIM to the Deputy Executive Director regarding pending issues with the Assassination Records Review Board (ARRB) related to JFK assassination records. It details the CIA's multi-step approach to protecting Agency equities. The memo discusses specific issues such as unacknowledged covert actions, alias documentation, organizational materials, Mexico City tapes, protection of a CIA asset, and certain files of CIA officials. It outlines the CIA's strategies for negotiation and compromise with the ARRB staff and Board, including arguments for relevance, potential damage from release, and the need for detailed presentations. The document also mentions the resources dedicated to the JFK records review and anticipates increased effort as the statutory end of the Board approaches.
