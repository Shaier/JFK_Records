This document is a cable from the Hague, dated May 4, 1976, classified as Secret. It discusses William L. Oltmans, a Dutch freelance journalist. It describes his history of sensational journalism and homosexual activity, his leftist views and pro-communism stance regarding Indonesia, his alleged close ties with prominent Indonesians including Sukarno, his ongoing conflict with NATO Secretary General Josef Luns, and his travels to the USSR and the Bloc. It also mentions his contacts with Soviet diplomats suspected of being KGB agents and his claims of knowing prominent figures like Jack Anderson and Howard Jones. The document references earlier information about Oltmans from London and suggests tracing him if it hasn't already been done.
