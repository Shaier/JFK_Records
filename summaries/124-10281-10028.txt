This document is a memorandum from the FBI regarding the possibility that the Communist Party of Venezuela may kidnap the child of a CIA employee named Thomas Keogh. The FBI obtained fingerprints and photographs of Keogh's family members, including Joseph Edward Keogh, Thomas Patrick Keogh Jr., Christina Margarita Keogh, Elizabeth Teresa Keogh, Francis Xavier Keogh, Thomas Patrick Keogh III, and Maria Rita Keogh, and furnished them to the CIA. The memorandum recommends sending a copy and attachment directly to SA Carl Volker to classify the fingerprints.
