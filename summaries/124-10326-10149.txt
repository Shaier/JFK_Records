This document is an FBI record regarding anti-Castro activities related to Cuba. It details information provided by Michael J.P. Malone, Vice-President of Czarnikow-Rionda Company, who is also employed by Robert Kleberg of the King Ranch. Malone shared memoranda of conversations he had in Washington D.C., written for Kleberg after Kleberg's meeting with Vice President Lyndon B. Johnson.

The memoranda contain information about discussions with a CIA contact ("HERBERT"), regarding US government opinions and potential actions concerning Cuba. Additionally, the memoranda mention conversations with Charles Maechin, another CIA contact, and Serafino Romualdi of the AFL-CIO, discussing potential plans, build-up, and the influence on President Kennedy's policy toward Cuba.
