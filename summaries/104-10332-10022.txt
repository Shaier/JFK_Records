This document contains correspondence and memos related to the Central Intelligence Agency's (CIA) compliance with the President John F. Kennedy Assassination Records Collection Act of 1992. It discusses various aspects of this compliance, including identifying and releasing records, designating compliance officials, the CIA's final declaration of compliance, and potential depositions of CIA officials. There's also discussion on conducting new searches for records, review of documents, as well as, a memorandum of understanding that lists obligations and responsibilities.
