This document is a collection of letters and internal memos related to the Assassination Records Review Board's (ARRB) review of CIA records concerning the JFK assassination in 1997. The ARRB expresses concern about the pace of CIA's review and declassification process, emphasizing the need to meet the deadline for transferring records to the National Archives. The CIA responds by affirming its commitment to meeting its statutory obligations but highlighting challenges such as resource constraints and unexpected deadlines. Internal CIA memos discuss the size of the JFK declassification project, staffing levels, and potential budget impacts on the effort. The CIA is requesting funds for the JFK Assassination Records Review Project and also for a project focused on the Declassification of Korean Documents.
