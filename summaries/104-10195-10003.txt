This document is a Central Intelligence Agency (CIA) Security Approval form, dated November 9, 1956, concerning James Bernard Wilcott, Jr. It indicates that security approval is granted for access to classified information. It also notes that the subject will be polygraphed as part of the Entrance on Duty (EOD) procedures.
