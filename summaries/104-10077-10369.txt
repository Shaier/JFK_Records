This document is a classified message sent from LABUZZETTI to JAVE on December 10, 1963. It is marked as "SECRET" and classified under the President John F. Kennedy Assassination Records Act of 1992. The message is routed to DEPERCEN, and the cite direction is 88252. The information pertains to the WAVE project and references PRUMEN (Quota) 88251. The message identifies four individuals: Odon Alvarez Delala Campa, Jose Luis Gonzalez Gallarretta, Gilberto Liway Rodrigues (201-338941), and Rogelio Iglesias Patino. The cable secretariat is instructed to attach a cable to this one. The message is routed to SAS/BOR Mr. Armstrong (TELECOORD) and SAS/MOB Mr. Maggio (TELECOORD).
