This document is an outline of a report titled "LEE HARVEY OSWALD, THE CIA AND MEXICO CITY". It covers the CIA's surveillance operations in Mexico City in relation to Lee Harvey Oswald's visit, information known by the CIA Mexico City Station prior to President Kennedy's assassination, and related investigation and analysis. It also addresses questions around a potential photograph of Lee Harvey Oswald and possibilities of wiretap information. The document further addresses information unavailable to the Warren Commission, including matters related to Silvia Duran and Elena Garro. Finally, it includes several appendices outlining procedural write-ups, biographies, glossaries, and listings of cited CIA documents.
