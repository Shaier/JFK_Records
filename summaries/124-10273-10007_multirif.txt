This document is a collection of memoranda, reports, letters, and other materials related to the FBI's interactions with the Senate Select Committee (SSC) and the House Select Committee on Intelligence Activities, dated from April 1975 through May 1975. The materials pertain to various aspects of FBI information sharing with the committees, including security clearances, access to documents, requests for information, responses to inquiries, and the development of guidelines for handling sensitive material. The correspondence also concerns coordination with other government agencies, such as the CIA, Department of Justice, and the White House, in responding to congressional requests.
