This document is a collection of payroll authorizations and memoranda related to Yvonne Briggs' employment with the House of Representatives, specifically in the Assassinations Subcommittee/Committee. The documents detail her appointment, salary adjustments, and certifications regarding familial relationships with members of Congress. The records show her starting in March 1978 as a secretary, a salary adjustment in June 1978, and a review board notification about postponing the public release of the record for privacy reasons.
