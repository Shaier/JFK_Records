This document is a memorandum for the files regarding the "Movimiento Democratica Cristiano". It mentions the subject organization being of interest to (CA/1) because they forwarded a letter to Bela Kiraly of the Hungarian Freedom Fighters Inc. James Comion (CAL) requested security data on the organization and the writer of the letter. The memorandum also notes that the organization is an Anti-Castro group, and the letter's writer was of interest to the Agency under Operational Approval in 1960.
