This document is a memorandum from the FBI regarding James Angleton, a CIA official who is a valuable source of information for the Bureau. It details his role in the CIA, his sources of information (including the Israeli Intelligence Service and American Federation of Labor representatives), and his past cooperation with the FBI.

The memo recommends creating a control file for information received from Angleton and disseminating instructions to Section Chiefs on handling this information securely. It also mentions that Angleton has been authorized to cooperate fully with the FBI's Liaison Agent and provide any information he deems fit.
