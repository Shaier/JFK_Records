This document is a travel briefing form, marked as "Secret" when filled in, and pertains to the John F. Kennedy assassination records. It includes fields for personal and travel information, previous travel history, risk assessment, security considerations, and briefing details. The form also covers emergency contacts, security regulations, personal affairs, conduct, foreign associations, audio surveillance, cover problems, and responsibilities abroad. It indicates whether security approvals have been verified, and if the briefing has been waived. The form concludes with date and signature lines for both the briefee and security officer.
