This document is a memorandum from the SSCIA (Senate Select Committee on Intelligence Activities) regarding the assassination of Rafael Trujillo, dictator of the Dominican Republic. It lists documents related to the Special Group Minutes from 1960-1964 (excluding 1962) that the committee wants to examine.

The memo highlights documents related to covert actions against Cuba, Dominican Republic, Congo, Haiti, and specific proposals and discussions regarding Trujillo and Castro. It mentions various operations, proposals, and papers related to sabotage, support for dissidents, contingency plans, and the potential removal of leaders. The document also references meetings, memos, reports, and summaries, indicating that the assassination plots had U.S. involvement and knowledge.
