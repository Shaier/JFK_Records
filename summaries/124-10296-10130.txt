This document is a collection of memos and forms related to an FBI investigation into George Hoover Pratt and potential connections to James Warren Smedley, William Herman Jackson (aka Jack Karl Lawhorn) and possible CIA involvement. The FBI seeks CIA assistance in identifying Pratt and his associates, including through "official Thai sources," concerning their connections to the "Black Movement," and the CIA provides information on the individuals, including potential ties to Laos and Vietnam. The document includes requests for fingerprint records and discussions about the security classification of the information.
