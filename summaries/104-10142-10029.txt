This document consists of journal entries from the Office of Legislative Counsel on Wednesday, March 30, 1977. It details liaison activities regarding information requests and correspondence with various individuals and offices, including:

*   Responding to a request from Mr. James Fiesta in the office of Representative Clarence D. Long regarding a constituent's request for information on the Kennedy assassination.
*   Confirming transportation arrangements for Anne Karalekas of the Senate Select Committee on Intelligence to speak at a course and a conference.
*   Requesting and receiving copies of published subcommittee hearings from Linda, secretary to Pauline Baker of the Senate Foreign Relations Subcommittee.
*   Providing a copy of "A Study of Climatological Research as it Pertains to Intelligence Problems" to Winslow Wheeler from the office of Senator Jacob K. Javits.
