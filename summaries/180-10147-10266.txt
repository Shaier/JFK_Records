This document is a collection of records from the Assassination Records Review Board, relating to William A. Webb, who was a staff member. It includes payroll authorization forms, a memorandum regarding payroll certification, and another memorandum introducing Webb as a new staff attorney. Some information, such as social security numbers, has been redacted due to privacy concerns, with a planned review in 2017. The documents are part of the John F. Kennedy Assassination Records Collection Act and are scheduled for release in 2025.
