This document is a collection of payroll-related forms and memos concerning Ernestine G. Johnson's employment with the House Select Committee on Assassinations (HSCA) in 1977-78. These documents authorize her hiring as a Staff Investigator for the Martin Luther King, Jr. Task Force, detail her salary and adjustments, and include a payroll certification regarding her relationship to members of Congress. The first page is a notification of the determination to postpone part of the document from release due to Social Security numbers.
