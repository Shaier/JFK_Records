This document is a memorandum from the Deputy Director of Security to the Director of Personnel regarding the issuance of a CIA Identification Card to an employee of TFW under an alias. The memo requests the Office of Personnel to establish an indices card for the alias and to acknowledge Agency employment if inquiries are received, while also notifying the Cover Support Section of the Office of Security. It references a prior memorandum and explains that an exception was made to the general rule against issuing CIA identification in alias to (State Department) integreess due to the Subject's position and experience.
