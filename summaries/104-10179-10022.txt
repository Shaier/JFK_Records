This is a classified message from Havana to the Director, dated November 18, 1960. The message states that Nicolas Mendoza told the Havana station on November 17th that his brother, Ignacio, had not left Havana as planned and now intends to stay a few more days in hopes of obtaining permission from the Cuban government to leave legally. The message also mentions Ray, as a contact, and notes that more information will be advised later. A comment indicates concern that "Mendoza" is coming to Miami on September 12th.
