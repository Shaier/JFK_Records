This document is an outside contact report from the HSCA (House Select Committee on Assassinations), dated November 25, 1977, concerning Abraham Bolden, a former Secret Service agent. It details a telephone conversation with Mr. William Foster, a federal probation officer who supervised Bolden during his probation. Foster provided information about Bolden's prison record, parole violation, and prosecution, as well as names and addresses of Bolden's relatives. Foster noted the uniqueness of Bolden's case due to the lack of information provided to the probation officer by the prosecutor.
