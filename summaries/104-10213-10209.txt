This document is a CIA Directorate of Operations (DDO) instruction outlining restrictions on the operational use of certain categories of individuals. It defines "operational use," lists prohibited and restricted categories, and specifies approval procedures. The instruction covers various individuals and groups, including members of organizations like ACTION, Fulbright grantees, employees of U.S. government agencies, citizens of allied countries, members of the academic community, and others. It emphasizes the need for extra-agency concurrence or DDO approval in specific cases. The document also states that the provisions are subject to interpretation and modification by the Deputy Director for Operations.
