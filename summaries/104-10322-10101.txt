This document is a memorandum regarding a "Talking Paper for Briefing Chairman Stokes, HSCA, on KITTY HAWK." It discusses a request for access to reports and documents referring to "KITTY HAWK", described as a project concerning the debriefing of a Soviet defector and a KGB analysis of the Kennedy assassination. The document describes "KITTY HAWK" as a very sensitive in-place KGB source and states that their holdings have no reference to any KGB analysis of the Kennedy assassination. It also touches on KITTY HAWK's impression about Kennedy's assassination, support of Nosenko, and related implications.
