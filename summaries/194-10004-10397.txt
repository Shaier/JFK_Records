This document is an Agent Report from INSCOM/CSF regarding Manuel Marti. It details a neighborhood check conducted on October 24, 1968, at Marti's former residence at 905 East 167th Street, Bronx, New York. Residents were interviewed to find someone with knowledge of Marti and his family, who resided there from 1946-1965. The report summarizes the responses of Bonita Rodriguez, Simona Padron, and Celia Robateau, none of whom had any knowledge of Marti or his family. Agent's notes state that Alexander Castillo was the only competent source regarding the subject in the neighborhood other than Ramona Rodriguez and Luisa Perez.
