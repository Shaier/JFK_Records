This document is a classified message from July 1968, originating from JMCOBRA and addressed to the Director, Santo Domingo, with information for Port au Prince. It discusses several individuals, including "SECEN" (who was formerly "EM-SHAM-1"), his business contacts, and his possible involvement with Masferrer. The message also mentions "Father Georges" and explores the connections and activities of these individuals in the Dominican Republic. The second page discusses "Sy Mandel" who is potentially "Seymour Mandel," and their illegal activities.
