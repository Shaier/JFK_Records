This document is a memorandum from the Director of Security to the CIA regarding a manuscript titled "Give Us This Day; CIA and the Bay of Pigs Invasion" by Edward J. Hamilton. The memo provides comments on the manuscript, including its publication history, content, potential security implications, and the author's perspective on the Bay of Pigs affair and various individuals involved. It assesses the manuscript's impact on CIA security, particularly regarding potential disclosures of sensitive information and the author's criticisms of certain figures and policies. Overall, the memo analyzes the manuscript's content and its potential impact on the CIA and its past operations.
