This document is an FBI record from 1976 concerning the JFK assassination system, specifically detailing information related to Fidel Castro, speech, allegations of CIA involvement, the Cubana airliner bombing, and other relevant subjects. The document includes information provided by the DOS (Department of State) and outlines questions for the CIA regarding its relationship with individuals involved, such as Luis Posada Carriles, Orlando Bosch, and others, as well as relevant details surrounding the Cubana airliner bombing. The document also covers the CIA's relationship with other figures implicated in the events and allegations made by Fidel Castro. It includes data regarding the backgrounds, activities, and alleged connections of various individuals to the CIA and anti-Castro groups. It is subject to restrictions under the JFK Act 6 (1)(B) and JFK Act 6 (4).
