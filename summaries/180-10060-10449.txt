This document contains records related to Margo Jackson's employment with the House Select Committee on Assassinations (HSCA) staff payroll records. It includes payroll authorization forms from 1977-1978 showing her initial hiring as a document clerk, a salary adjustment, and her final employment date. Additionally, there are internal memos regarding her employment approval, salary offer, and the unit she was assigned to.  A staff certification form verifying her relationship to members of congress is also included.
