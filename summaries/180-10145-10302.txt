This document is a collection of records related to Victor Paneque, a Cuban national and reported agent of DIER (Departamento Investigativo Del Ejercito Revolucionario). The records span from 1960 to 1977 and include information on Paneque's background, political activities, associations, defection to the US, potential involvement in assassination plots, and eventual recruitment by the CIA. The documents consist of memos, cables, reports, and assessments, detailing Paneque's movements, contacts, and activities in both Cuba and the US. Some of the information contains references to covert operations, code names, and intelligence gathering efforts related to Paneque.
