This document is a CIA information report dated June 19, 1963, concerning a white paper being prepared by the government of South Vietnam. The white paper addresses the Buddhist issue and American presence and policy in South Vietnam. Gene Gregory of the Times of Vietnam is involved in drafting the paper, at the request of Madame Ngo Dinh Nhu. The white paper will compare South Vietnam to the United States, highlighting issues like gangsterism and race riots in the US, to suggest the Diem regime isn't so bad. The paper is intended for international distribution, primarily targeting the United States.
