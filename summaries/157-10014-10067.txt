This document is a transcript of a hearing held before the Senate Select Committee to Study Governmental Operations with Respect to Intelligence Activities on September 9, 1975. The hearing focuses on the JFK assassination system and includes the testimony of Bronson Tweedy, the former Chief of the African Division of the CIA, regarding the Congo, Lumumba's assassination, and the CIA's involvement. The document contains discussions about contacts with the State Department, meetings with key officials, the possibility of assassinating Lumumba, and the use of poisons. It also includes mentions of various individuals like Richard Bissell, Sidney Gottlieb, and O'Donnell, as well as references to cable traffic and operational details.
