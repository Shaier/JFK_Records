This document is a 1971 agent report from INSCOM/CSF regarding Henry S. Beer and his step-son Joachim Friedrich Eberhard Beer/Pehl. The report contains information such as names, dates of birth, marital status, military information, and security clearance details. The report indicates that Joachim Friedrich Eberhard Beer was adopted by Henry S. Beer. The last page was declassified in 1994.
