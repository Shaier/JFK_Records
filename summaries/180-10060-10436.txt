This document is a collection of payroll authorization forms and related documents for Gerald P. Hamilton, an employee of the House Select Committee on Assassinations (HSCA). The forms detail his employment, salary adjustments, position titles (including Deputy Chief Researcher), and periods of service from 1977-1978. Included is a memorandum related to payroll certification. It also includes the final determination notification that these records have been reviewed by the Assassination Records Review Board and are being released with redactions of Social Security numbers to protect personal privacy.
