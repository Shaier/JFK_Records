This document is an FBI memorandum dated March 1, 1963, concerning Angela Prince Moutsos, also known as SM-C, who departed from New York for Havana, Cuba in late 1960 to join her husband, Pedros Moutsos. The memorandum requests that CIA sources be alerted for any information regarding the subject's activities and residence in Cuba. The memorandum indicates that Pedros Moutsos was a source utilized by the CIA.
