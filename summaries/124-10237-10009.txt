This document is an FBI record related to the assassination of President John F. Kennedy in Dallas on November 22, 1963. It includes information from the Legat in Bern (Switzerland) concerning the interview of Anghel Dragomir. The record includes 25 copies each of insert pages 1 and 2, which incorporate the translated letter from Dragomir with comments. The document also mentions another government agency involved in the investigation, the Research Branch (Covert CID) of the Embassy in Vienna, Austria.
