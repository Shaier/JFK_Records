This document is a classified message sent from Havana to the Director on December 16, 1958. It contains intelligence regarding the movements of Ernesto Guevara and his forces, as well as information about the 26th of July Movement's activities in sugar centrales. The message also mentions the visit of Manuel Ray to Sierra Maestra to discuss the dissolution of the MRC into the 26th of July Movement. It details information on small FEU forces and a seized arms shipment, and lastly states that Batista has contracted two French army officers experienced in guerilla warfare to advise the Cuban army.
