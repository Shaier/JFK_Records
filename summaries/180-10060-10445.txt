This document is a collection of payroll authorization forms and related paperwork for Thomas Howarth, an employee of the US House of Representatives, specifically working for the Select Committee on Assassinations, and later as a Budget Officer, and Deputy Staff Director & Budget Officer. The forms detail his salary adjustments and position changes from 1976 to 1978. There's also a memorandum regarding payroll certification requirements. Some information has been redacted for privacy reasons.
