This document is a memorandum from the Director of Security to the Director of the Federal Bureau of Investigation. It concerns Court Foster Wood and his associations with Michael Weimer Stratton, a current Agency employee, with the focus on Wood's potential involvement with Cuba. Stratton was interviewed about his knowledge of Wood and has been advised to discontinue contact with him due to concerns about Wood's activities and associates. The Director of Security requests that the FBI provide any additional information they may have concerning Wood, especially regarding his past associations. The information is for internal use only.
