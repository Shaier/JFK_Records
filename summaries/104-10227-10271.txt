This document is a report from the FBI regarding Anti-Fidel Castro activities and internal security in Cuba. It details information received from a confidential informant about communications between Carlos Alejos, the Guatemalan Ambassador to the United States, and Miguel Ydigoras-Fuentes, the President of Guatemala, regarding Jose Miro Cardona, President of the Cuban Revolutionary Council. Cardona had apologized for missing an appointment and expressed gratitude to Ydigoras-Fuentes for his support of Cuba. Cardona also mentioned that individuals who had called for Ydigoras-Fuentes' removal had been expelled from his group. Alejos informed Ydigoras-Fuentes that Cardona wanted to meet to resolve misunderstandings, and Ydigoras-Fuentes agreed to see him and instructed Alejos to issue Cardona a visa.
