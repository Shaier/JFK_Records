This is a memo from H. Clark Schor to SA/DO/O responding to a House Select Committee on Assassinations (HSCA) request. It supplements a previous memo and provides additional information regarding individuals and entities mentioned in a letter from the HSCA. The memo details the number of documents CCS files contain on individuals such as George DeMohrenschildt, Leo Cherne, William Harvey, Richard Helms, James Angleton, Raymond Rocca, and the Wackenhut Corporation and their relevance to the HSCA's request. It also states that there are no records on Southern Research Corporation.
