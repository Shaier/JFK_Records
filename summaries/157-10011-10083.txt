This document is a deposition/transcript of William C. Sturbitts from April 16, 1975, related to the JFK assassination. It's from the Rockefeller Commission records, numbered 157-10011-10083, and marked as a "textual document" classified as "S" (secret) and restricted. The document was reviewed on September 29, 1993. It was subsequently sent to the CIA on February 16, 1994.
