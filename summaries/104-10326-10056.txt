This document is a secret debriefing report from the Canadian Department of Foreign Affairs about an interview with Georgi E. Mamedov, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of Russia, conducted in December 1995. The report covers Mamedov's observations and impressions on various topics, including Russian politics, international relations, ecology, and the economic transition in Russia. It also includes his views on presidential candidates and relations with Canada. Notably, it references the alleged mob involvement in the assassination of President Kennedy as a point of illustration.
