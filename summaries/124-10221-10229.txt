This FBI document, dated August 27, 1958, originates from the San Juan office and is addressed to the FBI Director. It concerns Pedro Luis Diaz Lanz, Renaldo Blanco Hernandez, and Alberto Eusebio Mojena, who are suspected of involvement with Fidel Castro. It contains information from a usually reliable source, as well as details regarding a communication where a communication states that Diaz Lanz is a Cuban pilot, courier, and possible agent for Fidel Castro. The document details DIAZ passing through Palisades Airport Kingston, Jamaica.
