This document is an intelligence report from the CIA, dated November 20, 1962, concerning an indication of planning for the reoccupation of North Vietnam. The report states that the Minister for Civic Action, NGO TRONG HIEU, informed his assistants that he was directed to assist in this planning. A questionnaire was being drafted for selected people in South Vietnam, and a second questionnaire was being prepared for people who formerly lived in North Vietnam. The document also references previous reports and includes comments on the strategic hamlet program.
