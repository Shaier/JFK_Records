This document is a secret communication from Budapest, dated May 19, 1971, concerning the World Peace Council (WPC). It details observations from local press coverage on the WPC's activities, including the participation of U.S. individuals like Sidney Peck, the awarding of a medal to Martin Luther King, and the presence of U.S. delegates at meetings. It also mentions that Americans Herbert Aptheker and Carlton Goodlett were reelected to the presidential board.
