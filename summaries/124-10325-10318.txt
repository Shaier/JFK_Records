This document is a memo from the FBI to Sullivan, dated September 11, 1963, concerning the CIA liaison file. It discusses the relationship between the FBI and CIA, highlighting instances of both cooperation and conflict, and provides information on a variety of topics including Soviet and Cuban espionage operations, double agents, defectors, and concerns about security breaches. It also touches on issues related to personnel, technical capabilities, and interactions with other government entities like the President's Foreign Intelligence Advisory Board.
