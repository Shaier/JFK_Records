This document is a CIA Information Report dated September 24, 1963, discussing potential coup plots in South Vietnam. The report details information from a Vietnamese Air Force officer regarding Ngo Dinh Nhu's alleged plans to deal with the Viet Cong and potential changes to the South Vietnamese economy. It also covers Phạm Ngọc Thảo's attempts to secure support from the Vietnamese Air Force for a coup against the Diem regime. The report indicates that Thao has spoken with President Diem and Nhu about Nhu's contacts with the Viet Cong and that Nhu is "considering dealing with the communists."
