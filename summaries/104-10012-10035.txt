This document is a collection of correspondence and related notes regarding Sergyj Czornonoh's claims of having provided information about Lee Harvey Oswald to American officials in Sofia, Bulgaria, in August 1963. The documents include letters from Czornonoh to British and Russian officials, detailing his allegations that Oswald was planning to assassinate President Kennedy, and that Czornonoh himself was targeted by U.S. authorities. The collection also includes internal correspondence from the CIA, indicating that they were aware of Czornonoh's claims and the need to coordinate any dissemination of this information.
