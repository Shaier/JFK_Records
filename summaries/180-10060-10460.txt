This document is a collection of payroll records and related correspondence for Melvin Kreidman, who was employed by the House Select Committee on Assassinations (HSCA). The documents include payroll authorization forms, leave records, and internal memos regarding his appointment, salary, administrative leave, and eventual termination from the committee. There is also a memo regarding a reimbursement issue related to a trip to Canada. The documents contain certifications regarding the employee's relationship to members of congress.
