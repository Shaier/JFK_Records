This document is an agent report, dated February 5, 1972, regarding an incident of possible counter-intelligence interest. The report details an interview with a Security Guard, who was approached by a man identifying himself as "John the Dee Jay" at a bar in Datteln. John asked about military personnel, radar, and security sergeants, and specifically mentioned an interest in the XR-71. He also claimed to have been caught wearing US fatigues in downtown Datteln. The source met with John again, and John gave the source a slip of paper with information about an Air Force Security Sergeant. The source did not believe he had supplied any official information to John, but felt that it was possible. The report includes descriptions of John and the owner of the Althoff Photo Shop.
