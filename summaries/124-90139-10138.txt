This document is a memo from the FBI to HQ, dated May 10, 1963, concerning CIA liaison material and foreign political matters related to Cuba. It summarizes comments made by Raul Roa, Cuban Foreign Minister, regarding the situation in Cuba and its relations with other countries. The memo indicates that the informant's evaluation of the situation in Cuba was highly pessimistic. It also discusses Roa's views on the United Nations, the Organization of American States (OAS), and various other political matters.
