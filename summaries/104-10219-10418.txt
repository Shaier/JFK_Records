This document is a classified message from the Mexico City station to the Director, dated August 9, 1961. It discusses the activities of LITAMIL-6, who traveled from Mexico to Havana on August 6 to contact LIOOZE-1 and carry out instructions given by LITAMIL-5. The message also mentions a sabotage team in Cuba, codebooks, radio communications, and other individuals involved in the operation, including Enrique Collazo and "Jesus Badal." The message expresses concern about potential leaks that could damage relations within the LITAMIL complex.
