This document is a dispatch from the Chief of Station, JMHAVE, to the Deputy Chief, WH/SA, regarding the Subfytic/Amtrunk/Mhapron operational matter. It transmits a copy of a memo passed to ACSI on January 19, 1965, concerning AMTRUNK-9 infiltration, and includes updated information on related individuals and events. The document also includes attachments and details concerning alleged operations, individuals, and funding related to a Cuban infiltration effort.
