This document is a memorandum for the record regarding the Wackenhut Corporation. It discusses the Agency's interest in George R. Wackenhut and his company, mentioning their involvement in providing cover support for Western Hemisphere Agency employees in Florida. The memo notes that Mr. Wackenhut received a Covert Security Approval, which was later rescinded. It also mentions the Agency's knowledge of Wackenhut Corporation providing guard coverage to Global Marine Development, Incorporated (GMDI), despite the severed relationship.
