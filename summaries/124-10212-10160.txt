This FBI document, dated May 1, 1963, concerns Luis Alberto Condom Cajigal (aka Luis Condom), and Alvaro Martin Fernandez Fox (aka Alvaro Fernandez), who are connected to Cuba. It includes information gathered about their activities, particularly concerning Cubans in Mexico. The CIA is also involved, as the document contains classified CIA information and a CIA representative in Miami is mentioned as the original source of some information. The document mentions bolita rackets and possible connections to drug trafficking. Some Customs information and informats were invovled. Some of the document has been redacted.
