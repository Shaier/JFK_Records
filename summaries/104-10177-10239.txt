This is a memorandum from the CIA to the Drug Enforcement Administration (DEA) regarding Cuban exiles who have come to DEA's attention as potential informants or liaison contacts. DEA requested the CIA to check their backgrounds due to concerns about potential provocation operations. The memorandum contains the results of a check of CIA headquarters records on these individuals, and summarizes findings and conclusions. The document assesses the potential involvement of outside organizations, like Cuban intelligence, in manipulating these individuals.
