This document is a dispatch from the CIA, classified as SECRET. It discusses information obtained during an interrogation by a HUGLIB Case Officer regarding contacts between the Cuban Embassy, Mexico City, and the Guatemalan FAR. The interrogated individual, Ricardo Ismael VALLECILLO Perez, a Guatemalan FAR member, mentioned the FAR was expecting a shipment of "Red Star" machine-pistols from Cuba and identified two Cuban Embassy officials involved in shipping weapons and money to the FAR. The document also states that HUGLIB-10 has granted permission to share this information with the Mexican Service if it's considered valuable. The distribution list includes copies for C/WHD, Mexico City, Guatemala City, and files.
