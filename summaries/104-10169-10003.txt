This document is a secret message dated February 27, 1978, from the CIA station in Guayaquil to the Director, with information for Miami and Quito. It requests traces on a former Cuban citizen named Evelio Duque Miyar, approximately 50 years old and a former resident of Camaguey province, who was possibly involved in Cuban operations in the Miami area during the early 1960s. The document indicates no file exists.
