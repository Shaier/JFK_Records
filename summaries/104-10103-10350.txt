This is a CIA memorandum from January 14, 1977, regarding Jose Ricardo RABEL Nunez. It details RABEL's background, including his birth in Cuba, education, military service, and subsequent involvement in Cuban politics both before and after the rise of Castro. The memo explores the possibility that RABEL was a double agent, working for both the CIA and Castro, highlighting conflicting evidence and potential motivations for his actions. It mentions his involvement with the AMTRUNK Operation, his eventual capture, imprisonment, and release in Cuba. The memo also references reasons RABEL gave for his defection to the U.S. and questions surrounding his loyalty.
