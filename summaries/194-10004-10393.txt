This document is an agent report from November 19, 1968, concerning Manuel Marti. A character reference was developed through an interview with Luisa Perez, a neighbor who has known Marti since 1956. Perez provided information about Marti's upbringing, family, education, and personal characteristics. The report indicates that Marti was raised in a respectable home, never involved in gang activities, and is believed to be a loyal US citizen with a good moral character. Perez recommended Marti for a position of trust with the US Government. The report was prepared by Alfonso E. Marshall of the 108th MI Gp (I-SIB).
