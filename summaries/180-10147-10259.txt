This document is from the Assassination Records Review Board and includes:

*   A final determination notification regarding staff payroll records from the HSCA (House Select Committee on Assassinations).
*   The document is postponed in part due to redaction of Social Security numbers to protect personal privacy.
*   The document was reviewed in 1995 and scheduled for another review in 2017.
*   It contains payroll authorization forms for Thomas Wayne Sacco, a research attorney for the Assassinations committee/HSCA.
*   It also includes a memo indicating Sacco's acceptance of the position with the Martin Luther King Task Force/HSCA.
*   Lastly, there is a payroll certification form for an employee to declare any relationship to a current member of Congress.
