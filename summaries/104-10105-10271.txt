This document is a routing and record sheet from the CIA regarding a manuscript titled "Undercover: Memoirs of an American Agent" by E. Howard Hunt. It details proposed security deletions and revisions to the galley proof of the manuscript. A DDO panel has identified certain items as "hard core" and recommends their deletion or revision, and that the Agency be prepared to litigate if necessary. The document also lists specific passages within the manuscript that should be reviewed for potential security concerns.
