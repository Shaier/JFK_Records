This document is a JFK Assassination System Identification Form for a document concerning Manuel Marti. The document is a paper, textual document classified as unclassified and was released with deletions. It originated from the USA and contains information about Marti, specifically a character reference from a co-worker at the Mutual Insurance Advisory Association. The report details an interview with Richard H. Franken, a supervisor at MIAA, regarding Marti's employment history, personal life, and character. Franken speaks highly of Marti, recommending him for a position of trust with the United States Government. The report was submitted on November 20, 1968, by Alphonso E. Marshall.
