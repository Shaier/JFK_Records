This document is a transcript of a deposition of William C. Sturbitts, a CIA employee, given to the Rockefeller Commission on April 16, 1975. The document discusses topics related to anti-Castro activities, CIA clandestine operations, Cuban exiles, E. Howard Hunt, Robert Kennedy, organized crime, and other subjects. The document also discusses a number of the participants such as Desmond Fitzgerald, Frank Sturgis, Johnny Roselli, William Sturbitts, and Sam Giancana, The document also refers to a number of activities like the Bay of Pigs, the Miami Station, as well as the nature of the relationship with the AMOTS and the media at the time.
