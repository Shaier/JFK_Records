This document is a CIA message from September 1977, originating from the station in Managua. It reports that a local Peace Corps Director (PCD) received information about a man named Victor Paneque Batista, who identified himself as a CIA operative and attempted to recruit a volunteer in Bluefields, Nicaragua as an intelligence agent. The document mentions the station has two cards on an individual with that name, and that he was previously identified as a Cuban citizen. It requests traces on Paneque.
