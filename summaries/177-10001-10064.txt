This document is a CIA report, dated November 23, 1963, detailing comments made by Cuban officials in Santiago, Chile, regarding the assassination of President Kennedy. The report includes remarks from Jose Toha, Vice President of the Chilean Cuban Cultural Institute, and Pedro Martinez Pirez, Charge of the Cuban Embassy. The report also mentions the Cuban Consul at Valparaiso consulting with the Cuban Embassy in Santiago about flying the flag at half-mast.
