This document is a memorandum from the Chief of the Latin America Division, Clifton R. Strathern, to the Chief of Counterintelligence Staff, Mr. Julian Fox, concerning General Comments on Mr. David Phillips' Manuscript. It expresses serious concerns about the manuscript's potential damage to operational security, specifically regarding the disclosure of sources, methods, cover arrangements, and liaison relationships. The memorandum also highlights concerns about the manuscript's confirmation of Agee's effectiveness in compromising Agency operations and the revelation of sensitive information about CIA personnel and activities. The overall consensus is that publishing the manuscript in its present form would be detrimental to the Agency.
