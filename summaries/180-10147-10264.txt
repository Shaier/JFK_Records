This document is a notification from the Assassination Records Review Board regarding a postponed record related to the HSCA's staff payroll records. The document, initially reviewed on 10/24/95 and set for review in 2017, contains information about Mel Waxman. The postponement is related to Social Security numbers. The document contains four payroll authorization forms, and a memorandum from Robert Lehner to Mr. Blakey and another memorandum to All Staff with a payroll certification.
