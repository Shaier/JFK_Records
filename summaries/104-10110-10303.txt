This document is a record of an interview with Charlotte Z. Bustos-Videla, an Operations Officer, conducted on January 10, 1973, by James E. Peters and William A. O'Donnell. The interview concerned Agee, with whom Bustos-Videla had various periods of acquaintance (see memorandum of 31 May 1972). Bustos-Videla had no additional contacts for Agee. She suggested contacting Dorothy Hennick of the Europe Division/Bern to ascertain whether or not Agee had contacted Jim Noland of the Mexico Station. The document has a reference number of 56840.
