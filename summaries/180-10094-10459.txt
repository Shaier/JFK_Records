This document is an investigation interview schedule and summary report related to Sergy Alexandrovech Uslov, conducted by the HSCA in August 1978. The interview covers Uslov's personal information, his emigration from Russia, his knowledge of Russian life and the KGB, and his relationship to Lee Harvey Oswald through a mutual acquaintance, Pavez A. Golovachev. The interview explores allegations of comments Oswald made about killing the President. Uslov also mentions writing a manuscript in Russian and submitting it for translation and transmittal to the HSCA.
