This document is a payroll certification for the U.S. House of Representatives Select Committee on Assassinations, covering the accounting period from October 1, 1977, to October 31, 1977. It lists employees, their positions, annual salaries, gross pay earned during the period, and any relevant remarks such as pay rate changes or appointments. It also includes a payroll summary with the number of employees, payroll expenditures used, and salary projections. There is also a payroll certification for the accounting period 8/1/77 to 8/31/77.
