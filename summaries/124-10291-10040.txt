This document is an intelligence information cable from the CIA, dated October 22, 1976, concerning Chilean government information on Cuban exile leaders Orlando Bosch and Rolando Otero. It discusses allegations of Bosch's involvement in a plot to assassinate a Chilean government official, Otero's subsequent arrest and deportation to the US, and his alleged plans to assassinate a Chilean Marxist extremist under the sponsorship of the Chilean government. The document also mentions Bosch's arrest in Costa Rica and his possession of Chilean identity documents.
