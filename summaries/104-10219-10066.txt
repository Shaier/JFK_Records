This document is a collection of memos, cables, and forms regarding an individual named Manuel Machado Llosa (also referred to as GICITRON-4, LITAMIL-1, and Manuelito), and his activities in relation to CIA operations involving Cuban exiles. The documents cover the period from the late 1950s to the early 1970s and concern things such as his use as a cover, his history as a treasurer for the 26th of July Movement, inquiries into his connections with others, and the approval and management of his operational approval.
