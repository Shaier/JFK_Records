This document is a collection of paperwork related to the employment of Diana Jones by the House Select Committee on Assassinations (HSCA) in 1977 and 1978. It includes payroll authorization forms, a personal leave record, and memoranda regarding her hiring and termination. The documents show she was employed as a secretary in the King Unit, her salary, and her termination date. The collection also contains information about payroll certifications and employee relationships to Members of Congress.
