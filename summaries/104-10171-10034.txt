This document is a report dated July 16, 1962, detailing an interview with Juan Rodriguez Jobolca. He provides information about the remnants of the DRF after the Bay of Pigs, contacts in Havana, a brother of Diaz Lanz heading a DRE group, and a cousin, Salvador Jomolca, who is a guerrilla leader in Las Villas Province. The report mentions the guerrilla group's need for arms and ammunition, and the difficulty of communicating with them. It also notes that Salvador Jomolca is married to a daughter of "Campito", a guerrilla leader.
