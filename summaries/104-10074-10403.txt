This document is a classified message from the CIA headquarters, dated September 4, 1970. It discusses an organization of Arab students in the U.S. who requested Ishmael Flory, a CPUSA official in Illinois, to select five young Black Americans for a tour of the Middle East, particularly Jordan and UAR, to promote the Arab position among Blacks in the U.S. The group was scheduled to leave Chicago on August 24, 1970, via Olympic Airlines Flight 420. The message also identifies Thomas Curtis as part of the group. It requests any information on the travel of the individuals mentioned. It also lists James Dillard, Christine Johnson, Joe Howard, and Joe Walker.
