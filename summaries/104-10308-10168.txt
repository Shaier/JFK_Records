This document is a communication, dated October 11, 1964. It discusses several topics related to Cuba, including:

*   A person named Fernandez (A-27) who wants to leave Cuba due to disillusionment with communism.
*   A request to include Luis Rodriguez in the Jaguar group with Fernandez's old number.
*   Information on officials imprisoned for economic reasons.
*   An assassination attempt on Fidel Castro and subsequent actions by his guards.
*   The movement of tanks and the arrival of a Russian ship carrying fertilizer.
*   Information about Julio Cambas (Texcido).
*   The flight of Commandant "Contreras" and others from Cuban bases, suspected to be in the U.S.
*   Requests for a report on the National Bank.
