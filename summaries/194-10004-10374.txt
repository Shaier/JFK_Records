This document consists of a JFK Assassination System Identification Form and related materials regarding Manuel Marti. It details a court order translation from February 12, 1963, where Marti's father legally changed his name to Manuel Marti. The court order also addresses corrections needed on Marti's birth certificate and those of his sons, Ivan and Manuel, regarding their surnames and the name of their father. The document was compiled by the 771st MI Detachment, Fort Brooke, Puerto Rico in September 1968.
