This document is a restricted handling message from CIA Headquarters, dated September 4, 1970. It discusses an LNERGO source reporting on the organization of Arab students in the U.S. requesting Ishmael Flory to select five young black Americans from Illinois to tour the Middle East, specifically Jordan and UAR, to propagate Arab positions among blacks in the U.S. The group was scheduled to leave Chicago on August 24, 1970, via Olympic Airlines, stopping in Montreal and then Athens, Greece. The message identifies one member of the group as Thomas Curtis.

The message then identifies James Dillard, Christine Johnson, Joe Howard and Joe Walker as being affiliated with the trip.

The message requests information on travel and contacts related to these individuals and suggests querying a liaison in Athens regarding the travelers.
