This document is a secret message from April 22, 1963, regarding a proposed operation to sabotage Cuban ships. It discusses the use of AMLEGS (Cuban assets) to carry out the sabotage, and the potential consequences of such an action, including possible Cuban reprisals. The message also details specific information about potential AMLEGS involved, their concerns and limitations, and the possibility of planting explosives on a ship. It seeks guidance on briefing the Chief of Mission regarding this program.
