This document is a JFK Assassination System Identification Form, originating from CSF/INSCOM. It pertains to DOD-affiliated personnel incident investigations and focuses on an interview with Robert Scheer, born on April 4, 1936. The interview, conducted on October 5, 1966, at the Armed Forces Examining and Entrance Station in Oakland, California, involved Scheer's refusal to answer questions regarding qualification of DD Forms 98 and 398 without counsel present. He submitted documents, attached as exhibits, to substantiate his views on US policies.
