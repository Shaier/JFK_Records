This document is a report on the intelligence community's review of intelligence activities relating to the Cuban arms build-up from April 14 to October 14, 1962. It details the targets the agency was able to cover, the frequency and continuity of coverage, and the extent of coverage in military, political, and economic spheres. It also discusses targets the agency was unable to cover, why, and significant changes in the agency's coverage capability. The document also outlines support from other agencies and agent summaries.
