This document is a dispatch from the Chief of Task Force W to the Chief of Station, JMWAVE, regarding operational and GYROSE activities agreed upon between the two entities and ACSI (Assistant Chief of Staff, Intelligence). It details discussions held on June 7, 1962, concerning topics like Opa Locka refugee referral procedures, the status of specific individuals of interest to both JMWAVE and ACSI, third-country diplomat contacts, maritime infiltration of ACSI agents, documentation, coordination forms, W/T assets, and secret writing. It also discusses a luncheon, a subsequent visit to Opa Locka, and an agreement regarding future cooperation between JMWAVE and ACSI.
